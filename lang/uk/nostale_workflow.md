# NosTale Workflow : Gstar policies and channels

*Last updated: 16/04/2026*

This file documents workflows external to the game: content distribution, communication channels, exchange policies with players. Consult this when a player asks something that touches Gstar's services (nostar.fr, Insta, etc.).

---

## nostar.fr : guide access codes

`nostar.fr/progress` hosts NosTale progression guides per act (act 1 to 9). Act 1 is freely accessible. Acts 2 to 9 are locked behind an access code.

### Distribution channel
Codes are sent only via Instagram DM. The Insta account is called `Codes NosTale` (Abd production). Players must follow the account then send a private message.

### Quota
Each Instagram follower receives 3 access codes per week. Each code unlocks the exclusive content (gold/XP tips) of one act of their choice.

### Gstar exchange policy
Before sending the codes, Gstar asks the player to like his last 6 Instagram posts. Typical phrasing: "so you don't owe me anything". The idea is to keep the exchange mutual rather than one-sided.

### How to respond in Discord
When a player asks for a code or specific help on an act (especially 6+):
- Don't try to answer in place of the nostar guide (no access to detailed content)
- Redirect to Insta DM
- Let Gstar decide himself when to send the code (timing, like exchange, etc.)
