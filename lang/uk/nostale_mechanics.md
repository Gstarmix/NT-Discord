*Last updated: 21/04/2026*

## Level progression

### General leveling route
- **lv65 → 85**: LoD (classic server), A5.1 side quests, A4 main quest
- **85 → c1**: champion level start. Transition at act 6. Champion level begins after level 85
- **c1 → c10**: A6 Hell's Ruins 2 (path without Rement archers, return spot at the entrance), or Ancelloan's Will 3
- **c10 → c30**: A7 Polluted Path (watch out for mob debuffs)
- **c30 → c60**: A8 first LoL + Polluted Path for bonus XP, A8 Bees from c53
- **c60 → c67**: A8 second LoL + A8 bees map
- **c68 → c72**: A8 second LoL upper map + A8 sharks (map hopping penalized: 2 min no-XP buff)
- **c73 → c80**: A8 second LoL upper map + Ice Dungeon (drakes up to c83)
- **c80 → c90**: A9 third LoL + maps (LoL needs good setup, maps more forgiving but map hopping penalized)
- **c90 → c99**: A10 maps (A10 quest gives c90 → c91)

### Act 9 XP (c80 → c90) : maps, rate, dailies

**A9 XP map ranking:**
- Top 1-2 (classic maps): Polluted Area, Power Plant
- Top 3 (LoL): Lower Laboratory Level, rest of LoL lab afterwards

**XP rate c80 → c90:**
- Duo with good xp setup (divine fairy, noble medal, xp accessories) on double map: 11-13%/h
- Double xp: rate doubled
- Solo without double map: a bit less
- 1-2h of xp map per day = steady progression

**A9 dailies:**
- 4 dailies/day = 3 in 9.1 + Ultimate Giant Arma in 9.2 (only 9.2 daily)
- The 3 dailies in 9.1 give ~7%/day
- 9.1 main quests one-shot = level up to ~81
- Full list: https://nosapki.com/fr/quests?category=153

**LoL vs classic maps trade-off in A9:**
- A9 LoL = good xp, low drop
- A9 classic maps (polluted area, power plant, southern basement path) = good drop for crafting 90 gear, slightly less xp

## LoD farm : Multi-mule mage setup

### Context by server
- **Classic server**: start lv55, quests to level to 65, then LoD from lv65 to lv80. Goal: reach lv80 to unlock IC. Estimate: 65→80 ≈ 4h30 per mule
- **NosFire**: start lv80 with SP2M at +5 given in the starter pack. LoD only used to level from lv80 to lv85. IC only accessible from level 85 on NF (anti-bot measure)

### Prerequisites
- Family required to access LoD (200k creation)
- Bori pet on the Main SP1E, leveled to 60 (59 quality pet food), with dolls and premium pet food for ress
- Mount with at least 21 movement speed (Bunny Carriage ideal for the defensive buff, otherwise Jaguar / Tortucopter / Rodeotoro at ~2M)
- Redemption potions and Cylloans
- Tank gear: Honourable Boots + Magma Gloves at sum 5 in NosBazaar (120% fire and dark resistances)

### Optimal setup: 8 characters (classic server)
- Main SP1E = tank, aggros the Renés with mount
- Bori on the Main: triggers Berserk Frenzy during aggro (invulnerable 1 min, 5 min cooldown)
- 8th character (any class, min +10): only role = tank the Horn with its pet. Enters the map last → the Horn targets the last entered.
- 6 grouped mage mules: hit + Group Heal. No pet on mules (interferes with positioning)

### Mule stats
- **6 mage mule setup**: no stat points in SP2M (no HP/MP, no element). Reason: if the Horn pops on the mules, it one-shots them all at once → simultaneous redemption + the Horn switches target. Smoother than ress one by one. The number of mages is enough to clear aggro without stats
- **Team 3 setup (1 swordsman + 1 SP2M mage + 1 SP4A archer)**: put element points on the mage and archer to clear aggros faster. Without stats, it's too slow with a single mage
- Avoid offensive buffs: if a mule deals more than 10% damage on a René, the other mules dealing under 10% lose XP
- SP1E tank: full HP/MP, no fairy, remove the weapon if needed. Goal = provoke without dealing damage
- Video reference: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ERIzWLwQk5I

### Aggro technique
- Rule: never stop (NosTale sync isn't optimized → ranged hits if standing still)
- Path: dragon skull entrance → veer right → follow the right side → cut left at the end → semi-circle to the right → pick up the Renés on the left → return to the group with right-left back-and-forth

### XP and pace
- Buff: Ancelloan Blessings + Star Tarots on each mule
- Skeleton costume: +30% general XP (10M per mule, 60M for 6)
- Horn pops about every 4 min, Berserk Frenzy 5 min cooldown
- Each SP job level up resets skill cooldowns
- Classic server estimate: 65→68 in ~30 min (with bless + tarot, breaks and Horn included). 65→80 ≈ 4h30 per mule
- Gold: LoD jewelry at 7k to NPC → ~330k per session (empty inventory)

### Why mage for LoD multi-account
- Mage: SP2M directly available, base AoE → simplest
- Swordsman: long SP3 quest on each mule
- Archer: even longer SP4 quest + SP4A has no base AoE

## Instant Combat (IC)

- **Classic server**: accessible from level 80
- Available on all channels
- Instant combat against waves of mobs
- Important source of gold and mats (especially with alts)
- Around 500k per hour with a Main +10 minimum
- Asgobas IC: 4 phases (deal enough damage + survive), c30+ required, A8 mats drops (Draconium, sometimes eq box)

## SP boost soulstones

Three soulstones are given at the end of the main quests of certain acts. Each pushes one SP directly to 41+9:
- **Blue Soulstone** (A4) → use on the class's priority SP
- **Silver Soulstone** (A6) → use on the 2nd priority SP
- **Golden Soulstone** (A7) → use on the 3rd or 4th SP

**NosFire note**: the A4 Blue Soulstone is not distributed on NosFire servers (start at level 80, A4 quest already done or reward removed). Only the silver (A6) and golden (A7) soulstones are obtainable.

## Support SPs

Three SPs can play a support role to assist a main player. They are obtained via the SP3 and SP4 quests (long).

### SP2M (Holy Mage) : heal + buff support
- Group Heal (heal)
- Sacred Weapon (dps buff)
- Blessing (resistance buff)

### SP4A (Wild Keeper) : buff + debuff support
Obtained via the SP4 quest.
- Elemental Leech (dps debuff)
- Wolf + Elemental Light (dps buff)
- Bear (resistance buff)

### SP3E (Crusader) : optional support
Obtained via the SP3 quest. Less powerful than SP2M and SP4A.
- Attack Prayer (dps buff)
- Defense Prayer (resistance buff)
- Sacred Breath (dps debuff)

### Which support for your class
- **Swordsman (SP1E) / Martial Artist (SP1AM)**: SP2M + SP4A. Melee classes, no need for tank pet.
- **Mage**: SP4A required + pet required (melee tank). SP3E optional.
- **Archer**: SP2M required + pet required (melee tank). SP3E optional.

## Character transfer (from NosFire)

- 1 character per account via Battle Pass item (high reward)
- Additional transfers possible by reaching level 13 of the Battle Pass on other accounts
- Choice of destination server (different items per server, modifiable until closure)
- Server visible in the "Consumables" section
- What is transferred: everything on the character (P window), 10-slot BP warehouse, costumes, SP inventory, active market objects, miniland (pets and decoration), progression (quests, reputation, tattoos, skills, well-being)
- What is NOT transferred: gold (including bank), family, family warehouse, ongoing auctions (not refunded), friend list, marriage, blacklist, partner backpack
- Unclaimed NosMall items: claimable on the destination server
- If the nickname already exists on the destination server: mandatory change at first login
- Impossible to change name on NosFire (technical restriction)

## Partners : when to start using them

- At low level, don't play with a partner. Use only a pet (Bori, Tired Ram depending on class and context)
- Partners dropped in early game (Bob at TS25, etc.) are mediocre and don't keep up as you level. Keep for collection but not for play
- Look into partners once champion level is approaching (end of standard progression, transition to act 6)
- From champion level: pick a partner suited to your role (melee or magic depending on the role wanted) and optimize via partner gear (see next section)

---

## Partner gear : tank optimization

The goal of partner gear is to maximize survival (resistances), not damage.

### Resistance weapons (by partner type)
- Melee partners (Ragnar, Kliff, etc.): level 65 and 71 weapon → +10 to all resistances
- Magic partners (Graham, Erdimine, Yertirand, etc.): level 63 weapon → +10 to all resistances

### Rest of the gear
- Armor: highest level possible, R0+5 minimum
- Accessories: multi-resistance (ex: maritime)

### Context
- Investment relevant only from champion level
- At low level, partner tanks fine without specific gear

## Pets in early game (f2p mage)

- Pet role for a mage: tank melee mobs while the mage hits at range
- Best free tank in early game (classic server): Tired Ram, capturable at TS23 from level 25
- While waiting for level 25: use any pet capturable nearby
- On NosFire: the 16th anniversary cupcake (included in the Starter Pack at 330 ND) advantageously replaces the Tired Ram or Bori as first pet, already geared and available from the start
- Pet resurrection:
  - Power Seeds: automatic resurrection every 5 minutes, sufficient in early game, cheap
  - Eva Dolls (purchasable from Eva in NosVille): immediate automatic resurrection, but too expensive for a beginner without gold
