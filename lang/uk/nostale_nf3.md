# NosFire 3 (2026) : Specific info

> **SERVER ENDED.** NosFire 3 closed on 10/06/2026. Characters were transferred to classic servers (Dragonveil / Undercity). This file is kept as historical reference; for content that applies to the current game, see `nostale_mechanics.md`.

*Temporary event server. Opened: 01/04/2026, closed: 10/06/2026.*

---

## Format and starting conditions

- Start at level 80, job 80, quests from Act 4 (A1/A2/A3 already completed)
- Starter pack: level 80 R5+7 gear, 30/50% fairies, SP1 and SP2 at 50+5 for all classes (including MA)
- Duration: 10 weeks

## NF3 permanent bonuses

- EXP x2, gold x2, loot x2, reputation x4, fairy EXP x2, family EXP x3
- EXP x2 for fishing, cooking and pet training
- Special weekends with higher bonuses (raid box, upgrade, etc.)

## NF3 changes vs NF2

- Tart Hapendam c60-c80: buff at 75% (improved vs NF2)
- c55 and c65 equipment now available
- Draconium x15 (vs less on NF2, bottleneck resolved)
- MA SPs included in the starter pack
- No premium Battle Pass (free only)
- No "first on server" ranking (unlike NosVersary)
- Arena of Talents disabled
- IC and group TS only accessible from level 85 (anti-bot measure)
- Rainbow Battle accessible from level 85
- Family penalty: 24h (unchanged)
- No duplicated maps (sub-channels instead)
- A8/A9 raids simplified (1 room instead of 2)
- A10 raids NOT simplified (recently buffed by GF)
- A4 raids: weakened Lord Mukraju version

## NF3 leveling route

- **80 → 85**: LoD, Ragnar side quests, A5.1 side quests, A4 main quest until lv81
- **85 → c1**: hero level start, transition to act 6
- Same route as classic server afterwards (see nostale_mechanics.md)

## Soulstones on NF3

- A4 Blue Soulstone: **not distributed on NF3** (start at level 80, A4 quest already done or reward removed)
- A6 Silver Soulstone: available, place on the 2nd priority SP (class's main dark SP)
- A7 Golden Soulstone: available, place on the 3rd or 4th SP
- Consequence: the class's priority SP (normally boosted by the blue soulstone) has to be leveled by hand on NF3

## Multi-account on NF3

- Team of 3-4 chars recommended given the limited time (10 weeks)
- 8-char setup viable on classic server but not very relevant on NF
- LoD on NF is only used to level from 80 to 85 (no 65 to 80 like on classic server)

## NF3 Battle Pass

- Free only (no premium version)
- Contains the transfer item as a high reward
- Contains the transfer warehouse (10 slots)
- No classic warehouse on NosFire (not buyable from NPCs nor craftable) → only the BP warehouse is usable
- Consequence: no Draco/Glacerus amulets from MA (needs warehouse)

## NF3 Community Achievements

- Each creator gets a unique in-game code
- Player enters the code → linked to the creator + welcome reward
- 4 missions: Easy (A7.1 quest, 15/04), Medium (c30, 15/04), Hard (SP10, 22/04), Very Hard (5x weak Asgobas, 29/04)
- Higher completion rate = more reward codes redistributed
- Rewards: Relics, Ancelloan Blessings, Divine Potions, Fairy Boosters

## NF3 Progress Book

- Similar to NF2
- Rewards: gold, materials, SP7/8/10/11, R5+7 gear for c53/55/65
- Complementary system to the Battle Pass

## NF3 community feedback

- EXP x2 too low (wall starts at A9/A10 : NosVersary at x3 = c80 in 2 weeks, NF2 at x2 = most stagnate before A9)
- No c80-c90 Tart Hapendam (community asked for it)
- SP3/SP4 not given at start → class imbalance (mage favored with SP2M AoE)
- Fairy EXP only x2 (x3 requested)
- A10 raids potentially impossible for the average player (SP +15/16, tattoos +7/8, no DV/UC gear)
- Late announcement (1 week before, vs 2 weeks last year)
- AI-generated promo art (poorly received)
- No A9 duplicated maps (sub-channels insufficient)
- No c83 gear given after A9.1 quest
- General feeling: "NF2 with a 3"
