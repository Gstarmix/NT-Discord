# Q4 : Pourquoi les mobs sont si durs à tuer ?

**Question EN:** why does it feel like every mob is so hard to kill?

**Answer EN:**
its normal to struggle at server start, the game clearly pushes you towards the nosmall and its intentional. the starter pack is really worth it if you can afford it, its 330 nosdollars and contains a lot of useful stuff to get going comfortably:

- 16th anniversary rainbow cupcake
- wonderland set
- halitus prototype
- random unicorn mount (white, black, pink)
- inventory extension ticket (60 days)
- nosmerchant medal (30 days)

outside the pack a lot of players also use happy hours to round out their setup. heres whats recommended and what it costs in nosdollars:

- 5 tarot packs at 10 nd each = 50 nd total. each pack gives 5 cards out of 10 possible, so 5 packs = 35 tarots and you can hope for a good base of dps tarots (devil, sun) or tank (lovers) among them
- 1 divine fairy for your main sp = 200 nd, no need to grab 4 right away
- 100 giant recovery potions = 25 nd, mostly useful while waiting to hit lv85 for ic
- a melee partner like ragnar at 50 nd to start, knowing he gets replaced later by graham (free via the a6.1 quest), then maybe yuna or marie via the noswheel. halitus is included in the pack so its up to you depending on how much comfort you want
- 80 partner medicine and 80 quality pet food to level your partner and nosmate, 160 nd each = 320 nd total. if you grab kliff at ts49 he comes lv50 so you save 49 medicines

the total comes around 975 nd. the 49.99€ offer is the most cost-effective for that, and depending on when you buy the real cost varies a lot. these prices are based on french localization, may vary by country:

- without happy hours: about 41€
- with happy hours +30%: about 32€
- with happy hours +50%: about 28€

if you cant go through the nosmall, my guide is still a good base even tho nosfire has its own rules, a dedicated video is coming soon. in the meantime heres what i recommend by class.

if youre a swordsman in warrior or martial artist in draconic fist, a holy mage and a wild keeper can support you. holy mage heals you with group heal and buffs you with sacred weapon for dps and blessing for resistance. wild keeper debuffs enemies with elemental leech and buffs you with wolf and elemental light for dps or bear for resistance. wild keeper is obtained via the sp4 quest which is pretty long so its an investment.

if youre a mage you play at range so your pet is mandatory to tank mobs in melee, and a wild keeper supports you with the same buffs and debuffs as above. if youre an archer same thing, your pet tanks in melee and a holy mage supports you with the same heals and buffs. as an option for both you can do the sp3 quest to get a crusader who buffs attack prayer for dps and defense prayer for resistance, and debuffs sacred breath for dps. its the weakest of the three support sps so its really optional, but worth knowing about.
