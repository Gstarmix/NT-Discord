# Q19 : Est-ce qu'une place reste réservée si on ne rentre pas dans le CI ?

**Question EN:** actually, if someone stays connected and has their queue rank, if they dont enter, will those behind them slide up to take their place, or does their slot stay permanently reserved and counted? basically if they're position 20 in queue, if they dont enter, will there be 20 empty slots split across 1 or 2 ICs? or will those 20 be filled by people behind them?

**Answer EN:**
empty slots dont exist in an IC. if someone doesnt enter, those behind them in the queue take their place automatically. the IC always fills in queue order, no holes.

**Context:** NosFire 3 (01/04/2026 -> 10/06/2026) + classic servers
**Validation date:** 07/04/2026
**Theme:** IC / placement / queue
**Note:** Direct follow-up to Q18. No reserved slot or hole in an IC. If a player doesnt enter, the queue advances automatically.
