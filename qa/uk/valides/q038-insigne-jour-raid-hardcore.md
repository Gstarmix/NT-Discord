# Q38 : Un insigne par jour ou par raid hardcore ?

**Question EN:** do we get 1 badge per day per act or 1 badge per hardcore raid? trying to figure out how worth it is to invest

**Answer EN:**
its 1 badge per successful hardcore raid, provided you make your points

doable max 35 times per raid per week

and remember to log in monday to reset your participations, theres a small bug

**Context:** NosFire 3 (01/04/2026 -> 10/06/2026) + classic servers. Thread with Djidje (Krem Survivor) who first asks if HC raid badges from acts 1-3 are exchangeable for other badges (Gstar's answer: not to his knowledge), then chains on the obtention frequency to estimate worth
**Validation date:** 19/04/2026
**Theme:** HC raid badges / frequency / points / weekly cap / reset bug
**Note:** 1 HC badge per successful HC raid, conditional on validating required points (the "puntos"). Cap: 35 HC raids max per raid per week. Known bug: log in Monday so participations reset properly. HC raids = weekly limit (not daily like normal raids). HC badges not exchangeable across acts. Answer level: 2 (casual).
