# Q14 : Quelles stats mettre sur les mules mages au LoD ?

**Question EN:** btw i put my swordsman full hp/mp, for the mages what do you recommend, full element?

**Answer EN:**
for mage mules at lod it depends on your setup. if you play 6 mules, put no stat points in the sp, no hp/mp no element. the idea is when the horn pops, it one-shots them all at once, they ress at the same time and the horn switches target automatically. its smoother than ressing one by one.

if you play with a single mage and an archer in team 3, then you put element points to clear aggros faster, otherwise its too slow.

i talk about it in my video here https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ERIzWLwQk5I

**Context:** NosFire 3 (01/04/2026 -> 10/06/2026)

**Note:** Mule stats rule for SP2M: 0 stat if 6 mules (one-shot Horn = simultaneous redemption = auto target switch). Element stats if team 3 (1 mage + 1 archer) to compensate lack of DPS. SP1E swordsman always full HP/MP in both cases.
