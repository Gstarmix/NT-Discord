# Q60 : Lower level crossbow with good SL vs higher level?

**Question :** Jozsi, I have a lower lv crossbow with really good sl should i try to find higher lv or is it ok to keep the lower if I don't really care about crusader? Does it effect my dmg output or only if i actually attack with the crossbow?

**Réponse UK :**

if you're on the 72 crossbow you basically have two options

you can keep it but you lose the sl
or go c25 instead and get the sl but a bit less damage
i'd go with c25 personally, damage stays more stable in the long run

either way the real move is getting to c60 asap, that's where you get both
there's a page that lays out all the gear swordsmen need at each stage: https://www.nostar.fr/en/progress/a6
