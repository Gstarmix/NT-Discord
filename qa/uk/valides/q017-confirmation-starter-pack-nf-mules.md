# Q17 : Confirmation starter pack NF : les mules mages démarrent au niveau 80 avec la SP2M+5, pas besoin de les stat ?

**Question EN:** were agreed that when you say 6 mage mules, you take into account they start lv80 on nosfire with the sp2m+5 that wont need stats (thats what you explain in your video)

**Answer EN:**
yeah exactly, on nosfire mules start directly at lv80 with holy mage at +5 in the starter pack.

and as explained in the video, no stat points on it, no hp/mp no element. the goal is precisely for them to get one-shot by the horn so that resurrection is simultaneous and the horn switches target automatically.

on nosfire lod is for leveling 80 to 85, while on classic server you start from 65 and aim lv80 to unlock IC.

**Context:** NosFire 3 (01/04/2026 -> 10/06/2026)
**Validation date:** 05/04/2026
**Theme:** LoD / mule stats / multi-account / NF starter pack
**Note:** Classic server: start lv55, quests to reach 65, then LoD 65->80, IC accessible from lv80. On NosFire: start lv80 with free SP2M+5, LoD only for leveling 80->85, IC and group TS accessible only from lv85 (NF3 anti-bot measure).
