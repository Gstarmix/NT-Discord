# Q65 : How does the flying fire devil (LoD) aggro work?

**Question :** how does dh(LoD) aggro work? i want to level up my alts but it just killed them even though i clipped into a corner next to skull passage

**Réponse UK :**

the flying fire devil targets whoever entered the map last
if your tank didn't enter last, the ffd went straight for your alts regardless of where they were standing

fix is simple: always enter your tank last
it'll lock on to your tank when it spawns and your alts stay safe

the corner near skull passage won't protect against that, positioning only helps against the revenant skeleton warriors, not the ffd

**Suite (réponse à sp12enjoyer sur les résistances) :**

nah on the res actually, i'd say the opposite
you want the ffd to kill your alts fast if it randomly pops on them
once they're dead it switches target back to your tank
if they tank the hit and survive the ffd just stays on them longer

they'll respawn and reposition, no permanent damage done
putting res on alts is just wasted money on chars that are supposed to die quick and come back

**Suite 2 (réponse à sp12enjoyer) :**

the issue is even if they tank the first hit the ffd stays locked on them
no res = oneshot = it switches target right away
with res they eat 2-3 hits before dying, more time on your alts instead of your tank
that's a constraint not a fix
