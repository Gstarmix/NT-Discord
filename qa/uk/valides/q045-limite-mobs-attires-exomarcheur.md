# Q45 : Y'a une limite de mobs attirés par l'exomarcheur ?

**Question EN:** is there a limit to mobs pulled by the exo?

**Answer EN:**
yeah theres one, around 50-60 mobs based on player feedback
with a speed booster the limit goes up, some duos manage to clean a full map instead of half

its not written explicitly in the game, you have to test yourself for the precise value depending on your zone

**Context:** NosFire 3 (01/04/2026 -> 10/06/2026) + classic servers. Thread with Youness (A8 bees map farm under booster, aggro limited to half map). Limit info 50-60 brought by AbrègeFrère. Booster = larger limit info confirmed by forum (Gaby quote 29/05/2025 citing Rod1: full-map duos thanks to booster, cleaning 2 maps instead of one)
**Validation date:** 20/04/2026
**Theme:** Exo-walker / aggro limit / speed booster / farm / A8 bees
**Note:** Exo aggro limit ≈ 50-60 mobs (community feedback, not documented in-game). Speed booster increases the limit (also undocumented, confirmed by forum and duo feedback). In-game Exo description mentions booster interaction: speed -4, duration +10, all debuffs removed up to lv4, Camouflage Field replaced by Strobe Panel. Exo has no effect in some raids, no xp if in a group. Answer level: 2 (casual).
