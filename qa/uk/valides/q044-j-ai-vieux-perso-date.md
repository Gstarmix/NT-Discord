# Q44 : J'ai un vieux perso qui date d'avant le starter pack lv56, comment profiter des items offerts ?

**Question EN:** my char is from before the lv56 starter pack, how do i get the items given today on classic server?

**Answer EN:**
make a new char of the same class on the same account, you directly get the lv50 r5+7 gear, the 4 fairies (one per element), sp1, sp2 and lv45 rez items

then transfer everything via the miniland warehouse to your old char

big time save vs farming everything from scratch
