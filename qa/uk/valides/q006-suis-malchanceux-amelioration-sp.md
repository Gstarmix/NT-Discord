# Q6 : Suis-je malchanceux avec l'amélioration de ma SP ?

**Question EN:** im trying to push my sp from +5 to +6 with 40% success, ive failed 7 times in a row. am i crazy or just biased by frustration?

**Answer EN:**
its just bad luck. 40% success means 60% failure each try, its not 50/50. mathematically, failing 7 times in a row at 60% failure happens about 3 times out of 100, so its rare but really not impossible.

the protective scroll just prevents destruction, it doesnt boost the success rate. there is no pity system for sp upgrades, every try is independent of the previous one. keep going, it will eventually pop.
