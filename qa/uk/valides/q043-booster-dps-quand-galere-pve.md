# Q43 : Comment booster son dps quand on galère en pve (farm / xp / quête) ?

**Question EN:** how do i boost my dps when im struggling in pve?

**Answer EN:**
most effective is a devil or sun tarot (+250 atk), otherwise the death tarot as a cheaper option (+100 atk)
you can also push your buffs with fairy boosters and attack and defense potions

and if you have mules alongside, they can buff you too

remember to equip rez matching the mob's element otherwise you take more damage than necessary

**Context:** NosFire 3 (01/04/2026 -> 10/06/2026) + classic servers. Thread with Peaktchi (recent char on Dragonveil, struggling on the SP4 quest at the mama moles then giant jellyfish). General PvE optimization advice taken out of SP4 quest context
**Validation date:** 19/04/2026
**Theme:** DPS optimization / pve / tarots / fairy booster / potions / mule buffs / rez
**Note:** Devil Tarot and Sun Tarot = +250 atk (top tier). Death Tarot = +100 atk (budget tier). Fairy booster + atk/def potions = complementary boosts. Mule buffs = multi-account bonus. Base rule: equip rez of mob's element. Answer level: 2 (casual).
