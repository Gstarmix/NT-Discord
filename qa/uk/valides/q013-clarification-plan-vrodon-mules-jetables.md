# Q13 : Clarification plan Vrodon : mules jetables + groupe principal pour transfert

**Question EN:** thanks for your advice, the goal was to rush fast to lod then polluted path then lol and id planned to only transfer a main group of mage + swordsman + archer, the rest were just chars to hit at lod or polluted path.

**Answer EN:**
ah ok, in that case your base plan was already well thought out. for the lod mules you dont need to push them far, they just serve to level from 80 to 85, after that lod loses interest. once your main group is lv85 you move to polluted path in a7 up to c30, then chain into lol in a8.

for transfer, each extra char requires reaching battle pass level 13, so depends on how many you can level in the time available.
