# Q15 : C'est quoi les mules mages, et comment faire du multicompte techniquement ?

**Question EN:** mage mules?? so after lv85 i start act 6 if i got that right, and to wrap up multi-accounting how do i do that? in the same client as my main account i can relaunch the game several times then put another account??? how do i do multi-account

**Answer EN:**
mage mules are just secondary accounts that play mage, their only role is to hit at lod while your main swordsman does the aggro. they dont need to be leveled far, just up to lv85.

for multi-accounting, each account is a separate gameforge account with its own credentials. you can create multiple accounts with the same email by adding extra game accounts directly from the launcher. once you have all your accounts, you launch them one by one from the launcher switching account between each launch, which opens a new game window. you end up with as many windows open as you have accounts.

for everything about lod mules, i made a video that explains the setup in detail, how to position mules, manage aggro and optimize the farm. https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ERIzWLwQk5I

**Context:** NosFire 3 (01/04/2026 -> 10/06/2026)

**Theme:** Multi-account / mules / launcher technique
**Note:** A Gameforge account = one email, but can contain multiple NosTale game accounts. Simultaneous launch = open the launcher, switch game account, relaunch, repeat. Steam limited to a single account, unusable for multi-account.
