# Q25 : Quel familier pour un mage débutant sans NosMall ?

**Question EN:** i'd rather not spend real money (context: lv20 mage asking which pet to take)

**Answer EN:**
for an f2p mage the most important thing is having a pet that tanks in melee while you hit at range
at your level you dont have access to the best options yet, but from lv25 you can capture a tired ram in ts23
its the best free tank for a mage in early game, it tanks well and lets you cast your spells without interruption
in the meantime grab whatever you can capture nearby, even a basic pet beats nothing

**Context:** Classic servers (Dragonveil/Undercity) + NosFire 3
**Validation date:** 14/04/2026
**Theme:** Pet / mage / early game / f2p
**Note:** Tired Ram = best free tank pet in early game for mage, accessible at TS23 from lv25. Side tip: Eva dolls let the pet auto-resurrect, but too expensive in early game, go with power seeds (auto resurrection every 5 minutes, enough to start).
