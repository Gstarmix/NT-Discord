# Q22 : Comment utiliser la compétence d'apprivoisement avancé ?

**Question EN:** i could do the quest and a few side quests, but i still have a question... how do you use the advanced taming skill? i do have a lure but nothing happens when i use it

**Answer EN:**
the lure isnt used directly, it just needs to be in your inventory when you cast advanced taming
the skill is used on a 2-star monster whose hp has turned yellow
if the monster is 1-star then its the normal taming skill you need, not the advanced one

**Context:** Classic servers (Dragonveil/Undercity) + NosFire 3
**Validation date:** 11/04/2026
**Theme:** Nosmate / training / pet trainer SP / taming
**Note:** The lure is a passive prerequisite in inventory, not an item to use manually. Advanced taming = 2-star monsters with yellow HP. Normal taming = 1-star monsters.
