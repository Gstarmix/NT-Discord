# Q20 : Comment atteindre le niveau 13 du pass baston pour le transfert de serveur ?

**Question EN:** thanks for the video! any tips to complete the pass to level 13? it seems so long to do for the server change item. thanks for your advice

**Answer EN:**
yeah it looks long at first glance, but its doable if you target the right missions. level 13 needs 601 battle points and gives you the server transfer item. level 18 needing 851 points additionally gives a nosfire warehouse to transfer more items.

most missions will happen naturally as you play: raids, ic, hero level progression. the main goal is to reach c80, the rest follows almost on its own. everything related to jobs like fishing and cooking, or pvp like arena and ice crown, is a bit separate and requires deliberate effort, but its still accessible and worth it if you aim for level 18.
