# Q57 : What partner is recommended for LoL?

**Question :** What Partner is recommended for lol? I have forga on Jennifer but she dies Like a Lot lot

**Réponse UK :**

forga is a pure attack sp, it has no healing at all, that's exactly why she keeps dying
lol hits hard and partners take a beating there

swap to sp marie, it has a passive heal that procs automatically in combat and keeps the partner alive way better
it's the go-to for survivability

if you go with marie, convert the level 63 wand into a partner wand, that gives her +10 to all resistances

also worth knowing that keeping your partner alive gives you xp bonuses on top of everything else
full list at https://www.nostar.fr/en/xp/list if you want to check what you're missing out on
