# Q12 : Est-ce que 2 escrimeurs SP1E + plusieurs mages peuvent OS les packs en chemin conta et LoD ?

**Question EN:** so basically i dont need to make 15 chars? i was planning 2 swordsmen in warrior to tank lod and polluted path and the rest mages in holy mage + 1 wild keeper archer for buff debuff. is that too much in your opinion? i was thinking it could OS mob packs on polluted path + lod.

**Answer EN:**
two warriors to tank isnt a bad idea per se, its even a good base if you handle multi-accounting well.

that said, 15 chars is clearly too much. having that many holy mages plus a wild keeper, you dont need that at this stage. if your plan is to spend your time soloing IC and you have a good pc and the technique to keep everyone in the same IC without getting cut, then yeah your comp can be interesting. depends if you can level them all to lv85 fast, that takes time.

if youre just here to xp, 3 chars is already a lot honestly, most players run 1 or 2 and thats plenty.

for lod specifically you have two options. either you play 2 warriors, one doing the renés aggro on mount and the other tanking the horn with its pet, plus 6 mage mules to hit. with 6 mages you dont need a wild keeper, they clear aggro fine.

or you stick with 3 chars with the swordsman for aggro, the holy mage and the wild keeper well leveled in element. they clear no problem.

you can also add a second swordsman just to tank the horn with its pet, but his role will be limited to that. he wont xp at the same time, you'd have to level him separately at lod or in a5.1.
