# Q11 : Quelle est la meilleure composition d'équipe en multicompte pour farmer NosFire rapidement ?

**Question EN:** hi, whats the most optimal multi-account team to roll on nosfire fast? lod/quest/raid/lol?

**Answer EN:**
on my side the team i recommend is 1 swordsman in warrior, 1 mage in holy mage and 1 archer in wild keeper.

the swordsman provokes mobs, holy mage heals you with group heal and buffs you with sacred weapon, and wild keeper debuffs enemies with elemental leech and buffs you with wolf and elemental light.

if you want to transfer 2 chars to the classic server after nosfire, the swordsman is almost mandatory given his tank role, and you take one of the other two classes based on preference.

to level all 3 to 85 and get raw gold at server start, a5.1 side quests together are the best plan. lod is an alternative if you already have gold and youre tired of quests. raids and lol come later, once all 3 chars are at lv85.
