# Q28 : La SP1E est-elle jouable jusqu'en THL H80+ ?

**Question EN:** is warrior playable HL - THL +30-c80+? (extended version PIPIDSHAOP: playable in all content or troll at lol, limits vs stone breaker, point distribution by content and level)

**Answer EN:**
warrior is really good up to c30, its base aoe hit is a big advantage to xp on classic maps and its tank role stays useful in many situations.

after c30 limits start to show. to xp at lol you'll want to switch to berserker which is way more efficient there. for classic maps crusader takes over with better light options. in raids if you want to be truly opti, stone breaker is the swordsman fire reference.

what doesnt change: warrior keeps all its value in multi-account for aggro and in setups where you need a tank. its not troll to play it, its just not the best pure dps option past c30.

for sp points, before champion level a mix attack/element works well. from champion level onwards, go full attack and put the rest in element.

**Note:** Warrior = fire element (not light). Crusader = light. Warrior strong up to c30 thanks to base AoE and tank role. After c30: berserker for LoL, crusader for classic maps, stone breaker for opti fire raids. SP points: mix attack/element before HL, full attack + rest in element from HL onwards.
