# Q18 : Pourquoi se connecter tard avant le pop du CI ?

**Question EN:** i have a question for you, why do people tend to try to connect as late as possible before IC pop to be in a single non-split IC? while technically connecting fast and staying connected the whole time amounts to the same. or is there a system im missing?

**Answer EN:**
ic placement is decided when you pick the channel, not the character. the order in which you connect to the channel determines your queue position, and an ic can only fit 30 players.

connecting just before pop lets you arrive last in the queue, where competition is lower. if youre the last to connect your chars, you have a better chance of having them all in the same ic rather than split with others.

staying connected permanently isnt necessarily better. you depend on what others around you in the queue do, and if an ic fills up because of people disco/reco at the right moment, you can end up split without controlling anything. wednesday updates make this worse since everyone disco at the same time.

the difficulty of connecting late is that placement happens in two clicks, channel choice then char choice with a refresh in between, and doing that on several accounts almost simultaneously is really not trivial. thats why most people dont risk it.

**Context:** NosFire 3 (01/04/2026 -> 10/06/2026) + classic servers
**Validation date:** 07/04/2026
**Theme:** IC / multi-account / placement / queue
**Note:** IC placement = channel connection order (not char). 30 max slots per IC. Connecting late = less competition at end of queue = less split risk. Staying connected permanently = depends on others, no control over split. Technical difficulty: two clicks (channel + char) + refresh to manage across multiple accounts simultaneously.
