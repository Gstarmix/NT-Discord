# Q23 : Kliff ou Ragnar pour un mage niveau 67 ?

**Question EN:** hello! beginner question but im picking up nostale after years off. im a lv67 mage looking for the best partner to come along, ive got a lv62 kliff who tanks fine but does no damage and i was thinking of buying ragnar who looks like he has as much hp. only based on stats from nosapki, he seems to deal as little damage as kliff.

**Answer EN:**
for kliff vs ragnar, both are melee partners so their role is to tank mobs while you hit at range
partner damage stays mediocre with or without gear, so ragnar isnt really better than kliff on that point
might as well keep kliff and save your nosdollars, or even play with just your pet in the meantime
partners become really useful later in champion level, and thats also when youll think about gearing him properly: prioritize a weapon that boosts resistances rather than damage (lv65 and lv71 weapons give +10 to all resistances), the highest level armor possible at R0+5 minimum, and multi-resistance accessories like maritime so he tanks well in most situations
for now you have time, it'll come naturally when youre at champion level

**Context:** Classic servers (Dragonveil/Undercity) + NosFire 3
**Validation date:** 14/04/2026
**Theme:** Partners / Kliff vs Ragnar / partner gear
**Note:** Melee partner role = tank, not DPS. Ragnar (50 ND NosMall) replaced for free by Graham via A6.1 quest. Kliff dropped at TS49, comes lv50. Tank partner gear: resistance weapon (lv 67/71, +10 all resistances), high-level armor R0+5 min, maritime. Partner investment relevant only from champion level.
