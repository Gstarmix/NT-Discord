# Q33 : Je meurs en aggro aux ruines infernales pour xp héroïque, quelle map prendre ?

**Question EN:** im dying on aggro at hells ruins, hells ruins but not 2, im getting killed by respawn, im playing near the npcs

**Answer EN:**
take hells ruins 2 not 1, its the path without rement archers with return spot at the entrance, you take less hits at respawn

otherwise ancelloans will 3 in a6.2 as alternative, you try to grab ninja/scout/dark gunner to xp there
