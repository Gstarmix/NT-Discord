# Q27 : Quelle config de mules pour xp les alts héroïques de H1 à H30 ?

**Question EN:** for hero alts, whats the fastest to xp them. two by two, with my main three by three playing all three? 1-30

**Answer EN:**
the route is the same regardless of mule count: hells ruins 2 up to c10 (better suited than ancelloans will since you can do aggros), then polluted path up to c30
duo is the best compromise imo, you have enough power to clear fast without drowning in management
if you want to focus your main, the most comfortable setup is a warrior mule that provokes mobs and your main on a light sp who clears the aggro, that way he tanks nothing and you can really focus on him
i have videos of my xp sessions in duo and solo across several configurations, ill dm you the procedure to see them (and its free obviously)
