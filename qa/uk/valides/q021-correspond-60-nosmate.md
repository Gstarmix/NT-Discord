# Q21 : A quoi correspond le (+60) sur un nosmate ?

**Question EN:** im picking up the game after a few years off! i wanted to know what the (+60) written next to a nosmate means, and how you can level it up?

**Answer EN:**
the (+60) is its training level, a mechanic added a few years ago via the pet trainer sp
the more you train it the more it gains stats and skills
the number in parentheses is the current training level and the cap depends on the number of stars on your nosmate (1 star = 10 levels max, 6 stars = 60 levels max)

to level it you have to play the pet trainer sp, summon protomonsters in your miniland and let your pet fight them
the pet trainer lucie quests in nosville give you the basics

**Context:** Classic servers (Dragonveil/Undercity) + NosFire 3
**Validation date:** 11/04/2026
**Theme:** Nosmate / training / pet trainer SP
**Note:** Training level cap = number of stars x10 (1 star = 10 levels, 6 stars = 60 levels). Increases via Pet Trainer SP + miniland protomonsters. Intro quests via Lucie in NosVille.
