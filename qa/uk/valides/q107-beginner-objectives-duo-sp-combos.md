# Q107 : New player: general objectives and best duo SP combos (two real players)

**Question :** Since there is no beginner guide, would anyone be so kind to give me some general objectives? e.g. get to x level, stop there, farm for x SP and gear etc. I also plan to play as a duo (two actual players, not just two accounts) so I'd appreciate if you could tell me of any op duo SP combos or classes in general

**Réponse UK :**

there is one actually, one progression guide per act, act 1 to 10
https://www.nostar.fr/progress/a1
each act page has a "how to optimise your progression in act X?" section and a "which SP to choose" one, that's literally what you're asking for

short version of the objectives anyway

you start at 56 with the free kit, full lv50 gear, the 4 fairies and your first two sps
from there the main quest carries you to 80, you don't need to buy anything on the way, that's where the game actually starts

80 to 81 is the act 4 quests, then the act 5.1 quests take you from 81 to 85
you grind lod (the act 2 map) alongside them, that's your first real gear and gold step
ic opens at 80 too
after that it's heroic levels, act 6 gets you c1 to c10, act 7 to c30, act 8 to c90

don't sink gold into gear before 80
the starter stuff carries you fine, keep your gold for your sps and your 5.1 gear

for the duo, swordsman + mage is the strongest pair by far
swordsman goes warrior to grab the aggro and hold the packs, mage goes holy mage and aoes everything down
same combo still works when you get to heroic xp, so you don't have to reroll later

archer works too if one of you wants ranged, wild keeper first then demon hunter for the aoe

one thing though, even as a duo you'll each end up with an alt or two at some point
buffs and lod runs are way smoother with 3 chars each

**Relance de Rox (09:52) :** Is War + Holy the best duo for the whole leveling?

**Réponse UK (suite) :**

the pair is right for the whole run, but the sp cards change along the way

warrior is great up to c30, that's where it starts falling behind on damage
after that your swordsman goes berserker, and berserker carries him to c90

same story on the mage side
holy mage is your free sp and it does the job for a long time, then you swap to archmage, still light, way more damage

so war + holy is the correct start, just don't plan on staying on those two forever
what never changes is the setup itself, swordsman grabs the aggro and the mage aoes the pack down, only the sp cards under it get swapped

**Relance de Rox (10:07) :** Thank you very much sir. Could you give me a list of the things we should buy in order of priority?

**Réponse UK (suite) :**

sure, in order

before 55 you buy nothing at all
the free kit at 56 already hands you the lv50 gear, the 4 fairies (one per element) and your first two sps

if you're willing to put money in, the one thing worth it is the starter pack in the nosmall, 330 nd
it's the best value purchase in the shop by far, everything else can wait

and never buy your nosdollars outside a happy hour
gameforge runs +30% ones roughly every two weeks, usually on a saturday
the 49.99 euro offer is the sweet spot, around 1180 nd normally, around 1520 nd during a happy hour
so waiting for the right day is basically a free 30%

after that your gold goes into your sps, that's the real money pit
you either buy them at the bazaar or you run the sp quests

what you're aiming for is one light sp and one dark sp each
the mobs at lod, hell's ruins 2 and the contaminated path are dark, so you clear them with the light sps, crusader on the swordsman and archmage on the mage
the mobs at lol are mostly light, so there you swap to the dark ones, berserker on the swordsman and seer on the mage

and don't sleep on the soulstones
the main quests of act 4, act 6 and act 7 each hand you one, and each one takes an sp straight to 41+9
so think before you use them

then your gear and the sl on it, but only from c10
below that, sl is a nightmare to find and you're paying for nothing

pet, partner and costume, you pick them up along the way, depending on how your gold comes in
they help a lot through the whole run, so don't forget them

and keep some gold aside for potions and xp buffs, that's your running cost and beginners always forget it

**Relance de Rox (10:48) :** So I assume starting at level 1 is better than starting at 56?

**Réponse UK (suite) :**

nah, the other way around, take the 56

starting at 1 gains you nothing
the levels below 56 hand out no gear worth keeping, no fairy, no sp, and the mobs there teach you nothing about the actual game

the 56 start is exactly what puts you in the game with the lv50 gear, the 4 fairies and your first two sps in hand
that's material you would have to farm or buy otherwise

the game really begins at 80 anyway, everything before that is just the road to get there
so skipping the emptiest part of that road is a free head start, not a loss

**Relance de Rox (11:23) :** Is upgrading the Dark/Light SPs to +9 for free enough for the whole leveling process or is it better to buy better SPs as soon as possible?
**Reformulation de Rox (13:47) :** So basically 56-C30 free War +9 / Holy +9, C30+ we buy like Zerk +15 / Archmage +15

**Réponse UK (suite) :**

your plan is right, the free +9 is exactly what carries you to c30

small thing though, war isn't a light sp, it's fire
it doesn't hold up early because of its element, it holds up because of the provoke, you grab the whole pack and your mage aoes it down
so war + holy isn't a light/dark pair, it's a tank/dps pair

two things to keep in mind

don't push war or holy past +9 yourself
the soulstone puts them at 41+9 for free and that's exactly where you stop
+10 and up costs a fortune and you're dropping those two sps at c30 anyway, that's gold burnt

and +15 isn't the entry ticket at c30
you buy the sp first, you upgrade it over time
a zerk +9 already beats a war +9 at lol just because the element is right, so don't wait until you can afford +15 to make the swap
also don't forget the +100, the perfection matters as much as the upgrade level

one trick while you're at it
you get three soulstones per char, blue at act 4, silver at act 6, golden at act 7
put the blue on your free sp, but save the golden one for the sp you'll buy
it works on a level 1 +0 card even if it's already perfected +100, takes it straight to 41+9, and you keep the perfection
so your bought zerk or archmage lands at +9 without you paying a single upgrade

that's basically a free head start on the exact sp you actually plan to keep
