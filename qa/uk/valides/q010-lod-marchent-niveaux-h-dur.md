# Q10 : C'est quoi le LoD, comment marchent les niveaux H, et c'est dur le multicompte ?

**Question EN:** what is the lod? (sry im really new) when you say c10 is that lv85 to lv99 (+10)?? i watch your videos and multi-accounting looks tempting even tho idk if its hard to switch all the time

**Answer EN:**
lod is the land of death, an act 2 map where mobs give a lot of xp between lv65 and 80. thats where most players farm with mage mules.

for champion levels, its a second progression tier that starts after lv85, from act 6 onwards. c10 means champion level 10, not level 95. the cap is c99.

for lod multi-accounting, the switching itself isnt that complicated once your setup is in place. you leave your mules where they are and manage the main tank. the most demanding part is setting up at the start, but once running it almost runs itself.
