# Q9 : Quelle est la différence entre NosFire et Undercity ?

**Question EN:** whats the difference between the two? (NosFire vs Undercity)

**Answer EN:**
nosfire is a temporary event server, you start lv80 with free lv80 R5+7 gear and theres xp/gold bonuses. it lasts 10 weeks and at the end you can transfer your char to an official server like undercity.

undercity is a permanent server, at character creation you choose between starting lv1 or lv56 with free lv50 R5+7 gear, no xp/gold boost, but thats where you play long term.

basically nosfire is to level fast and get a clean start, undercity is the "normal" game.
