# Solvent (item enhancement) continue button not responding

**Q:** When using a solvent on an item, clicking "Continue" on the confirmation prompt sometimes does nothing, even after relogging or moving the item in the inventory.

**A:** This is a known issue as of May 2024: the confirmation button can fail to register clicks. As a workaround, use the mouse and click the continue button slowly/deliberately rather than clicking rapidly; in some cases the action only starts working again after a few hours or a relog. The team acknowledged the interaction is not reliable and said they are looking into it.
