# What items from Infernal Crypt (IC) are actually worth selling?

**Q:** Do the items obtained from Infernal Crypt sell for anything? Is it only the legendary ones that are worth something, and can legendary items actually drop from IC?

**A:** Legendary items from IC are drops (also obtainable in raids), and they are the only ones worth much. The most valuable IC rewards overall are the gold given after each round and the full moon/angel feathers given after the last mob round (before the boss round); you don't need to deal any damage in that final round, just survive it to get the rewards. However, dealing meaningful damage in round 4 generally requires at least C10 equipment or strong SP builds, since mobs there die very fast, so lower geared players should focus on surviving IC for the free gold, while C10+ players can try to farm all the rewards.
