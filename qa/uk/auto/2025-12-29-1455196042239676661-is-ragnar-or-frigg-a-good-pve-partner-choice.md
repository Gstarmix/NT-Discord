# Is Ragnar or Frigg a good PvE partner choice?

**Q:** Is Ragnar actually one of the best partners for PvE, and is it worth buying Frigg instead?

**A:** The best partners for PvE are generally considered to be the Sundae or the Gingerbread. Since those are the most expensive options, Ragnar is a decent alternative thanks to skills that help with luring monsters.
