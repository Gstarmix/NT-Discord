# Main story blocked after TS68 due to missing laboratory material

**Q:** After completing the laboratory side missions, my main story quest routed me to TS68 and then got stuck because a laboratory material item seems to be missing. Is there a fix, and can the laboratory mission still be finished for its reward afterward?

**A:** Known issue as of this date: relogging fixes the main quest progression, since the laboratory side story is unrelated to the main quest chain — you can even cancel the laboratory quest afterward without affecting your main quest. The laboratory mission's item reward isn't used anywhere else in the game, so there's little point pursuing it, but if you still want it, contact support via ticket since the item appears to have gone missing due to a bug.
