# Why can't Martial Artists wear many costumes, masks, or hats?

**Q:** Why can't Martial Artists equip many costumes, masks, or hats that other classes can wear?

**A:** It's not a bug — the game simply hasn't had costume textures/models made for the Martial Artist class for every item yet. The developers add compatibility for more costumes to the class every now and then, so support is gradually expanded over time rather than being available for all items at once.
