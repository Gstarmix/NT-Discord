# Rune Polish sometimes fails to reroll an unwanted rune option

**Q:** After using a Rune Polish (reset) item on a rune, I ended up with two options I didn't want, seemingly because the polish silently failed to apply on a previous use. Is this a known bug?

**A:** Opinions differ among players. Some say Rune Polish cannot be reliably reapplied twice in a row and that players must verify the result themselves before reusing it, treating this as intended behavior rather than a bug. Others describe it as a known, rare bug that has been confirmed and reported multiple times. If it happens, the recommended step is to open a support ticket, as GMs have reverted rune options for affected players before.
