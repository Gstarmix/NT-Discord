# How does the +75% champion experience bonus actually apply?

**Q:** Does a +75% champion experience bonus (e.g. from Tartharpendam) mean a flat addition (30% becomes 52%), or is it a multiplicative bonus applied within the experience formula rather than a straightforward final increase?

**A:** It is not a flat/final percentage addition — it's a bonus factored into the underlying experience formula. As an example from testing at c70 in the same group: without the Tartharpendam buff, champion exp went from 26.60% to 58.58% in one kill; with the buff active, the same type of kill took champion exp from 58.58% to 99.54%. This confirms the bonus scales the gained experience rather than simply adding a fixed percentage.
