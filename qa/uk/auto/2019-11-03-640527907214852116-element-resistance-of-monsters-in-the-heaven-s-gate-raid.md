# Element resistance of monsters in the Heaven's Gate raid

**Q:** What are the elemental resistances of monsters in the Heaven's Gate raid?

**A:** In the Heaven's Gate raid, all regular monsters are neutral element (no elemental resistance/weakness) until you reach a guardian or the Mysticaron boss.
