# What to do with an accidentally crafted duplicate Nezarun Fairy +5 book

**Q:** I accidentally crafted a second Nezarun Fairy +5 book. Is there anything I can do with the extra one?

**A:** There's no in-game way to reuse or refund a duplicate crafted book. The suggested option is to open a support ticket, since staff may be able to refund the cores that were used.
