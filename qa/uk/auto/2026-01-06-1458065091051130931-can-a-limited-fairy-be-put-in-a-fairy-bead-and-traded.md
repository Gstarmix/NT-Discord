# Can a limited fairy be put in a fairy bead and traded?

**Q:** Can a fairy marked as 'limited' still be placed in a fairy bead and exchanged/traded? Also, can such fairies be used to create higher-tier fairies like Zenas, Erenia, or Fernon?

**A:** No — anything limited (pet, partner, PSP, fairy, etc.) cannot be traded, even inside a fairy bead. Drones don't require fairies to be made, and limited fairies cannot be used to craft Act 6 fairies (unless this has since changed).
