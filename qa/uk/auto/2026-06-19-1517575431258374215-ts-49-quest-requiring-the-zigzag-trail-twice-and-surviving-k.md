# TS 49 quest requiring the Zigzag Trail twice, and surviving Kliff

**Q:** My quest requires doing TS 49, but I already completed it once in the Zigzag Trail. Do I need to do it again?

**A:** This quest is known to be buggy and requires completing that time square twice. During the fight against Kliff, you can tank or heal him to keep him alive more easily; a cheap consumable item available in the bazaar can restore his HP and make the fight less punishing if you don't want to wait for natural regeneration.
