# How the Glaceron percentage bonus works

**Q:** How does the Glaceron % (passive bonus) mechanic work, do I need to actively play on every account to get it?

**A:** Once you have more than about 23 to 26 characters present, the percentage bonus keeps accumulating passively, you don't need to actively do anything on those accounts, just have enough characters online.
