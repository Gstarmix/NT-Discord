# Battle potions cannot be used to attack teammates during raids

**Q:** Battle potions can be used during raids, but they cannot be used to attack allied players. Can this be enabled for advanced raid strategies?

**A:** This is intentional behavior, not a bug: battle potions are not meant to let players attack their own teammates during raids. Staff confirmed this will not be changed, noting the request was seen as an attempt to bring an arena-style tactic (used with skills like Ice Princess Eliza and Chloe) into raids, which is not an intended mechanic there.
