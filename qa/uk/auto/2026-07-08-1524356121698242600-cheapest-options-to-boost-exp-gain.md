# Cheapest options to boost exp gain

**Q:** What are the cheaper items or food that increase exp gain, and are there better alternatives to standard exp dishes?

**A:** Rather than regular exp dishes, a specific food item is a stronger option than the special item Ancelloan's Blessing for exp gain, though the two do not stack with each other; Ancelloan's Blessing alone is enough for running Land of Death. Frogfish gives a similar boost but is slightly more expensive than a regular exp potion. Equipping the Star tarot card also adds 15% extra exp. An online exp simulator tool can be used to compare different gear/food loadouts and estimate exp gain.
