# Removing or changing an SPP partner removes an equipment HP bonus

**Q:** When removing or changing an SPP (Special Partner) partner, the character loses HP that came from an equipment bonus (5k HP in the reported case). Is this intended?

**A:** This is a known issue: swapping or removing an SPP partner can incorrectly strip an HP bonus that comes from equipment. Another player confirmed experiencing the same behavior and it has been reported to the team; no workaround is currently known.
