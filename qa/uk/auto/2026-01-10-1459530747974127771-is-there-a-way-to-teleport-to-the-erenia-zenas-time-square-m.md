# Is there a way to teleport to the Erenia/Zenas time square map?

**Q:** Is there a way to teleport directly to the Erenia/Zenas time square (TS) map?

**A:** Yes, there is a portal on the Cylloan map, located on the lower-right part of the map. Crossing it teleports you instantly to the Erenia/Zenas time square map.
