# Ground targeted skills like Blue Whale or Meteor Storm cannot have their cooldown reduced

**Q:** Is it intentional that ground targeted skills such as Blue Whale or Meteor Storm cannot have their cooldown reset by effects like the passive proc from wings or set bonuses, even though these skills do not have the "cooldown of this effect cannot be reduced" tag?

**A:** This is a known issue as of the message date: ground targeted skills are excluded from several cooldown reduction effects (including wing procs and set bonuses) even though nothing in their description indicates this. A staff member confirmed this is not intended and said it would be checked and added to the internal issue list. No workaround exists yet.
