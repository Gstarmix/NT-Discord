# Why do characters get frozen and fail during the Laurena raid

**Q:** Is there a hidden time limit for killing monsters in the Laurena raid? A player kept failing the raid, especially when several Caligor adds spawned while Laurena was still at around 25% HP, and asked why their characters got frozen.

**A:** The only timer that matters is the one shown in the bottom left of the screen. The Laurena raid was not designed to be completed with just 1 or 2 characters, so it is expected that all of a small party's characters can get frozen (unable to act) during the fight due to the raid's mechanics.
