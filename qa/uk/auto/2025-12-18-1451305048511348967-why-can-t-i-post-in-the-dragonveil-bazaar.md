# Why can't I post in the Dragonveil bazaar?

**Q:** Why can't I post an item in the Dragonveil bazaar? It says I don't have the right to do it.

**A:** You need to be at least level 5 to post an item in the Dragonveil bazaar, and level 10 to edit or reply to an existing post.
