# Should you level 3 accounts simultaneously for better profit?

**Q:** I was told that running 3 accounts is more profitable. Should I do the main quest on all 3 accounts at the same time?

**A:** Yes, it's better to progress all three accounts through the main quest simultaneously rather than one after another. However, the real gold/profit benefit of multi-accounting mainly becomes significant once characters reach around level 81-99+.
