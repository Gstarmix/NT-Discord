# Which Act storylines still reward equipment after Act 6?

**Q:** Is Act 6.1 the last storyline chapter that rewards equipment, or does Act 7 also give equipment?

**A:** Act 6.1 is not the last one — both Act 6.1 and Act 7.1 (possibly Act 7.2, though this part is less certain) reward equipment for completing their respective quests.
