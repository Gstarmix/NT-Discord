# Is Avenging Angel much better than War King for Archer?

**Q:** Is the Avenging Angel (AA) SP card that much better than War King (WK) for an Archer?

**A:** Not by much in general, but it depends on your build. If you plan to use the dagger as your main weapon (for Dark Head), Avenging Angel becomes better than War King. Before reaching that point, the comparison mostly depends on your bow to dagger damage ratio.
