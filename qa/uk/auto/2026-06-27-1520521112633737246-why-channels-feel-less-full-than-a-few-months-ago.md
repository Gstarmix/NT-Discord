# Why channels feel less full than a few months ago

**Q:** A few months ago channels 1 and 2 were full around 90% of the time, but now channel 1 is just busy and the rest are normal. What happened?

**A:** The channel capacity was increased, so the same number of players are now spread out and channels appear less full than before.
