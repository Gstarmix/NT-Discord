# Chef expert card Fry ability shows no smoke

**Q:** When using the Chef expert card's Fry ability, no smoke appears above the pan, even though the game tells you to stop cooking once the smoke turns black. What causes this?

**A:** The smoke effect can fail to display if the cooking spot has a cluttered background. Try cooking on a clear floor area, ideally in an empty miniland, so the smoke animation shows correctly.
