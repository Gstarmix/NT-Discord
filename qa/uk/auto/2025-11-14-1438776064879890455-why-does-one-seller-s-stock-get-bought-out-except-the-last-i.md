# Why does one seller's stock get bought out except the last item?

**Q:** Why do players buy up every unit of an item from a marketplace seller but leave the very last one unsold, so the whole marketplace ends up dominated by a single item at a fixed price?

**A:** This is a price-optimization tactic: buyers deliberately leave the last unit of a stack unsold so the listed price for that item doesn't reset or drop. If someone else wants to sell the same item, they now have to undercut that remaining listing, keeping the market price effectively anchored rather than falling.
