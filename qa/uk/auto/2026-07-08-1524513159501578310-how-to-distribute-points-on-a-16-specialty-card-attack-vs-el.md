# How to distribute points on a +16 specialty card: attack vs element vs HP

**Q:** On a +16 specialty card, is it better to build 100 attack / 95 element, or shift points toward higher attack (around 105) with slightly lower element (around 90)? Is it ever better to put points into HP instead, since HP also grants attack power at certain breakpoints?

**A:** The recommended allocation is the higher-attack option (around 105 SL Damage) paired with roughly 90 SL Property/element, since this still reaches the elemental breakpoint while maximizing attack. Diverting points into HP for its attack-power breakpoint bonus is not the preferred route in this case.
