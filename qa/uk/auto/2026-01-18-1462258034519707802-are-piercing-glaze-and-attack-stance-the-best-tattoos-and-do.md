# Are Piercing Glaze and Attack Stance the best tattoos, and does Attack Stance increase damage taken?

**Q:** Are Piercing Glaze and Attack Stance the two best tattoos to have? Attack Stance shows an effect "damage taken from all attacks is increased by 2%" — does that mean I take 2% more damage overall?

**A:** Yes, Piercing Glaze and Attack Stance are considered the two best tattoos, and yes, Attack Stance does increase the damage you take by 2%. In practice this downside rarely matters, since a lot of end-game content revolves around percentage-of-HP damage, which is unaffected by this flat damage-taken increase, and the offensive benefit from the tattoo is large enough to outweigh it for most classes/levels.
