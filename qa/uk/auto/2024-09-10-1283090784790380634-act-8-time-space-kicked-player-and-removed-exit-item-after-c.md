# Act 8 time space kicked player and removed exit item after clearing all rooms

**Q:** After clearing all rooms of an act 8 time space in a party for over an hour, my teammate could leave normally, but portals showed as unlocked yet gave 'portal closed' when I tried to use them, and eventually the time space kicked me out. Afterward the exit item was no longer in my inventory.

**A:** This is likely the act 8 time space that requires having a guardian's magic orb in your inventory to exit; check the time space description before entering. According to another player there should be no exit issue as long as you already have the required item in your inventory (not in the warehouse or elsewhere) at the moment you enter the time space; picking it up only after entering may be the cause of exit problems.
