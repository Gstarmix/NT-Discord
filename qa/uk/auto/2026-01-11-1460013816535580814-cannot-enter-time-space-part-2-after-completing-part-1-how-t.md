# Cannot enter Time-Space part 2 after completing part 1 / how to check completed Time-Spaces

**Q:** At level 57, after finishing Time-Space (TS) part 1, I can't enter part 2. Why, and how can I quickly check which Time-Spaces I've completed?

**A:** If you can't enter the next part of a Time-Space after finishing the previous one, it may be because another member of your party hasn't finished it themselves. To check your completed Time-Spaces, open the Character Info window with P, then go to the Act tab, which lists all completed Time-Spaces per act.
