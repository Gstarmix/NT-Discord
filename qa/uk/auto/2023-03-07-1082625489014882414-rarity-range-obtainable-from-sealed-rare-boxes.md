# Rarity range obtainable from sealed rare boxes

**Q:** When opening sealed rare item boxes, which rarity levels can drop, and do the highest rarities (r7/r8) stay separate?

**A:** Opening sealed rare boxes gives a random item of rarity 1 to 6 (r1-r6). Rarity 7 and 8 items are not included in these boxes and remain obtainable only through other, separate means.
