# How to get the PvE Titan title

**Q:** How do you obtain the PvE Titan title, which gives +10% attack, +1 movement speed, and a 10% chance to reset the cooldown of the attack skill used?

**A:** The PvE Titan title is an exclusive reward from the Nosversary event and cannot currently be obtained outside of it. It is not tradeable and not sold for gold. There is a precedent though: the Raid Master title was originally Nosversary exclusive too, but was later made available through normal means (NM), so the same could eventually happen for PvE Titan, though there is no confirmed timeline for that.
