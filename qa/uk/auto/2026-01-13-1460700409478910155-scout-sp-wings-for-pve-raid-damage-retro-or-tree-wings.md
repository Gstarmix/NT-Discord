# Scout SP wings for PvE raid damage: Retro or Tree wings?

**Q:** For a Scout SP, which wings are better for PvE raid boss damage: Retro wings or Tree wings?

**A:** Tree wings are recommended over Retro wings for raid boss damage, since they give a constant 10% attack bonus that is always active, requiring no proc. Retro wings rely on a buff proc that triggers very rarely, often only when the boss is almost dead, making it far less reliable.
