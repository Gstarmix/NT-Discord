# Natural HP/SP regeneration blocked while pet fights in Pet Trainer cages

**Q:** Is it a known bug that natural HP/SP regeneration doesn't work while standing still (not sitting) in town, even with regen-boosting equipment, if my pet is currently fighting?

**A:** Natural regeneration itself works fine on all servers under normal conditions; it only stops while the character or their pet is in combat. Having a pet fight monsters in the Pet Trainer cages counts as being in combat, so natural regen gets blocked during that. This has been a known issue since the Pet Trainer cages feature was released and has already been reported.
