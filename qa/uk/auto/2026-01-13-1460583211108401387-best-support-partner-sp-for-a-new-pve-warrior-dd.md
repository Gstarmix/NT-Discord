# Best Support Partner (SP) for a new PvE Warrior DD

**Q:** As a new player playing Warrior focused on PvE damage dealing (currently with a level 99 +5 Berserker and a low-level Crusader), which SP is best? Is Renegade good, or is something like Battle Monk better? Also stuck around act 6 due to dark mobs and looking for a more versatile option after already maxing a Varik.

**A:** Berserker is a solid choice for most of the game, and Stone Breaker is also very good, especially against Sharks at high combat level. Renegade is a fine SP too, but Crusader is generally considered the better "light" SP overall, though maining Crusader end-game requires a separate crossbow gear set (for leveling, the same set can work for both Renegade and Crusader). Note that finishing the act 4, act 6 and act 7 questlines rewards soulstones that can upgrade your Berserker to +9 for free, and similarly a silver soulstone works for Renegade while a golden soulstone can be used later for Stone Breaker or SP12.
