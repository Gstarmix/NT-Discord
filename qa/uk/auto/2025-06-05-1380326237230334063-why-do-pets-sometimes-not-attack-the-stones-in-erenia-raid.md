# Why do pets sometimes not attack the stones in Erenia Raid?

**Q:** In the Erenia Raid, why do companion pets sometimes fail to attack the stones?

**A:** Pets not attacking is usually not a raid-specific bug: it typically happens because the pet did not teleport in with you / did not follow you, or because it got stuck in Attack/Follow (A/F) mode. Checking the pet's follow/attack state and resummoning it if needed should resolve the issue.
