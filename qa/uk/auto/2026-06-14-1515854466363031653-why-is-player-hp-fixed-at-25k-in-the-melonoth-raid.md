# Why is player HP fixed at 25k in the Melonoth raid

**Q:** In the Melonoth raid, player HP is fixed at 25k. Is this specific to Melonoth or do other raid events also fix player HP?

**A:** This fixed HP mechanic is specific to Melonoth, not a general raid feature. Melonoth is a relatively recent raid addition, and it was designed with this HP cap specifically to prevent players from exploiting overly high HP stats.
