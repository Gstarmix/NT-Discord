# What gives a chance at a second raid box besides the Challenger title?

**Q:** Besides the Challenger title, are there other titles, minipets, or bonuses that give a percentage chance for a second raid box?

**A:** Several sources give an extra chance at a raid box: the Challenger title (5%), certain minipets (5%), the Hardcore Token passive book (5%, also obtainable as a Nosfire battle pass reward), and the Catacombs family buff (25% for one hour per day). These bonuses all stack and work independently from the double box event, so it is possible to get up to three boxes per raid.
