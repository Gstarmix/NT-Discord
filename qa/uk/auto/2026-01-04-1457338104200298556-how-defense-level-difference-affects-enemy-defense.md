# How defense level difference affects enemy defense

**Q:** If an enemy's defense level is higher than my attack level, does the effective defense go negative or does something else happen?

**A:** A higher defense level than your attack level gives the enemy a bonus to their defense stats, similar to an armor bonus. For example, if the enemy's defense level is 10 and your attack level is 8, they get roughly a +2 level bonus, translating to about a 15% defense increase.
