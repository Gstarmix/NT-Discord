# Does resetting pet training lose the pet's stars

**Q:** I have a +60 Panda pet trained with certain skills, but I was told the build is bad. If I reset the pet's training, do I keep its 6 stars? And which skills should I aim for on a mage?

**A:** Resetting a pet's training only resets the hero training level (the +60 progress), not the star rating, so the stars are kept. Common skill goals are Voke or Stun depending on personal playstyle - there's no single correct choice, so it's better to build around your own style rather than copying others exactly.
