**Q:** In the Act 9/10 TS area, how do I turn a marker from blue to green? Is it possible without doing the related quest?

**A:** To turn it green you need to fully complete the associated quest, or alternatively use a green paint item to change it directly. This is not part of the main Act 9 questline, so it can be skipped entirely if you do not need it.
