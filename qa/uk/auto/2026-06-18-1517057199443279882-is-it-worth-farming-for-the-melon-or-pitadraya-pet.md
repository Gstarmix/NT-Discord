# Is it worth farming for the Melon or Pitadraya pet

**Q:** If you already have 10 fruits from event boxes, is it worth farming further for a fruit pet like Melon or Pitadraya, and how valuable are they?

**A:** Melon and Pitadraya are considered the top fruit pet picks. The Melon pet adds an extra 10% chance on top of the base card perfection success rate, while the Pitadraya pet is a favorite among PvP players in Act 4.
