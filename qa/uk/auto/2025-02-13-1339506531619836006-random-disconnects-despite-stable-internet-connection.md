# Random disconnects despite stable internet connection

**Q:** Players with normally stable internet connections experienced multiple random disconnects within a short time (e.g. 3 disconnects in 15 minutes), affecting some characters on a channel while others stayed connected, and persisting even after switching channels or reducing the number of logged-in characters.

**A:** This was a known issue at the time affecting multiple players across different servers (reported on INT-Valehir and INT-Alzanor), with connections becoming randomly unstable regardless of the number of characters logged in or the channel used. No official fix or workaround was given beyond noting that the instability appeared server-side rather than due to the players' own network.
