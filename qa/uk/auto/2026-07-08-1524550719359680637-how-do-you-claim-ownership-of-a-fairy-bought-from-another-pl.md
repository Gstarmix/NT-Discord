# How do you claim ownership of a fairy bought from another player?

**Q:** If I buy someone else's fairy, what item do I need to use to make it mine?

**A:** You need to use perfume on the fairy to transfer ownership to yourself. The required amount of perfume depends on the fairy's level, and there is a community-made chart showing exact amounts per level, though overall the quantities needed are relatively small.
