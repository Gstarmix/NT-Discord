# What happens on a Major Failure during shell upgrade?

**Q:** What does a Major Failure mean when upgrading a weapon shell? Will I lose the weapon?

**A:** A Major Failure does not destroy the weapon itself. You only lose the last shell upgrade level, similar to how tattoo upgrades work on a failure.
