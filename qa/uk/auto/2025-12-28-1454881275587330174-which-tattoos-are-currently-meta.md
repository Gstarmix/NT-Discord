# Which tattoos are currently meta?

**Q:** Which tattoos are currently considered meta?

**A:** For general PvE, Attack Stance is good for every class. For Critic-focused classes, Piercing Gaze or Growing Experience are recommended. For Mage, Arcane Wisdom (or alternatively Curse Transformation) is the go-to choice.
