# Do you need a fragrance to equip a clean Steam Drone Shadow Pearl

**Q:** If I open a Steam Drone Shadow Pearl and get a clean 80% item with no upgrade level, can I equip it normally, or do I need to use a perfume/fragrance on it first?

**A:** If the item is not bound, you can equip and use it normally without any fragrance. However, if the item was previously upgraded by someone else and then had its shell removed, it will already be bound, and in that case you will need to use a fragrance on it before equipping.
