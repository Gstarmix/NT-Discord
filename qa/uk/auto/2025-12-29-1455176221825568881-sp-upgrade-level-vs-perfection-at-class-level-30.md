# SP upgrade level vs perfection at class level 30

**Q:** Around class level 30, should I prioritize a higher + (upgrade) on my Seer SP or a better perfection (upgraded to +9 with an orb)?

**A:** It's important to reach around 100 element/property and 50-60 attack from your SPs. If your equipment already has good SL stats and you can hit those numbers anyway, a better perfection is more valuable than a higher +. Otherwise, prioritize an SP with a higher + upgrade. For pure champion level purposes, a Perti-type or similar SP with comparable bonuses works too.
