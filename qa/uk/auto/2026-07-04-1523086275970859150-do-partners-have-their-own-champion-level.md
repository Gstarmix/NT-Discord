# Do partners have their own champion level?

**Q:** Does a partner's champion level increase on its own?

**A:** Partners do not have their own champion level (CLv); their effective level for equipment purposes depends on the owning character's champion level. For example, to equip a partner with champion level 28 (c28) armor, the player themselves must be champion level 28 or higher.
