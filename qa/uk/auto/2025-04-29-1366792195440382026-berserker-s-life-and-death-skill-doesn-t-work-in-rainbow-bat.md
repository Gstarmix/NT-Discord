# Berserker's Life and Death skill doesn't work in Rainbow Battle

**Q:** Why doesn't the Berserker skill Life and Death, which is supposed to grant immortality when used at critically low HP, work during Rainbow Battle?

**A:** This is a known issue: the skill's immortality effect appears to rely on a true near-death trigger, but since characters never actually die in Rainbow Battle, the effect fails to activate. This was acknowledged as a noted known issue.
