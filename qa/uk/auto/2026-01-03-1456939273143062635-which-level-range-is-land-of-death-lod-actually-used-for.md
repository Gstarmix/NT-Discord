# Which level range is Land of Death (LoD) actually used for?

**Q:** Is Land of Death (LoD) meant to be used starting from level 1, or only from level 55/65? How does it compare to questing for leveling speed?

**A:** LoD is only relevant for the level 56 to 65 range, not from level 1. Below that range, doing quests levels you up faster than farming LoD. Going from level 65 to 85 via LoD takes roughly 5 to 7 runs, which is about a full day of play.
