# How to equip a blessing (can't drag it from SP window)

**Q:** Why can't I drag a blessing item to equip it, and how do I actually apply it?

**A:** A blessing cannot be dragged directly from the SP (skill points) window. Instead, open the skills window with the K key and drag the blessing from there.
