# Graphic freezing and rubber-banding when changing maps

**Q:** I frequently experience graphic bugs: frame drops and jumpy movement while mobbing, freezing on map transitions (e.g. the A9 lab map) followed by teleporting backward several seconds later, which mobbing partners also notice. What causes this?

**A:** According to a helper, these graphic glitches and freezes on map transitions are in most cases caused by changing maps while mounted. Avoiding mounts when moving between maps reduces or eliminates the problem for most players. The exact root cause isn't fully confirmed, and the team was still collecting player feedback on whether it also happens without a party member accompanying you.
