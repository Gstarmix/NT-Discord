# Character appearance, titles and pets reset after switching channels

**Q:** After playing a Mara raid on channel 7 and logging into channel 1, my account shows a different appearance (hair color changed before I logged in), no titles available (which blocks progress on the trainer pet SP), no pets, and no NPCs in my company/family (family level not showing 19). A support ticket was answered that there is no solution and no compensation is given. What can be done?

**A:** This is a known issue as of August 2024 with no fix and no compensation offered by support. The only known workaround is to keep playing on a channel where the game and your data load correctly, and to avoid switching to channels where it does not. If possible, try loading the game from a different computer to check whether the issue is client side or account side.
