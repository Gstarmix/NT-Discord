# Family/pet training stat bonuses not visible on partner's right-click profile

**Q:** Why don't my pet's family buff stat increases show up when I right-click Lumi's profile, even though the bonus is visible in the P (partner) menu?

**A:** Pet trainer stats are a separate system that permanently boosts a pet's stats based on its stars/training level, and those are shown on the profile. Family buffs, however, are only temporary bonuses applied while the partner accompanies you, and they currently aren't reflected on the right-click profile display — this has been noted as something to potentially address in the future.
