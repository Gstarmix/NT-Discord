# Is it worth buying an SP+15 without PP if you mainly play PvE

**Q:** Is it worth buying an SP (Specialist card) at +15 without PP (perfect points) for someone who mainly plays NosTale for PvE content?

**A:** Yes, you can buy the SP+15 without PP and use it in the meantime. Later, once you have saved enough gold or resources, you can sell it and use the funds to buy or upgrade to an SP with around +100 PP.
