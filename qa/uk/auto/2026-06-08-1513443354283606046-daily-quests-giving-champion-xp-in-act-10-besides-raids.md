# Daily quests giving champion XP in Act 10 besides raids

**Q:** Besides the Nezarun and Crushed Nezarun raids, are there any other daily quests in Act 10 that give champion XP?

**A:** Yes: the Enrico daily quest (5x Alzanor and 5x Valehir) gives champion XP, as do the Pollu and Arma quests. The Cooking Frog quest and the AI Robot quest on the Act 9 map also grant champion XP.
