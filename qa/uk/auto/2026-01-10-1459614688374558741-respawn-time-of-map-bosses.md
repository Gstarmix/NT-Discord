# Respawn time of map bosses

**Q:** How long does it take for regular map bosses to respawn?

**A:** Normal map bosses, like the ones in Act 6, respawn roughly every 2 hours. Other special bosses (e.g. Valakus' Shadow, Poluto, Nezarun) instead respawn based on a percentage chance that increases per mob killed on the map, rather than on a fixed timer.
