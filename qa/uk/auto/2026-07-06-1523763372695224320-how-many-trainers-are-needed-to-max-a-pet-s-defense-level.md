# How many trainers are needed to max a pet's defense level?

**Q:** What is the maximum, or most efficient, number of trainers needed to train a pet's defense level, since some say 8 and others say 45?

**A:** Using 8 trainers is the most efficient option since it avoids running back and forth between attacks. 45 trainers is technically the maximum possible amount, but using that many is very inefficient.
