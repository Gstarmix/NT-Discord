# Does Fafnir's debuff removal still work against the Illusion debuff

**Q:** The Illusion skill now applies a level 4 debuff. Does Fafnir's snack, which removes debuffs, still counter it in PvP?

**A:** No. Fafnir's snack only removes debuffs up to level 3, while the Illusion debuff is level 4, so players can no longer use Fafnir to cleanse it in PvP. This closed off a previous way to abuse Fafnir against Illusion. There is currently no known way to remove a level 4 debuff (only tools that clear level 3 or lower exist).
