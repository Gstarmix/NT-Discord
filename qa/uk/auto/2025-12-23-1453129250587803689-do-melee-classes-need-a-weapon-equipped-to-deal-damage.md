# Do melee classes need a weapon equipped to deal damage?

**Q:** As a Sin or Archer Assassin, I can remove both main-hand and off-hand weapons and still hit enemies without a dagger equipped. Is this a bug? Also, do main-hand-reliant classes (Wildkeeper, Scout, Ranger) actually need their weapon equipped to deal damage?

**A:** This is not a bug — it applies to every weapon type except ranged weapons (bows and mage guns). You can attack without the weapon equipped, but you simply won't deal good damage without it.
