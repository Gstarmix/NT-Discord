# Partner (pet) stuck on the ground in miniland, not clickable

**Q:** My partner Jennifer is stuck on the floor in my miniland — she isn't following me and I can't click on her, even from the miniland UI list (similar to the raid list). How can I fix this?

**A:** This is a known bug where a partner becomes stuck and non-interactable in miniland. A server relog (sometimes only after the next maintenance) can fix it. Other workarounds to try: unequip the partner's gear via the P menu (select the specific partner), then use a battle potion while in miniland, and have a friend cast an AoE skill near the partner — this doesn't require directly targeting them and can reset the stuck state.
