# Leveling alternatives to bees between level 58 and 60

**Q:** Is there any way to level from 58 to 60 besides farming bees?

**A:** Polluted Path is an alternative, but it is not recommended: at character level 59 it only gives around 9% experience per hour, making it much slower than farming bees.
