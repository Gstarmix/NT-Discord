# How does the double drop event bonus actually work

**Q:** Are the bonus drop-rate effects limited during a double drop event? It feels like a much lower chance than expected even after farming for two hours.

**A:** The event bonus is not a flat 75% increase, it is two separate rolls of 50% and 25%. Being in a group prevents the bonus from applying. There is no requirement for the item to physically drop on the ground for the bonus to apply, it also works on drops that go straight into the inventory, as confirmed by a screenshot showing multiple event items received directly.
