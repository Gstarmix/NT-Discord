# How to survive heavy damage while leveling as a low-level character

**Q:** While leveling, my character takes huge amounts of damage. What can I use to mitigate this at a low level?

**A:** You can equip the Doctor Plague costume set, which heals you when hit by monsters, or use a Laser weapon skin for a similar effect. Note that these two effects don't work at the same time — only one applies.
