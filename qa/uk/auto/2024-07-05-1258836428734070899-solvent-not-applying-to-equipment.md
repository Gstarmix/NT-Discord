# Solvent not applying to equipment

**Q:** I'm trying to use a solvent on my wand but nothing happens. Is this a known issue and how can it be fixed?

**A:** This is a known, frequently reported issue. A workaround is to move the equipment to another inventory slot or relog, then keep using the solvent repeatedly, ideally with slight pauses between attempts so the server registers the action. It may take many attempts (some players needed 100+ tries) or a full game restart before it works.
