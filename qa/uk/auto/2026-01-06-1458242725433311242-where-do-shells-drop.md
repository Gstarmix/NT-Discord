# Where do Shells drop?

**Q:** Where can shells be farmed? Forgotten Land doesn't seem to drop any even after a long farming session there.

**A:** Shells do not drop in Forgotten Land. They drop in Act 4, from regular mobs there or from Act 4 raids/raid boxes. Forgotten Land instead drops Soul Slivers (a material for Cleansing Powder used in the Laurena raid) and, from almost any mob, a small chance at PP Stones (SP1-6), which also grant contribution points per kill.
