# What pet defense and attack stats are good enough for training?

**Q:** When training a pet, what defense value is considered okay, and does attack matter at all or can it be ignored? (Context: early pets like Draco and Glacerus, later Zenas and Erenia.)

**A:** Any positive defense roll works reasonably well, a class-level 50 pet will tank fine even with a low def roll, though a defense of +8 or higher is considered good. Attack barely matters because mobs in higher acts have too much defense, so the pet ends up dealing only 1 damage regardless of its attack stat.
