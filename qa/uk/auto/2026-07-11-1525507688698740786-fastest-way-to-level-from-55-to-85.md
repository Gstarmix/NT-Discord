# Fastest way to level from 55 to 85

**Q:** What is the fastest way to level from 55 to 65, and then continue on to 85?

**A:** Questing works well for this range. If you can't find a team for LOD, you can keep doing quests until level 81, then switch to the Act 5.1 questline, which provides good gold and levels all the way to 85.
