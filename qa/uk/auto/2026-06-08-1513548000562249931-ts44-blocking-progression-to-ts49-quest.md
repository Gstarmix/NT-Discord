# TS44 blocking progression to TS49 quest

**Q:** A player was told they needed to complete a previous TimeSpace before doing TS49, even though they believed they already had. The blocking quest turned out to be TS44. What was the fix?

**A:** TS44 needs to be completed twice for it to properly register as done, which then unlocks progression toward TS49.
