# How to get Celestial Spire Keys for the Infinite Celestial Spire quest

**Q:** The quest 'To the Infinite Celestial Spire' requires clearing the Infinite Celestial Spire 5 times, but where can you obtain Celestial Spire Keys to attempt it?

**A:** Celestial Spire Keys can be obtained from the side quest / daily quest for the Paimon raid, which rewards 10 keys.
