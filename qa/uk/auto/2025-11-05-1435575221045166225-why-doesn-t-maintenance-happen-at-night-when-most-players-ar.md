# Why doesn't maintenance happen at night when most players are online?

**Q:** Since about 9 out of 10 players are European, wouldn't it make more sense to schedule maintenance during the night instead of during the day?

**A:** Maintenance is scheduled during Gameforge's (the publisher's) local daytime hours because Gameforge is a German company and their staff work day shifts, not night shifts. This is why maintenance windows fall during the day for European players rather than at night when fewer people are online.
