# How to increase a partner's stats beyond specialist fusion

**Q:** Besides upgrading a partner's SP through fusion at Malcom, is there another way to increase the partner's own stats, similar to how the pet trainer works for pets?

**A:** Partner stats can only be increased through leveling and equipment resistances/bonuses. Orange (special) effects granted by equipment also apply to partners, and even negative effects (debuffs) from equipment can proc on them.
