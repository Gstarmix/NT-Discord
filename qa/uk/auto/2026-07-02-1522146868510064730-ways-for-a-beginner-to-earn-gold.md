# Ways for a beginner to earn gold

**Q:** How can a beginner character (around level 57 swordsman) make gold early in the game?

**A:** Gold is hard to come by early on. The earliest viable method is having three level 70 characters and soloing party Time Space (TS) instances. Once you're level 80 or higher, you can join Icy Coffin (IC) for gold roughly every 2 hours, and you can also farm iceflowers in Act 4 at level 80.
