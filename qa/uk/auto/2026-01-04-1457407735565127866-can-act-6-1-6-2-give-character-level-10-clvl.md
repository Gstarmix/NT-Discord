# Can Act 6.1/6.2 give character level 10 clvl?

**Q:** Can I reach 10 clvl (class level) just by completing Act 6.1 and 6.2?

**A:** No. Act 6.1 and 6.2 only grant normal experience, not class level (clvl) experience. To gain clvl you need to grind experience on regular maps.
