# Celestial Spire / Catacombs monthly family reward eligibility

**Q:** Why might a player not receive their family's Celestial Spire or Catacombs monthly top-ranking reward?

**A:** Monthly top-ranking rewards for Celestial Spire and Catacombs are only granted to players who are still members of the family that achieved the ranking at the time rewards are distributed. If a player leaves that family before the reward is sent out, they will not receive it.
