# Taking a pet out of a pearl when it is a higher level than your character

**Q:** Can I take a pet out of its pearl if the pet's level is higher than my character's level?

**A:** Yes, you can remove the pet from the pearl, but you will not be able to equip/bring it with you if its level exceeds your character's level. There is a specific item that removes levels from a pet, which allows you to lower it enough to equip it despite the mismatch.
