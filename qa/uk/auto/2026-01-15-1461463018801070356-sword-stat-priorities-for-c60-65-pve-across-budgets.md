# Sword stat priorities for c60-65 PvE across budgets

**Q:** I want a decent c60-65 PvE sword to pair with my c60 crossbow (an Excellent Beast King's Crossbow, swordsman-only, 761-881 damage). What shell options/runes should I prioritize at low (40-70kk), mid (100-200kk), and high (300kk+) budgets — is it worth pushing for a higher + level or a 13% atk rune, or is a simple +7/8 with no/mid runes enough for grinding toward A9/A10?

**A:** A sword with S%dmg is sufficient on its own, since swordsmen already get a huge damage boost at c60 compared to other classes. Prioritize a sword with S dmg plus EXP property (ideally also SL property + EXP), because reaching higher character levels quickly matters more than maximizing the sword's combat stats at this stage. A high + level or extra runes like 13% atk aren't necessary just for leveling.
