# Can't log back into Gameforge account after being logged out of the Launcher

**Q:** I got kicked out of the Gameforge Launcher and can no longer log back in. Resetting my password via email works, but I still can't sign in afterward. This has happened twice. What could cause this and how can I fix it?

**A:** This is a known Gameforge Launcher/account issue. The usual workaround is to fully uninstall the Gameforge client (and the game) and reinstall it from scratch, rather than just reinstalling the game alone — a simple client reinstall has worked for some, while others needed the full uninstall of both client and game. If the problem persists, open a support ticket with Gameforge support.
