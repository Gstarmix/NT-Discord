# Is it viable to start NosTale fresh as a returning veteran player?

**Q:** After years away, is it possible to start NosTale completely fresh? Do you need 15 characters, and what's the current state of the game? Is there a guide for returning veteran players?

**A:** Yes, starting fresh is completely doable. A starter kit at level 56 gives full gear plus the first two SPs so you're not building up from absolute zero. You don't need 15 characters, 3 alts is considered the sweet spot: your main class plus a mage and an archer, which together cover the main buffs needed in miniland. The game now goes up to act 10 with heroic levels on top, and both servers (Dragonveil and Undercity) are active. Many items that used to be expensive are now cheap, making it a good time to return. Community sites like nostar.fr offer per-act progression guides.
