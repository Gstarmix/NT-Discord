# XP bonus for grouping in party (normal maps vs LoL vs LoD)

**Q:** Does farming in a party of 3 give more experience than solo or duo, and by how much?

**A:** It depends on the map. On normal maps, XP is split between party members and reduced compared to solo: solo gives 100%, duo 75% each, and trio 70% each (as long as every member deals at least 11% of the damage), so soloing is better for leveling a single character fast. On LoL, grouping actually increases XP: duo gives 110% and trio 115% per player. On LoD the bonus is even higher: duo gives 115% and trio 130% per player. So a full trio is optimal on LoL/LoD, while normal maps favor solo farming.
