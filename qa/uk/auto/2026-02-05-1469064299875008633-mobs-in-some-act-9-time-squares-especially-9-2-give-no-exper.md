# Mobs in some Act 9 time squares (especially 9.2) give no experience

**Q:** Why do mobs in some Act 9 time squares, especially Act 9.2, give no experience?

**A:** If this refers to Combat Experience (C Exp), the issue is that some time square maps were reused from a previous act and simply don't have that experience configuration enabled on them, rather than being a general Act 9 bug.
