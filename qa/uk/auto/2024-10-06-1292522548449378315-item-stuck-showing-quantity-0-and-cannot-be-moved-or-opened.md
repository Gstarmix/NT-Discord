# Item stuck showing quantity 0 and cannot be moved or opened

**Q:** An item (such as a Kiro box) is showing a quantity of 0 in the inventory and cannot be moved to another slot or opened. How can this be fixed?

**A:** This is a known visual bug that occurs occasionally with items. Relogging or changing map (or channel) refreshes the inventory display and resolves the issue.
