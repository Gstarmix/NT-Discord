# Timespace 18 unavailable after completing up to Timespace 17

**Q:** After completing the Act 1 quest Time Spaces up to number 17, Timespace 18 cannot be entered. Is this normal?

**A:** Yes, this is a known issue. Whenever a new set of Timespaces is released, Timespace 18 tends to get bugged and requires a special maintenance from the developers to fix it. There is no player-side workaround, players just need to wait for that maintenance.
