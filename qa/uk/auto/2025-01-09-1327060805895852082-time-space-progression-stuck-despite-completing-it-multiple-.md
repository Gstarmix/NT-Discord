# Time Space progression stuck despite completing it multiple times

**Q:** I cannot progress a Time Space (TS) even after completing it 3 times, what should I do?

**A:** Check whether there are other time capsules or instances available for the same act. You are most likely repeating the Time Space you have already completed instead of moving on to the next required one.
