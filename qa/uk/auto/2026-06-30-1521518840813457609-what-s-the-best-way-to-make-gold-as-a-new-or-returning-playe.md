# What's the best way to make gold as a new or returning player?

**Q:** What's the best money-making approach, especially for a returning player: should you rush to clvl 60 and spam solo raids like the elemental raids, and which raids are mainly farmed solo?

**A:** Early game, making money without at least a few alts (for things like group timespaces) is hard. Late game, the best income comes from running solo raids, such as the elemental raids, with multiple alts. It's still possible to make money without that setup by grinding farming spots, but it's not the optimal method.
