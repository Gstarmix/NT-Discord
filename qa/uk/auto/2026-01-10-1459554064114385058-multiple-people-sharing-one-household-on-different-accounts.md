# Multiple people sharing one household on different accounts

**Q:** Is it allowed for two different people to play on separate in-game accounts under the same Gameforge account/household, and does this need to be reported (e.g. same IP)?

**A:** Two people playing from the same household on separate accounts is generally fine; playing on the same account would count as account sharing instead. However, if either account is found doing something against the rules (cheating, botting, etc.), both accounts sharing the household/IP risk being banned together.
