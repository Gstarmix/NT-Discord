# SP +9 100pp vs SP +15 100pp strength difference

**Q:** Is there a big strength difference between an SP (specialist card) at +9 with 100 perfection points versus one at +15 with 100 perfection points?

**A:** SP perfection is distributed randomly across stats, so no one can give an exact comparison without knowing how the perfection points are actually spread. In general, perfection points (pp) matter more for strength than the upgrade level (+) of the SP. A damage calculator is available on NosAPKI for testing specific combinations.
