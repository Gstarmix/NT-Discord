# Cost to upgrade a partner special card (psp) to +39

**Q:** How expensive is it to upgrade a psp (partner special card) to +39, and which psp should be used given that buying pre-upgraded ones on the black market/Nosbazaar is expensive?

**A:** During a 50% psp fusion event, reaching +39 costs roughly 5kk gold plus around 16 psp cards and 20 small pp (fusion powders). Costs would be higher outside such a fusion event.
