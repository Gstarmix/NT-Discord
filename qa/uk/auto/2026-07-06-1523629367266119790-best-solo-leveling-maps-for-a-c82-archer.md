# Best solo leveling maps for a c82 archer

**Q:** Are there maps where a c82 archer can level efficiently solo, or is Land of Death (LoD) the only option at this level?

**A:** LoD is not the only option, solo grinding works too. Power Plant is a solid solo spot at c82: Demon Hunter (SP7a) is great for mobbing since it holds packs and AoEs them down without needing a pet to provoke, while Wild Keeper (SP4a) also works well as an all round PvE and leveling SP. LoD gives slightly better XP, but classic A9 maps drop the crafting materials for level 90 gear, so it's an XP versus drops tradeoff. Soloing without a double map buff will run a notch slower than commonly quoted duo rates. The nostar.fr website lists A9 leveling spots per SP and has an XP simulator preset for Power Plant where you can plug in your own gear to see your actual rate.
