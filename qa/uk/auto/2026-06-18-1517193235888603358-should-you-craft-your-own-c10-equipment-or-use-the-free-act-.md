# Should you craft your own C10 equipment or use the free Act 6.1 set

**Q:** Should a player craft their own C10 equipment, or is it fine to use the free equipment from Act 6.1? Also, does completing Act 6.1 give a free SP upgrade token?

**A:** Whether to craft your own C10 gear depends on how good your current SP already is; if it's not great yet, using the free Act 6.1 equipment is not a bad option. Separately, completing Act 6.1 does reward a free SP upgrade token, usable for SP levels 5 to 8 (or 1 to 4 for Mecha/MA type SP).
