# Is Warrior + Holy Mage the best duo for the whole leveling process?

**Q:** Is the Warrior and Holy Mage combo the best duo for leveling from start to end?

**A:** The swordsman + mage pairing is correct for the whole run, but the specific SP cards used change over time. Warrior is strong up to C30, after which it falls behind on damage and should be swapped for Berserker, which carries the swordsman to C90. Holy Mage is the free starting SP and performs well for a long stretch, then should be swapped for Archmage (still light element) for much higher damage. So Warrior + Holy Mage is the right starting combo, but not meant to be kept forever; the underlying setup (swordsman tanking aggro, mage AoEing packs) stays the same while the SP cards get upgraded.
