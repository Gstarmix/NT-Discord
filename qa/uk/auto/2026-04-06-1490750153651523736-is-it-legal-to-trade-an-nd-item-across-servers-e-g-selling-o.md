# Is it legal to trade an ND item across servers (e.g. selling on DragonVale, delivering on NosFire)?

**Q:** Is it allowed to change an ND from the DragonVale server to the NosFire server, for example selling an ND on DragonVale while the buyer pays and wants delivery on NosFire?

**A:** Cross-server ND trades like this are legal. However, players should take safety precautions when doing this kind of deal, such as taking screenshots of the agreement or making a live recording (e.g. via Discord or a similar tool) as proof in case of a dispute.
