# How many dishes need to be cooked to unlock the next specialist recipe

**Q:** To unlock a specialist's next cooking recipes, is it necessary to cook 5 dishes of each already unlocked recipe, or just 1 of each?

**A:** It is the amount the specialist requests, shown as a progress count (for example 0/5 means 5 dishes need to be cooked). All already unlocked recipes need to reach their required count in order to unlock further recipes.
