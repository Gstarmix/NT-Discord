# Do equipment box drop rates differ by class?

**Q:** In equipment boxes (e.g. Act 8 boxes, Paimon boxes), do some classes' equipment have a lower drop chance than others? After around 300 boxes only 4 pieces of equipment dropped (3 Archer, 1 Mage), and Paimon boxes almost always give swords.

**A:** Yes, drop rates are not equal between classes: Martial Artist (MA) equipment has a noticeably higher chance to drop than the equipment of the other three classes combined. Some players find this frustrating since they end up with far more MA gear than gear for their own class.
