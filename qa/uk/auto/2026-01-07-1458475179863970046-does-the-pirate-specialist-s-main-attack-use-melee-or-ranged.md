# Does the Pirate specialist's main attack use melee or ranged stats?

**Q:** The Pirate specialist card doesn't consume ammo but still requires a ranged weapon to be equipped. Does its main attack scale off melee or ranged weapon stats?

**A:** Although the Pirate specialist is an archer-class card requiring a ranged weapon equipped, its main attack actually scales off dagger (melee) stats rather than ranged weapon stats.
