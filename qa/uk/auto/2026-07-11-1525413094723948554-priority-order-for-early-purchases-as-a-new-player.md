# Priority order for early purchases as a new player

**Q:** What is the recommended order of priority for purchases when starting the game?

**A:** Before level 55, buy nothing; the free kit at level 56 already provides level 50 gear, four fairies (one per element), and your first two SPs. If spending real money, the NosMall starter pack (330 ND) is by far the best value purchase and everything else can wait. Never buy ND outside a happy hour: Gameforge runs roughly biweekly +30% bonus events (usually Saturdays), and the 49.99 EUR pack (normally about 1180 ND, about 1520 ND during happy hour) is the sweet spot. After that, gold should go into SPs (via bazaar purchase or SP quests), aiming for one light and one dark SP per class: light SPs (Crusader for swordsman, Archmage for mage) clear the dark mobs of LOD, Hell's Ruins 2 and the Contaminated Path, while dark SPs (Berserker for swordsman, Seer for mage) handle the mostly light mobs of LOL. Save soulstones from the Act 4, 6 and 7 main quests, since each instantly raises an SP to level 41+9. Gear and SL should be prioritized only from C10 onward, since SL is very hard to find below that. Pet, partner, and costume can be picked up along the way as gold allows, and some gold should always be kept for potions and XP buffs as a running cost.
