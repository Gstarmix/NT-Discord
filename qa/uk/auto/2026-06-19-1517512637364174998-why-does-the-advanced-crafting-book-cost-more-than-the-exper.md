# Why does the Advanced crafting book cost more than the Expert book

**Q:** Why is the Advanced crafting book better than the Expert one, and why does it cost significantly more?

**A:** The Advanced book builds on the Expert book: you need the first (Expert) book before you can use the second (Advanced) one, which is part of why it is priced higher.
