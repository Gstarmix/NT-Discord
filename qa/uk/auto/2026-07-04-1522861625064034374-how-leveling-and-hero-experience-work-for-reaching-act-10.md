# How leveling and hero experience work for reaching Act 10

**Q:** Has anything changed about experience gain recently? How long does it take to level from 80 to 90, specifically in terms of hero experience needed to attempt Act 10?

**A:** Normal character level is no longer the main progression metric, hero level is what matters now. Hero experience becomes available starting at normal level 85. Story quests take a character to level 81, at which point the old level 90 weapons and armors (rescaled to level 81) can be equipped. The current endgame goal is to farm hero experience rather than push normal level further.
