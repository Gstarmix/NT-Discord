# Is it safe to leave alt characters in First Safe spot during a raid?

**Q:** When running a raid (e.g. C90) with a strong main character, can I just leave the alt characters standing in the first safe spot?

**A:** You can, but be careful: the devil enemy can still spawn on top of alts even in a safe spot. For that reason it is safer to only bring a couple of alts that can actually fit into the safe spots rather than a full group.
