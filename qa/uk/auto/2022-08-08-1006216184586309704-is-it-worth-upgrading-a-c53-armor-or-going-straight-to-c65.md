**Q:** I bought a C53 armor to replace my C28 armor but I can't get it upgraded (+) due to lack of upgrade-fortune events, and I'm about to reach character level 59-65. Should I keep waiting to upgrade the C53 armor, or should I skip it and go straight for a C65 armor?
