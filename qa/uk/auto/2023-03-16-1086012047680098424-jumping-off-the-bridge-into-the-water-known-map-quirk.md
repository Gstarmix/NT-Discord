# Jumping off the bridge into the water (known map quirk)

**Q:** In the southern area, if you jump off the bridge into the water, does it still work, and was it ever fixed?

**A:** This was never fixed. Depending on the spot, jumping off the bridge into the water makes the character either float or sink instead of behaving like normal ground. The same thing also happens on the Berios map. For frozen lifeforms the effect is reported to work the opposite way.
