# Daily time limit for Land of Life

**Q:** Is there a daily time limit for Land of Life, such as 2 hours per day?

**A:** Land of Life has a total daily limit of 130 minutes, which resets at midnight. The recommended strategy is to enter a few minutes before Asgobas spawns and stay for a full hour, since the Asgobas period gives double experience, then repeat later (for example 21:00 to 22:00 and 23:00 to 00:00). The map opens every 2 hours on the hour, with Asgobas spawning 1 hour after each opening.
