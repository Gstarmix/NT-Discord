# How does the 'final damage from critical hits' bonus interact with crit chance and total damage?

**Q:** If a stat gives '+10% final damage from critical hits', does it apply as 10% of the crit damage portion only, or to the whole damage output? For example, with a base hit of 5k and 300% crit (making it 15k), is the extra 10% applied to the full 15k or only to the crit bonus part, and does this bonus only apply when the hit actually crits?

**A:** The final damage bonus applies to the entire damage dealt, both physical and elemental parts combined, not only to the critical hit portion (which by itself is only physical). This bonus is only granted when the attack actually lands as a critical hit; on a non critical hit it does not apply.
