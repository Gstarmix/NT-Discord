# Character bugged after entering Act 4 (family level reset, titles locked, pets/inventory missing)

**Q:** After entering Act 4, my family level dropped to 1, all titles became locked (except an eternal one), active events stopped showing, my inventory expansion disappeared, and my pets vanished — while everything works normally outside Act 4. Re-entering ML (main lands) restores pets and family level, but after relogging I have to enter ML again to fix it, and inventory/title issues persist longer. This happened to me before too, and back then it took support and staff about 6 weeks to resolve itself with no known fix. Is there a fix?

**A:** This is a known but poorly understood issue: staff report very few similar cases in the past, found nothing wrong with the affected accounts/characters, and in all prior cases it eventually resolved itself with no fix applied. Repairing the client and re-entering/leaving Act 4 can temporarily restore titles, family level and pets, but the effect may not persist across channel switches, and the inventory display issue tends to fix itself after a few hours while titles/familiars can take longer. As a general troubleshooting step, closing other running programs or trying a different PC has been suggested, though it's not confirmed to fix the root cause.
