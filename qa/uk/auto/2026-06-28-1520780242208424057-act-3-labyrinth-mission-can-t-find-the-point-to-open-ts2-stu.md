# Act 3 labyrinth mission: can't find the point to open TS2 (stuck on TS3 out of map)

**Q:** In the Act 3 labyrinth mission, the point needed to open timespace 2 can't be found, and timespace 3 appears to be located outside the map. Is there a way to fix this bug?

**A:** This issue is related to the game client's keyboard or system language setting. Changing your Windows system language or keyboard language to English or German and restarting the game fixes the bug.
