# How to obtain the Macavity Halloween raid seal

**Q:** During the 2024 Halloween event, how can you get the seal needed for the Macavity raid?

**A:** The seal for the Macavity raid can only be obtained by completing the Jack O'Lantern raid and getting it as loot; completing Foxy does not grant it. Because of this, there is a shortage of players willing to join Jack O'Lantern runs.
