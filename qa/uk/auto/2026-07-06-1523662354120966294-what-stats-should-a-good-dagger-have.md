# What stats should a good dagger have?

**Q:** What stats make a dagger worth buying for dealing damage?

**A:** For a dagger meant to be your main source of damage, prioritize crit chance, crit damage, or both, along with a percentage damage bonus and a stone level (SL) damage property. Enhanced damage is also a nice stat to have. An example of a well-rolled dagger is a Legendary Sealed Heavenly Dagger +7 for Archers, with a 20% chance of 340% critical, +7% critical chance, +124 enhanced damage, +17 SL damage stat, and +15% increased damage.
