# Budget mage setup for cexp (pet, partner, outfit)

**Q:** What is a good budget pet, partner (psp) and outfit setup for a mage to start doing cexp, without spending more than 70kk?

**A:** For a cheap setup, Otter is the recommended pet (Panda is the next best option but costs around 50kk more). For partner, Ragnar is suggested (a skin can be added later, it is not a priority at first), paired with Altus as the psp. Dino is a good budget outfit choice. Forga is also a solid alternative for cexp if it is priced similarly to the other options.
