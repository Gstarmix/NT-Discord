# SP point distribution and damage tips for a mage stuck in Act 6

**Q:** As a mage with decent resistance but very low damage output, stuck unable to farm Act 6, what is the recommended gameplan to fix this, particularly around SP point distribution and other damage boosters?

**A:** For a mage, SP points should go full into element with the rest into attack, and none into defense or HP; a minimum ratio of around 30+ attack to 80 element is needed to be playable. To boost damage further, stack buff alts, use a fairy booster, attack potions, and a devil or sun tarot card if available.
