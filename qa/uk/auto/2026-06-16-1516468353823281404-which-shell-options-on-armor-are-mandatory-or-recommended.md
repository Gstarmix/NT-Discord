# Which shell options on armor are mandatory or recommended?

**Q:** What shell (rune) options on armor are considered mandatory or good to have?

**A:** All three defense shells and antistun/anticrit chance are considered core picks. The S-effect option is a matter of preference: S-defense is favored for PvP and higher acts in PvE, while S-effect is preferred in most other PvE content where S-defense isn't as necessary. S-resistance is also useful at lower levels if your resistance is below 100%.
