# How to get clvl equipment

**Q:** How do you obtain clvl equipment, and what should you look for as an archer, given that these items reportedly come with shells built in from the start?

**A:** Clvl equipment is obtained by finishing the Act 6 or Act 7 story quests, and it's good gear to use at the start. Which shell options are worthwhile depends on where you currently are in progression.
