# Quest 6.2 level requirement bug on Oasis

**Q:** On the Oasis server, quest 6.2 cannot be taken at level 92, even though the quest's intended minimum level is supposed to be 88+. What is going on?

**A:** There was an attempt by the team to lower quest 6.2's required level to 88, but the change failed to apply, so the requirement was never actually updated. The quest level requirement remains as it has always been (level 95), which is why lower level characters cannot take it.
