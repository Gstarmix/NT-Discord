# Do items kept in the NosFire storage become account-limited, or can they still be traded to other accounts?

**Q:** If items are brought into the NosFire server storage using an account, do they become limited to that account, or can they still be traded to other accounts afterward?

**A:** Items keep whatever trade status they already had: if an item was already limited (untradeable), it remains limited after being placed in the NosFire storage. If it was not limited, it can still be traded normally to other accounts.
