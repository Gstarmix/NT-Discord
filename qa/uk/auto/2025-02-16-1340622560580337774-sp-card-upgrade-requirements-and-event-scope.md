# SP card upgrade requirements and event scope

**Q:** When trying to upgrade an SP card from +1 to +2 during an SP upgrade event, the upgrade failed with only a generic 'upgrade failed' message and consumed materials. Is the +2 upgrade actually blocked below a certain job level (e.g. job 22), and is there an SP upgrade event that should improve success odds?

**A:** The +2 upgrade level has no special job level requirement tied to job 21 or 22; SP cards can be upgraded up to +5 as soon as job level 41 is reached. The failed attempts were simply bad luck, since upgrades always carry a chance of failure and consume materials regardless of outcome. Also, there is no SP upgrade event for regular SP cards, only for partner SP.
