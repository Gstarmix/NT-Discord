# Best ways to level and make gold as a fresh/returning player

**Q:** As a returning player starting a new character, what are the best ways to level up and earn gold in the early game?

**A:** In the early game, focus on farming rainbow pearls and ice flowers, and running Instant Battle for experience and gold. Once your character level is high enough, switch to raid marathons for much faster gold income (one player reportedly makes around 20kk per day, though this is considered a low rate for the time invested).
