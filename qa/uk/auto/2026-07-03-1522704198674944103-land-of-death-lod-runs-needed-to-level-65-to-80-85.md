# Land of Death (LoD) runs needed to level 65 to 80/85

**Q:** How many Land of Death (Rebs) runs are needed to level from 65 to 80, and then to 85? Roughly how many full LoD clears would that take?

**A:** Roughly 3 to 4 hours of Land of Death farming to reach level 80, and about another 3 hours on top of that to reach level 85. The exact number of runs depends heavily on how efficiently the party clears the dungeon.
