# History of SP (skill) rebalances for mage-type classes

**Q:** Is there a list of SP rebalances showing what changed for classes like Archmage, Blue Mage, Dark Gunner, Volcano, Time Lord (TL) and Seer? For example, Archmage cooldowns were lowered - did all mage SPs get rebalanced?

**A:** Only Archmage's meteors, ultimate, and mana shield were changed for that class. Going further back (since the Volcano SP era): Blue Mage's ice block gained a mana shield and movement speed and now blocks debuffs up to level 4 (previously it only blocked damage), but its duration was reduced from 4 to 3 seconds and its cooldown no longer decreases, making it an overall slight nerf/rebalance. Dark Gunner received a major nerf: direct debuff removal was changed into a debuff-removing effect, crit damage increase was removed, but some cooldowns (dead hand, possession, requiem) were buffed. Volcano mostly had debuff changes rather than skill changes, with reduced cooldowns on its sword skill and ultimate. Time Lord got reduced cooldowns, more AoE on shark, a buffed large waterfall debuff (now level 5, with some trade-off), and all chain skills across every SP (including Time Lord's thunder chains) were generally reduced to 0.3s cooldown. Seer had a major rework: its first skill now deals damage, illusion no longer fears the opponent, its special debuff works properly, mana shield was reduced in value but increased in duration, Time Loss and Blade chains were nerfed, Sink no longer absorbs damage as HP but instead heals for a portion of damage taken (capped at 2 procs per cast), and several cooldowns were lowered.
