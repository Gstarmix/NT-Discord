# Joining Act 10 maras as c90 without top-tier gear

**Q:** For Act 10 maras, is it realistically possible to join as a combat level 90 character with good but not top-tier gear (for example strong +8/9 with 13% attack but not perfect +21/21 +13), or does it require playing as a buff/support with a family raid?

**A:** It is possible by joining in a support role. Support SPs have lower gear requirements to be accepted into the raid, but in exchange you are expected to reliably land your debuffs and keep the team buffed.
