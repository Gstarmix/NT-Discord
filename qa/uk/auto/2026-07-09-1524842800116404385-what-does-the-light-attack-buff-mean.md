# What does the 'light attack' buff mean?

**Q:** What does the 'light attack' buff/stat actually refer to (as opposed to light versus heavy attack)?

**A:** It refers to elemental damage, specifically damage dealt using the light element, not a distinction between 'light' and 'heavy' physical attacks.
