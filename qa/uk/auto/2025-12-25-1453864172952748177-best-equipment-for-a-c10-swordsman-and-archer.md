# Best equipment for a c10 swordsman and archer

**Q:** At character level 10, what equipment should a swordsman and an archer aim for?

**A:** The equipment from the Act 6 quest ("hero eq") is generally considered the best available at c10 and is free, so there is no need to buy anything at this stage if you plan to keep leveling. It's fine to use until around c25/c28, when the Act 7 quest grants an upgrade. When shopping for gear in general, look for these core effects: on the damage weapon, S-grade damage, C-A tier enhanced damage, and C tier crit chance/damage, plus B-A tier attack and an elemental stat; on the support weapon, S-grade all stats plus B-A tier attack and an elemental stat; on armor, any defense increase, S-grade defense or S-grade resistance to all effects (or S-grade general resistance if all-res is lacking), with light and water resistance being useful later around c30. Attack stat only needs to appear on one of the two weapons since it doesn't stack between the damage and support weapon.
