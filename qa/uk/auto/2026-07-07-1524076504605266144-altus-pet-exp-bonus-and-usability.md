# Altus pet exp bonus and usability

**Q:** Does the Altus pet only give a cooldown reduction buff, or does it also give cexp? How does it compare for an Altus + Dino + Otter setup?

**A:** Altus does provide both exp and cexp bonuses, though somewhat lower than Forga or Perti. In practice it is annoying to use because its large 3D model tends to end up in awkward positions on screen, causing frequent misclicks. Pairing Altus with Dino and Otter is still considered a fine setup overall.
