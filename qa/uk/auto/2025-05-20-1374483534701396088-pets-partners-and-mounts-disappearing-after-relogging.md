# Pets, partners and mounts disappearing after relogging

**Q:** After keeping a pet and partner active through a full League of Larka run, they disappeared from the character (not shown in character info) even without being sent away. Relogging, restarting the client, and restarting the PC did not fix it. A mount (Ragnar) also disappeared under similar circumstances. Is this a known issue and how can it be fixed?

**A:** This is a known bug where pets, partners, and mounts can randomly vanish and not reappear through relogging or client/PC restarts. The only known remedy is submitting a support ticket — support staff can restore the missing pets/partners, but confirmed it takes a significant amount of time (in one case, restoration was still pending 10+ days after the ticket was filed).
