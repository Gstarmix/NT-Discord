# Ulti Arma raid becomes unbeatable when boss is stunned near HP thresholds

**Q:** If the new Ulti Arma boss is stunned long enough, it can trigger multiple chest spawn mechanics at once, making the raid unbeatable. Is this a known issue?

**A:** Yes, this is a known issue related to raid mechanics that trigger at specific HP percentage thresholds; stunning the boss near those thresholds can cause the trigger to fire multiple times. A fix was being considered but was not yet available at the time.
