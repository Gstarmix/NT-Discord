# Act 9 patch nerfed attack/defense potions

**Q:** Why were attack and defense potions reduced in power by about 50% in the Act 9 release patch, and shouldn't their duration have been increased instead?

**A:** The Act 9 release patch cut the effectiveness of attack/defense potions by roughly half without extending their duration. The community broadly considers this nerf unjustified, since these potions were a free power boost with no clear downside to balance; some speculate it's meant to push players toward paid attack pets or Nosmall alternatives. The change is especially felt in harder content such as CLOD 30+.
