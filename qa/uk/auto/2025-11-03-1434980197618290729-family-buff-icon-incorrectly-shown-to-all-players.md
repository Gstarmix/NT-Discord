# Family buff icon incorrectly shown to all players

**Q:** A family skill buff is only supposed to apply to members of the family that used it, but the buff icon is currently displayed to all players - is this intended?

**A:** This is a known display bug: the buff effect is only actually granted to members of the family that activated the skill, but the buff icon is currently shown as active to every player, which is incorrect.
