# Automatic (loop) production randomly stops working

**Q:** Automatic/loop production (e.g. crafting half moons) stops working and doesn't respond to relogging or client repair. Is this a known bug?

**A:** This is a known intermittent bug affecting loop-based production in general, not just half moons. It randomly stops functioning and then starts working again with no identified trigger; relogging and repairing the client do not reliably fix it. No permanent workaround is known besides waiting it out.
