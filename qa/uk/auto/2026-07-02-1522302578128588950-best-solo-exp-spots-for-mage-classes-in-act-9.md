# Best solo exp spots for mage classes in act 9

**Q:** What's the best way to farm exp solo as a mage in act 9, and which spots have easier (melee vs ranged) mobs to lure?

**A:** The best spot depends on your SP. Seer can farm the Land of Oblivion (LoL) solo all the way to level 90. Gravity mages and Holy Mage/Archmage do better on field maps: Supply Route and Lower Lab for Gravity, and Polluted Area and Power Plant for Holy Mage/Archmage. Southern Basement is also recommended, especially for Archmage, since all mobs there are melee, making luring easier; Labs and Power Plants mix melee and ranged mobs. Gravity is generally the most comfortable for solo play since it multi-targets even on auto-attack and doesn't need a pet to provoke, while Seer benefits more from a grouping skill or pet and shines more in a group. LoL gives slightly better xp than classic maps, but classic maps drop better level 90 crafting materials, and playing solo without a duo yields noticeably lower rates than commonly quoted duo numbers.
