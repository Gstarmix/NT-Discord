# Login blocked after creating too many accounts

**Q:** I can't log in on my account and suspect a shadow ban, since I can still log into other GameForge accounts, even though I didn't create extra accounts.

**A:** This login block is typically triggered by creating too many accounts; the fix is to wait about 24 hours and try again. Restarting your PC and router before retrying can also help in some cases.
