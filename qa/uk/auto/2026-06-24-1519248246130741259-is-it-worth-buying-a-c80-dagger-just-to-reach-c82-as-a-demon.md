# Is it worth buying a c80 dagger just to reach c82 as a Demon Hunter

**Q:** As a Demon Hunter currently using a c65 weapon set (already owning the c82 set) and lacking damage to farm the power plant efficiently, is it worth buying a c80 dagger just to reach character level 82 and switch weapons again?

**A:** It is not worth buying an intermediate c80 weapon just to gain two character levels. Instead, rely on daily quests and grind experience gradually with your current gear until you naturally reach the level needed for your c82 set.
