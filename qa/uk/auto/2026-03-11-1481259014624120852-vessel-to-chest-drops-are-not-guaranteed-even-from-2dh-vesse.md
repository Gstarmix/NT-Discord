# Vessel-to-chest drops are not guaranteed even from '2DH' vessels

**Q:** After opening 30 vessels with 0 chests, even from '2DH' (2 Dungeon Heroes) vessels which are supposedly 100% chance for loot, is the drop bugged?

**A:** This is not a bug. Even '2DH' vessels do not actually have a guaranteed 100% chance to yield a chest, contrary to popular belief. A run of vessels producing no chests can happen without anything being broken.
