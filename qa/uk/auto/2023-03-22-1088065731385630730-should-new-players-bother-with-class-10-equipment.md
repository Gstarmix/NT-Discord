# Should new players bother with class 10 equipment

**Q:** Is it worth using mediocre class 10 equipment, or should new players skip it, for example when starting on a fresh server with no money or premium currency?

**A:** Class 10 equipment can be skipped without major issues, even by players starting with no gold or premium currency, as one player did successfully with a mage. The common advice given to new players, however, is to get alt characters as soon as possible and start preparing them for leveling once they reach level 85, rather than investing in class 10 gear.
