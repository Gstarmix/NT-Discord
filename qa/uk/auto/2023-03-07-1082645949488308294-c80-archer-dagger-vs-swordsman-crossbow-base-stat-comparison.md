# C80 archer dagger vs swordsman crossbow: base stat comparison

**Q:** What is the actual stat difference between the archer's champion level 80 weapon (Dragon Bone Dagger) and the swordsman's champion level 80 weapon (Dragon Crystal Crossbow)?

**A:** The Dragon Bone Dagger (Archer, champion level 80, rarity 0) has damage 679 to 757, hit rate 714, 25% chance of 437 critical, +6% damage proc at 80% chance, +20 to all elements, -25 enemy elemental resistance, and +150 hit rate on all attacks. The Dragon Crystal Crossbow (Swordsman, champion level 80, rarity 0) has damage 843 to 988, hit rate 628, 23% chance of 265 critical, +280 to all attacks, +15% damage proc at 50% chance, +30 to all elements, and -27 enemy elemental resistance. In short, the swordsman weapon hits noticeably harder at base but has lower hit rate and crit than the archer weapon. Players also pointed out that a rarity 8 archer dagger has nearly the same base damage as a rarity 7 swordsman C80 weapon, and that archers partly compensate through a roughly 55% soft damage chance.
