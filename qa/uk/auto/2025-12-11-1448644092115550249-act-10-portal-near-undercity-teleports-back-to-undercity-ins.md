# Act 10 portal near Undercity teleports back to Undercity instead of the intended map

**Q:** In Act 10, after reaching the map two before the Undercity (the underground battlefield), a portal that should lead further teleports the player back to Undercity instead. Is this a known bug or intended behavior tied to quest progress?

**A:** This is a known, long-standing bug that occurs when a player has not completed the quest required to unlock that map/city, combined with duplicated map instances (as seen previously during EXP events in ASPT). Without the correct quest completed, the portal loops the player back to Undercity instead of advancing. Other players have confirmed encountering the same loop on different servers (e.g. NF1). The workaround is to make sure the required Act 10 quest is completed before using that portal.
