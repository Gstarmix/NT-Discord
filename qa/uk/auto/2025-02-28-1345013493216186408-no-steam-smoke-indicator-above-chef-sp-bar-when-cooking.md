# No steam/smoke indicator above chef SP bar when cooking

**Q:** The smoke indicator that normally appears above the chef SP bar while cooking is missing, so there is no way to tell when to stop cooking. Restarting the game, which used to fix this in the past, no longer helps.

**A:** This is a known display bug affecting the cooking smoke indicator. A workaround that has worked for some players is to cook in your miniland without a fairy and without wearing armor.
