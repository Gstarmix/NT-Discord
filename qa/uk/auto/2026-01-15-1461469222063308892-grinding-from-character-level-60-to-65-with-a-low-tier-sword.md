# Grinding from character level 60 to 65 with a low-tier sword

**Q:** With a c60 crossbow, is a c25 sword enough to grind up to character level 65, or should I upgrade sooner? Also, is the Bees map a solo or a duo/trio farming spot?

**A:** A c25 sword paired with a c60 crossbow is enough to reach c65 without needing an upgrade. Bees are very weak and remain a viable, easy farming spot up to about c62; beyond that, leveling gets slower outside of Lord of Larvae (LoL) runs, which give strong exp (over 40% per run at c62-65 with a decent group).
