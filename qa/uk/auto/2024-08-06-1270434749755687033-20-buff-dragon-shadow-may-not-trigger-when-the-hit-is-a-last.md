# +20 buff (Dragon Shadow) may not trigger when the hit is a last hit

**Q:** Is it intended that the +20 buff fails to trigger when you land the killing blow on an enemy, similar to the old Mecha Wings bug that was eventually fixed?

**A:** As of this report, this is treated as a known issue rather than intended design: the buff can fail to trigger when the enemy dies from the triggering hit, similar to the previous Mecha Wings bug. A staff member noted that even though the extra damage itself is not needed once the enemy is dead, the buff should still be granted, and said they would check the behavior in lures and discuss with the game design team whether it should be changed. No fix was confirmed at the time.
