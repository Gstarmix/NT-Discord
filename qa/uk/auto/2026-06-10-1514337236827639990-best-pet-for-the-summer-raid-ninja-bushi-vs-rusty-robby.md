# Best pet for the summer raid: Ninja Bushi vs Rusty Robby

**Q:** What is the best pet to use in the summer raid, Ninja Bushi or Rusty Robby?

**A:** Both Ninja Bushi and Rusty Robby are good choices for the summer raid. Rusty Robby works well for the three mobbing/AoE areas, while Ninja Bushi significantly speeds up killing the miniboss and boss. Note that some raid leaders blacklist Ninja Bushi because careless players spam the spacebar and let it auto-target mobs, interfering with manual mob selection, so it is worth checking with your raid leader before using it.
