# When does clvl content start being profitable?

**Q:** At what point does clvl (class level) content start making money, and how are you supposed to get there without money to begin with?

**A:** Reliable income generally starts around class level 99 (with proper equipment and alts). Before that, players either grind extensively or spend real money to catch up. Act 4 farming for Rainbow Pearls with Ice Flower or a party doing time spaces is considered a decent early money-making method.
