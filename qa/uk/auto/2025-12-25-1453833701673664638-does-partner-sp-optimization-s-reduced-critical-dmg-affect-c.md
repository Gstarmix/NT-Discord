# Does Partner SP Optimization's reduced critical DMG affect crit chance or crit damage?

**Q:** Does the "reduced critical DMG" stat on Partner SP Optimization reduce critical hit chance or final critical damage?

**A:** It works like the regular critical damage reduction found on armor or jewelry, i.e. it reduces the final critical damage taken, not the critical hit chance.
