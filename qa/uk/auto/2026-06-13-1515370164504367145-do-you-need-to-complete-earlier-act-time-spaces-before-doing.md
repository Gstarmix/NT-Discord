# Do you need to complete earlier Act Time Spaces before doing a later one?

**Q:** If a character level 80 wants to enter the Act 9 Time Space 1 (TS1), do they need to have completed the previous Time Spaces starting from Act 1?

**A:** Yes, if the specific character attempting the Time Space has not completed the previous Time Spaces, it must complete all of them in order from Act 1 through Act 8 before being able to do the Act 9 TS.
