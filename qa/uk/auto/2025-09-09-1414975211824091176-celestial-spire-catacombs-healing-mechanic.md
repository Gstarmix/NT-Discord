# Celestial Spire Catacombs healing mechanic

**Q:** How does healing work inside the Celestial Spire Catacombs floors?

**A:** Players cannot use healing potions inside the Catacombs; healing instead relies on a totem. Player feedback noted that, as released, the totem is quite fragile and dies too easily given that it's the only healing source.
