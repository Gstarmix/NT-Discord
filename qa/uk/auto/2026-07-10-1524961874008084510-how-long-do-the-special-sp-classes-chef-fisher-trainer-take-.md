# How long do the special SP classes (Chef, Fisher, Trainer) take to finish?

**Q:** How long does it take to complete the special SP quests (Chef, Fisher, Trainer) up to receiving the passive book, given that they are semi-AFK but require a lot of time overall?

**A:** Trainer is best done as a side activity: it is not very expensive and lets you train your pets while doing other content. Chef has a high price tag for ingredients but can be finished quickly if you buy them. Fisher is the worst of the three since it requires active play and can take weeks to complete.
