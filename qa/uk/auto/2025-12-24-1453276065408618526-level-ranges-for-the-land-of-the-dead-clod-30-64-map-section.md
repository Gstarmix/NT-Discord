# Level ranges for the Land of the Dead (Clod) 30-64 map sections

**Q:** At what character level should players start the top-right and middle sections of the Land of the Dead map (the one covering levels 30-64)?

**A:** The c30-c64 Clod map is roughly divided into four sections by level: the entrance area suits c30-c35, the bottom-left side suits c35-c40, the top-left area suits c40-c49, and the top-right/remaining area suits players up to about c60.
