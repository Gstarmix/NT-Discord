# Gameforge launcher freezes/stuck on account selection screen

**Q:** The Gameforge launcher gets stuck (freezes) on the account/game selection screen and won't proceed. How can this be fixed?

**A:** This is a known launcher issue reported by multiple players. A workaround is to kill the gfclient.exe process via the process/task manager and relaunch the client.
