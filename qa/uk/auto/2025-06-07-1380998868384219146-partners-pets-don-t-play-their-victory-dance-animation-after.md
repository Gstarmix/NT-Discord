# Partners/pets don't play their victory dance animation after raids (mostly Act 8-9)

**Q:** Why don't partners and pets (like Marie or Panda) play their dance animation at the end of raids anymore? Instead they just show a static idle pose. This seems to happen especially in Act 8-9 raids, while it still works in Act 1-6.

**A:** This is a known long-standing issue: pets/partners try to start their dance animation every frame, which effectively locks them in a static idle pose instead of animating (possibly a side effect of a change meant to stop pets from picking up loot, though this isn't confirmed). A workaround is to press S right before moving after the boss is defeated — the nosmate will then play the dance animation until you force it to move again, at which point it freezes back into the idle pose.
