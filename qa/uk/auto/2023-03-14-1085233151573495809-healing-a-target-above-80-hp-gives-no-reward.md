# Healing a target above 80% HP gives no reward

**Q:** Why do the new Holy specialist changes give no benefit for healing a target that is already above 80% HP?

**A:** Under the new Holy changes, healing points/benefits are only gained when healing a target below 80% HP, so healing an already near-full-HP target is effectively wasted. This design pushes healer players to stop investing in HP-boosting stats and instead go full elemental attack and attack power, since raw increased healing power becomes less valuable when most heals land on already-healthy targets.
