# Event seed boss spawn locations in Act 5 and Act 6

**Q:** Where do the bosses for the event seeds spawn in Act 5 and Act 6? Do they spawn randomly or are there several fixed spawns?

**A:** In Act 5 the event seed bosses spawn on the volcano maps, while in Act 6 they can spawn on any map. Only one boss spawn is active per map at a given time.
