# Must have equipment while leveling, and whether C45 gear can be skipped

**Q:** What set of equipment is a must have while leveling a character, and can the C45 gear be skipped?

**A:** You can keep using your C25 weapon while leveling up through C60/C65. The C45 rare 1+0 weapon is still worth getting temporarily for its heal buff: swap to the C45 weapon, land a hit to trigger the heal buff, then swap back to the C25 weapon to continue using it.
