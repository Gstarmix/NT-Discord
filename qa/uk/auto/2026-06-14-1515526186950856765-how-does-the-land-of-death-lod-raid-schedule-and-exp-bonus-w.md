# How does the Land of Death (LoD) raid schedule and EXP bonus work?

**Q:** How does the timing of the Land of Death (LoD) raid work, and is there an increased EXP rate after the boss (DH) spawns?

**A:** According to one player, the LoD portal stays open for a total of 2 hours, but is only accessible to enter during the first hour. Once the boss (DH) spawns, players already inside can continue through the second hour, but no one outside can enter anymore. After DH spawns, EXP gain is said to increase by 3x, which is why many players wait until just before the spawn to start playing, while more dedicated players do both hours to rush alts as fast as possible. This information was not confirmed by other players in the conversation, so it should be treated with some caution.
