# Best partner for Sword class: Forga or Perti?

**Q:** What is the best partner for a Sword build: Forga or Perti?

**A:** It depends on your equipment and playstyle. Perti's S-buff gives 20% damage reduction, offering more tankiness useful for League of Legends (LoL) runs or raids. Forga's S-buff gives 5% more combat EXP and 7% more damage, which helps meet minimum damage thresholds against mobs in LoL. Choose Perti if you need survivability, or Forga if you want more overall damage output.
