# Expected XP rate per hour while leveling from 80 to 90

**Q:** How much XP per hour can you expect from farming maps between level 80 and 90? Is 10%/h solo a reasonable expectation with all XP boosts active?

**A:** XP gain per hour depends heavily on your boosts, the map you farm, and whether you play solo or in a group. With average boosts, solo farming typically yields around 6-8% per hour, so 10% per hour solo is a good result when using all available XP boosts.
