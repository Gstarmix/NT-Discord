# Why do two identical Legendary Long Swords of Admiral Yi have different crit chance?

**Q:** I have two of the same Legendary weapon (Long Sword of Admiral Yi) but they show different critical hit chance (417% vs 477%) and different bonus stats. Is this a bug or a typo?

**A:** This is not a bug: legendary equipment gets randomized bonus stats (from crafting, random boxes, or shell balls), so two copies of the same base item can end up with different crit chance and other rolled bonuses depending on who built them and how. Separately, a stat-rebalancing maintenance changed how crit is calculated on some equipment; older items obtained before that update may not have been retroactively updated to the new correct values, while newer ones reflect the current formula.
