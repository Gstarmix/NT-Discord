# Login blocked repeatedly with 24-hour wait after using Facebook login

**Q:** After logging in a few times recently, a popup appears that blocks me from playing even though I enter the correct password, and I then have to wait 24 hours before I can try again. How can this be fixed?

**A:** This is a known issue reported around login attempts made via a linked Facebook account, and the in-client password reset links may not work either. The workaround that fixed it was to reset the account password directly through Gameforge's password recovery page (gameforge.com password recovery) rather than through the client, after which login worked again.
