# Stuck with an unusable quest after reaching level 99

**Q:** I can no longer accept a certain quest because I'm level 99, but the game also won't let me give up/cancel that quest, so it's stuck showing. How do I remove it?

**A:** This is a known bug affecting other players as well. Fully relogging (disconnecting and logging back in) resolved the issue and cleared the stuck quest.
