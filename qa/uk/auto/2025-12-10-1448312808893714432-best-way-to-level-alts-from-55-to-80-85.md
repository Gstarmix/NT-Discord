# Best way to level alts from 55 to 80/85

**Q:** What is the best way to level up alt characters from level 55 to 80/85, now that Land of Death (LoD) is accessible from level 65+?

**A:** Level through quests until level 65, then switch to Land of Death (LoD) grinding, or alternatively keep doing quests all the way if preferred.
