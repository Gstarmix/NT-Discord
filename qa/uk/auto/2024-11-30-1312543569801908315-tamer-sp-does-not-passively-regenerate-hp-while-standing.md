# Tamer SP does not passively regenerate HP while standing

**Q:** Is it normal that while using the Tamer SP, HP does not regenerate passively while just standing (unlike without SP or with other SPs), and only recovers while resting?

**A:** Yes, this is expected behavior. It has been known since the Tamer SP's release that it lacks the passive standing HP regeneration other SPs have, and HP only recovers while resting.
