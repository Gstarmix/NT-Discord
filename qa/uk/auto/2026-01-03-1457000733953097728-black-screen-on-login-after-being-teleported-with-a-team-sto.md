# Black screen on login after being teleported with a team stone

**Q:** After being teleported with my group using a team stone, I land on a black screen at login and nothing happens; afterward my inventory also appears bugged. How do I fix this?

**A:** This is a known issue: the team stone can place you on a map that isn't properly loaded in your client files, resulting in a black screen. The recommended fix is to repair or reinstall the NosTale client. If your inventory looks bugged afterward, first check whether a temporary inventory expansion simply expired before assuming it's related — but repairing the client is still advised either way.
