# Why the honor shop can cost more Honor than the listed price

**Q:** I have 96,225 Honor but the shop still tells me I don't have enough to buy an item priced at 90,000 Honor. Why?

**A:** Low Dignity applies a penalty (around 10%) to shop prices, including Honor shop prices. So an item nominally priced at 90,000 Honor can effectively cost close to 99,000 Honor if your Dignity is reduced. To pay the listed 90,000 price you either need to raise your Honor above that penalized amount, or restore your Dignity so the penalty no longer applies.
