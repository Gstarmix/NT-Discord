# What does each round of Infernal Crypt (IC) reward?

**Q:** What rewards does each round of an 80 Infernal Crypt (IC) give?

**A:** The main round to care about is wave 4 (the Magmaros wave), which can give full moon and angel feathers. The final round afterward mainly rewards gold and fame; you don't need to fight much there, just try not to die and let others clear the mobs.
