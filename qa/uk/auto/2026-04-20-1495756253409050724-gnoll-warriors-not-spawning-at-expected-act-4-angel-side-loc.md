# Gnoll Warriors not spawning at expected Act 4 Angel side locations

**Q:** Why are there no Gnoll Warriors on the Act 4 Angels side in Dragonveil channels 1 and 2, even though their spawn points from NosAPki show nothing there? I got 3 of them the day before, so something seems to have changed.

**A:** There are normally only a small number of Gnoll Warriors (around 4) on that map, so an empty spawn point can simply mean someone else already killed them (channel switching doesn't reset this, since channels share the same instance state, e.g. Glace acting as 'ch51'). Separately, on NosFire specifically, mob counts are multiplied compared to the official reference, so Gnoll Warriors can appear at rougher/different spawn points than what NosAPki shows for the live server.
