# Old singular rarity 7/8 boxes were not converted to the new stacked box format

**Q:** Is it normal to still have old singular (non-stacking) rarity 7/8 item boxes, or should they have been converted to the new stacked box type?

**A:** It is normal. Old singular boxes obtained before the change to stacked boxes were not retroactively converted or replaced. Only boxes obtained after the change use the new stacked format.
