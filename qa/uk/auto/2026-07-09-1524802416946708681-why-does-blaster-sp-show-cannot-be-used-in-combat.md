# Why does Blaster SP show "cannot be used" in combat?

**Q:** As a Blaster, my SP skill sometimes shows "cannot be used" in combat. Why does this happen?

**A:** Blaster SP builds up heat as you use it. Once you reach the highest heat/buff stage, the skill locks and you can only use normal hits for a short period before it becomes usable again.
