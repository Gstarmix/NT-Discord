# How to count as a helper for raid box rewards

**Q:** How do you complete raids as a 'helper' to get credit for the box reward? Being in the box reward range didn't seem to count when doing the Cuby raid with a level 46 character among level 80-90 players.

**A:** You need to be within the level range required for the chest reward, shown as a winged heart icon (or red nickname) next to your name in the raid list. Additionally, at least 3 players within the reward level range are needed in the raid for it to count, and the team leader may also need to be within that reward level range.
