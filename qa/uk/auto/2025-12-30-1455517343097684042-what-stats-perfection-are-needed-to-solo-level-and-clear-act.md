# What stats/perfection are needed to solo level and clear Act 9/10 content?

**Q:** What is a good perfection level for a character around level 30 who wants to solo-level up to around level 80, and also solo Act 9 and Act 10 content?

**A:** Around 60% perfection is okay for leveling, and it starts getting good around 75%. As a bare minimum for combat effectiveness, you should aim for at least 100 attack and 80 proprioception (defense stat), along with equipment that provides bonus damage and critical stats. Overall requirements depend heavily on the rest of your equipment, not perfection alone.
