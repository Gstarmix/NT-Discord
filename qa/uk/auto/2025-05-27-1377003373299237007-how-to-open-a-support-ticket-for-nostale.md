# How to open a support ticket for NosTale

**Q:** How do I submit a support ticket for an in-game issue?

**A:** Tickets are submitted through the Gameforge support site (support.gameforge.com). You need to create a separate account on that support site — your in-game account credentials cannot be used to log in there.
