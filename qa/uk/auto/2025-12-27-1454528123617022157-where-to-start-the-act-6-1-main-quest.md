# Where to start the Act 6.1 main quest

**Q:** At what level can the Act 6.1 quest be started, and which NPC gives it? (Was mistakenly looking for it from an NPC in Cylloan.)

**A:** The Act 6.1 quest is actually given by Graham in NosVille, not by an NPC in Cylloan. To reach the relevant progression, you can either teleport to Cylloan or pick up the Act 6.1 quest directly.
