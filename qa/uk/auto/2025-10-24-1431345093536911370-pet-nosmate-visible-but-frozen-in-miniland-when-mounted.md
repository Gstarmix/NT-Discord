# Pet/nosmate visible but frozen in Miniland when mounted

**Q:** If you relog (or haven't visited your Miniland yet this session), mount up, then enter your Miniland, your pet and nosmate appear inside the Miniland and can move despite you being mounted. Once you unmount, they teleport to you inside the Miniland, and afterward they will no longer appear inside the Miniland while mounted (no matter where you mount up) until you relog and reproduce the sequence. Is this a known bug?

**A:** Yes, this is an already known issue: entering your Miniland for the first time in a session while mounted causes your pet/nosmate to be visibly inside the Miniland and movable, but unmounting teleports them to you. After that happens once, they won't reappear inside the Miniland while mounted again until you relog and repeat the sequence.
