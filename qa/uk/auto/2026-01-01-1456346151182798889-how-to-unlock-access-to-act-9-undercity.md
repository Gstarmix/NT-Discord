# How to unlock access to Act 9 Undercity

**Q:** How do I get to the Act 9 Undercity?

**A:** Take the quest from Mimi and keep repeating it until Undercity access unlocks. Once unlocked, you can set a return point there or use Friend Wings to get back. It may also now be possible to walk in normally through the upper portal, though this wasn't confirmed with full certainty.
