# How to complete the battle pass minigame raid quest

**Q:** How do I complete the battle pass quest that requires winning minigames during a raid three times?

**A:** You need to actually win each minigame. In the Meteors minigame, survive until the last wave. In the Sheep minigame, avoid getting negative points. In the Bushi minigame, win with your team and reach enough action points.
