# Do Sealed Chests from Vessels still drop Tarot Card Games, Fairy Experience Potions, Huge Recovery Potions and Seed of Power

**Q:** Since when do Sealed Chests obtained from Vessels no longer drop Tarot Card Games, Fairy Experience Potions, Huge Recovery Potions and Seed of Power? After opening over 2000 chests during an event none of these items appeared.

**A:** This does not appear to be a confirmed drop rate change. Extra event-exclusive drop items are announced separately in a dedicated forum thread for each event, and Fairy Experience Potions and Seeds of Power were not part of the announced extra drops for that particular event. Huge Recovery Potions were checked and confirmed to still be part of the chest drop pool. Whether Tarot Card Games were removed from the base chest content remained unclear and needed further checking, and it was also suggested some of the reported items may never have actually been part of the chest drop table. Event-exclusive bonus drops do not affect the base chest and jackpot drop rates.
