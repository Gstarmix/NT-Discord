# Archer fairy comparison: Forga vs Marie vs Feraspa

**Q:** For archers, which fairy is better: Marie, Wasp, or Forga? I currently have Forga S/S/S +89 and I'm considering switching to Marie.

**A:** Forga is decent, but Marie is considered better because of its DBFS boost, higher attack%, and its heal. Feraspa is regarded as the best of the three unless you specifically need the heal or the level 5 DBFS that Marie provides.
