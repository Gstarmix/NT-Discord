# Can you remove an account from the Gameforge client?

**Q:** Is it possible to remove some accounts from my Gameforge client?

**A:** This is no longer possible directly in the client; the option was removed, reportedly due to abuse related to one of the NosFire servers. To have an account removed, you need to open a support ticket instead.
