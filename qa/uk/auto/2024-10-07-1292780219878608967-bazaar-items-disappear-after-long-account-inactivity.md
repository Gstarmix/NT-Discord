# Bazaar items disappear after long account inactivity

**Q:** I listed SP cards and items in the Bazaar using the 30-day medal, then couldn't access my account for about 2 months. When I logged back in, my items and the gold from any sales were gone. Can this be recovered?

**A:** This is expected behavior, not a bug: items listed in the NosBazaar remain available to collect for some time after the listing expires, but if they aren't collected within that window they are permanently lost, along with any pending proceeds. Support may look into individual cases via a ticket, but recovery is not guaranteed.
