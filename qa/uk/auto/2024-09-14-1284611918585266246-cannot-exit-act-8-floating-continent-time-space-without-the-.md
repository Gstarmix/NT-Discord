# Cannot exit act 8 'Floating Continent' time space without the guardian's magic orb

**Q:** I got stuck in a time space and couldn't finish or exit it (I got kicked out). What do I need to do to complete and leave it? Having the required item in my equipment window didn't seem to work.

**A:** Always check the time space's description before entering it. For the act 8 'Floating Continent' time space, you need to be carrying a guardian's magic orb in your inventory (not equipped or elsewhere) in order to exit properly.
