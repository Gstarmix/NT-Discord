# Family history log breaking / displaying incorrectly

**Q:** Is there a way to fix the family history/log when it stops working properly?

**A:** This is a known issue: long multi-line system messages in the family history log can break its display. Players report a workaround where family members post several short one-line messages (e.g. 'quote of the day' with very short text) — it takes quite a few of them, not just one, to push the broken entry out of the visible history and make the log readable again. This is only a temporary fix; the log can break again later.
