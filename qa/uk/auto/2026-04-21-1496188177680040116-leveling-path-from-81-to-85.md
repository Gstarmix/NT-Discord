# Leveling path from 81 to 85+

**Q:** At level 81, what should I be doing to progress and prepare for higher levels?

**A:** Level up to 85 by completing Valhalla missions or running Land of Death (LoD), then move to Act 6 for further experience grinding. Act 5.1 (desert) quests are also recommended over Act 5.2 (volcano) quests, since the 5.1 questline gives up to about 23kk in rewards overall and levels you up to around 86.
