# How to report a cheater when no GM is online

**Q:** How can you get a Game Master to intervene against a player who is using cheats on the exp map, given that no GM seems to be online?

**A:** There is usually no live GM presence to catch cheaters in real time. The recommended approach is to submit a support ticket with evidence (screenshots, video, etc.) of the cheating and wait for staff to review it; there is no other way to get an immediate in-game intervention.
