# Land of Death channel distribution constraints

**Q:** Why can't Land of Death events be distributed evenly across all channels (e.g. like in League of Legends) instead of a fixed 1-1-1-1-1-3-3-3 pattern?

**A:** Land of Death is capped at occurring a maximum of twice per day on channel 1. Community discussion suggested this uneven distribution across channels is likely due to code/technical limitations rather than a deliberate design choice, though this was not confirmed officially.
