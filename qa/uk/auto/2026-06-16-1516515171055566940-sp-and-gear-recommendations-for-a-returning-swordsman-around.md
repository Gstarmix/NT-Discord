# SP and gear recommendations for a returning swordsman around level 80-85

**Q:** As a returning swordsman player (last played around 2014-15), besides Warrior and Berserker, are there any widely recommended newer SP classes worth trying? Also, at level 80 heading to 85, what weapon and armor should I aim for? I currently have Broken Elemental, Heavy Defender and Ballista, and I'm progressing through Act quests and time spaces (just finished TS 68).

**A:** Berserker can carry you through everything for now; later on, Sader (SP3) is a good support option and Stone Breaker (SP12) is a strong future pick. If you haven't done the Act 4 main quest yet, do it, since it rewards equipment, and the Act 6 main quest also gives class-10 equipment useful while leveling. The Act 80 main quest gives a level 79 rarity 5+7 item plus a stone to upgrade one SP (1-4) to level 41+9, and instantly levels you to 81; look for level 81 gear afterward (such as the Great Leader's or Forgotten Hero's sets), rarity 4 and +5 is enough to keep progressing. Good weapon/armor options around this level include Magmoros Sword, Flame Giant Shell Arm, Ballista (if not playing Sader), or Phoenix's Claw for Sader. To unlock Act 4 you must first complete all Act 2 and Act 3 quests; from Act 6 onward you only need the corresponding time spaces completed, no further quests required.
