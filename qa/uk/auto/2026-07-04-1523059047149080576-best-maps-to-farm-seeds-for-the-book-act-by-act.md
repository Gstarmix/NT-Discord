# Best maps to farm seeds for the book, act by act

**Q:** What is the best way to farm seeds for the book: solo or in a party? Which maps are best per act?

**A:** Farming in a party is generally better than solo, since even if the party can't kill on one map members can spread across several maps and use Wheel of Fortune (WoF) teleports to gather everyone efficiently. Recommended maps by act: Act 5, opinions vary between Eastern Path, Kertos' Claws or Katol's Street (Act 5 maps can occasionally bug out and stop spawning bosses for a long time); Act 6, Hell's Ruins 2; Act 7, Polluted Forest/Path or Celestial Spire; Act 8, Eastern Skytrail (bee mobs); Act 9, Western Mine 1, Supply Route or Power Plants, ideally wherever mob density is high and Polluts/Arma bosses don't spawn.
