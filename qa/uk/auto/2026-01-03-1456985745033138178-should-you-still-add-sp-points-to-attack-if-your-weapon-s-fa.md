# Should you still add SP points to attack if your weapon's fairy bonus already covers it?

**Q:** My weapon has 87 base damage, and my weapon's fairy gives +13 SL damage, bringing my total to 100 damage. Is it still worth putting SP skill points into attack in this case?

**A:** No — if the weapon's own points (e.g. 87) already sum with the fairy's SL bonus (e.g. +13) to reach the cap (100 in this case), adding more SP points to attack is wasted since the total is already maximized. Also, you cannot exceed 100 attack until your SP reaches roughly +16 to +20; any points invested beyond the cap are worthless until then.
