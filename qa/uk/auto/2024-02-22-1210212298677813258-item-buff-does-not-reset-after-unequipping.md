# Item buff does not reset after unequipping

**Q:** A buff from an item doesn't seem to reset after unequipping the item. Is this a bug and how can it be fixed?

**A:** This was a known issue as of February 2024. The workaround is to remove the item, relog, and then re-equip it again, which clears the stuck buff.
