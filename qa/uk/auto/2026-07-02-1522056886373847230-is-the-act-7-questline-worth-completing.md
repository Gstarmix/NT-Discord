# Is the Act 7 questline worth completing

**Q:** Is it worth doing the act 7 questline?

**A:** Yes, if you want the golden stone, which allows a +9 upgrade on any SP (potentially useful for SP12 if it turns out to support the golden stone, though that's not confirmed), plus a bound c25/8 equipment set. If you don't plan to keep questing on that character, there's no real point pushing through it just for leveling, since you can level via the normal path instead.
