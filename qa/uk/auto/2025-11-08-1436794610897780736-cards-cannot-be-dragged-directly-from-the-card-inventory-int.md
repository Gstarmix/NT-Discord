# Cards cannot be dragged directly from the Card inventory into Card Upgrade/Perfection

**Q:** Why can't I put my cards into the perfection or card upgrade window?

**A:** Cards must first be moved to the 'Equip' tab of the inventory before they can be used in the Card Upgrade/Perfection window; dragging them directly from the raw Card inventory tab does not work.
