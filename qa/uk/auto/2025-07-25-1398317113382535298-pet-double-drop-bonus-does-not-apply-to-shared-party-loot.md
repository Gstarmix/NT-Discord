# Pet double-drop bonus does not apply to shared party loot

**Q:** After the recent patch, event seeds (and several other act 4 items, possibly because they share the same item group) no longer seem to benefit from the double-drop effect from pets like Purrsival and Rainbow Ghost, even though shells in act 4 still do.

**A:** The x2 drop bonus provided by pets does not apply when the loot is shared among all party members. This explains why some items (like event seeds) don't seem to double while others (like shells) do, depending on how their drop is shared in a party.
