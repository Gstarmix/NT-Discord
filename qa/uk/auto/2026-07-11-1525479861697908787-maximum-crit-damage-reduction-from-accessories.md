# Maximum crit damage reduction from accessories

**Q:** Is there a maximum amount of crit damage reduction obtainable from accessories, and should crit reduction be prioritized on every accessory piece?

**A:** Crit reduction on accessories is worth prioritizing on every piece. There is no hard cap because it works additively, being subtracted directly from the enemy's crit damage (e.g. 340 crit damage minus 140 crit reduction equals 200 final crit damage). Each of the three accessory jewels can give up to 50% (150% total), coming mostly from the Cellon option rolled into the jewel: Cellon level 9 gives up to 45%, Cellon level 10 up to 55%, plus a base 5% shown on the jewel itself. These three jewels also grant +4 fairy element (unlike the C50 jewels, which are pure defense), and passive crit reduction from the rest of your gear stacks on top.
