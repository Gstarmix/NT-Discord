# Act 10 equipment upgrade pity system

**Q:** How does the new pity system for equipment upgrades work in Act 10 (Dimensional Tales)?

**A:** A pity system begins applying starting at the +10 upgrade step. Without it, upgrading from +9 to +10 would actually be harder than going from +10 to +11, so the pity mechanic exists specifically to offset that difficulty spike at +10.
