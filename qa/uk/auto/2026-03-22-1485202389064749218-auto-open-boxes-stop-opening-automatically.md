# Auto-open boxes stop opening automatically

**Q:** Why do auto-open boxes stop working automatically? I saved up boxes all week and tried to open them all at once but the auto-open stopped.

**A:** There is a known bug affecting Draco and Glace boxes when they are opened while on an Adventurer character, causing the auto-open feature to stop prematurely.
