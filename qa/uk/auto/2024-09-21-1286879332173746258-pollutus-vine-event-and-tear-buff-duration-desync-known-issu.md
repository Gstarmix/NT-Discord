# Pollutus vine event and tear buff duration desync (known issue)

**Q:** In the Pollutus fight, the vine event duration seems to be extended by a few seconds when Pollutus spawns pieces while the vine event is active, but the tear buff that lets other players free vined allies is not extended the same way. This can cause vined players to die because the tear buff runs out before the vine event ends. It also looks like Pollutus cannot end the vine event while his pieces are still alive, which delays the vine-to-kill window without extending the tear buff.

**A:** This is a known issue as of the message date: recent quality-of-life changes to Pollutus introduced inconsistencies between the vine event duration and the tear buff duration, and pieces being active appears to block Pollutus from ending the vine event, indirectly extending the gap without extending the tear buff. The team is aware and stated it needs a deeper review of all rules, cooldowns and durations before it can be fixed; no workaround or timeline was given.
