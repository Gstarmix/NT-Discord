# SSL certificate error when launching the Gameforge client

**Q:** The Gameforge client shows an SSL error stating the certificate for spark.gameforge.com is not yet valid, warning that the connection might be compromised. How can this be fixed?

**A:** This is a known client side issue related to the Gameforge client itself rather than NosTale. It can generally be ignored by choosing to proceed anyway (Okay) on the warning. If it prevents you from playing, open a support ticket with Gameforge support since it concerns the GF client, not the game.
