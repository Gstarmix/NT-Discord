# Act 7 Sky Tower quest requirements

**Q:** Do I need to complete the level 85 Time-Space instances (TS) to progress the Act 7 Sky Tower quest?

**A:** The Act 7 main quest is obtained from a new NPC at Port Alveous, reached by landing in Moritios (the ship departs from Mortaz port). Progressing through the Act 7 missions leads you to level 85 Time-Space instances, but you must have completed all quests through the end of Act 5 before you can access them. Once the main quest is completed, you can enter the Sky Tower.
