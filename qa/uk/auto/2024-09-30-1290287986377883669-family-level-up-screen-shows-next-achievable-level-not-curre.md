# Family level up screen shows next achievable level, not current family level

**Q:** My family's visual level shows 19, but the achievement panel shows level 20 as reachable and the upgrade can be purchased. Has the family level actually been updated correctly?

**A:** The achievement panel entry you see is the next unlockable achievement, not proof the level was already reached. Scroll down in the achievement list: only achievements that are actually completed show a 'Completed dd/mm/yyyy' date next to them.
