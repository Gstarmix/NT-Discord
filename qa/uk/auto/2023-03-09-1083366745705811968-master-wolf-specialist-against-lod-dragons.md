# Master Wolf specialist against LoD dragons

**Q:** Can the Master Wolf specialist hit and group the dragons in Land of Dead (LoD)? Which skills work on them, and is upgrading to SP3 worth it there?

**A:** Master Wolf could already hit and group the LoD dragons even before recent changes, it was one of the few specialists able to group them. You can hit the dragon (bone) with all of its skills except the base form's uppercut. For LoD specifically, SP1 tends to give better results than SP3, so bothering with the SP3 upgrade there is not considered necessary.
