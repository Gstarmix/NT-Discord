# How to level up solo in Act 7 without doing the quests

**Q:** How can I level up alone in Act 7 without doing the Act 7 quest chain, since I still need to finish the Act 5.2 time space first? I'm level 86+10 with +10 Act 6.1 equipment.

**A:** Spirit Forest is a good solo leveling spot, especially with a Bishop, Dark Healer or Seer. The Polluted Path (near Olorun's Village) also works well for a light SP; a common trick there is to mob all the witches into a corner first so they stop disturbing your exp, since their debuff heavily lowers shadow resistance and can get your party members killed easily. You do not need to complete the Act 5 quests to progress or complete the Act 6, 7, 8, 9 or 10 quest chains.
