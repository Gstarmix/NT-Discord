# Random speed increase in the dynamic sheep event

**Q:** In the dynamic sheep event, sometimes a player moves noticeably faster than everyone else, which makes other participants accuse them of using a speed script. Is this a known bug, and is it related to being mounted or to a specific mount?

**A:** This is a known issue and not something caused by a specific factor like a particular mount. It happens randomly to different players, and at least one other player confirmed experiencing the same unexplained speed boost. It may be more likely to occur when playing in a group, though this isn't confirmed as the root cause.
