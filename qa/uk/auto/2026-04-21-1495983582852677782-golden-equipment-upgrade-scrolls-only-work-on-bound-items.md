# Golden equipment upgrade scrolls only work on bound items

**Q:** Why don't the free golden equipment scrolls from the NosFire event work on my C-Weapon?

**A:** Limited/event scrolls (such as the golden equipment upgrade scrolls from the NosFire event) can only be used on items that are bound to your character. If the C-Weapon is not bound, the scroll will refuse to apply. This restriction exists specifically to prevent using limited items obtained on alt characters on a main character's gear.
