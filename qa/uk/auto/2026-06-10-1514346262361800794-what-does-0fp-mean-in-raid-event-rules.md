# What does 0FP mean in raid/event rules?

**Q:** In the context of a raid or timed event, what does the rule '0FP' (zero full potions) mean?

**A:** 0FP means no full HP/MP potions are allowed. Instead, players must use percentage based potions, such as ones that restore 100% HP, rather than fixed full-restore potions.
