# Unable to progress to the second TS57 (Time and Space) run

**Q:** I can't do the second TS57 run even though I've done the first TS57 several times and completed both floors the day before. I'm not doing side quests, just trying to rush through the TS floors - do I still need to collect items along the way?

**A:** Check whether TS56 on Western Krem is marked green or blue on the minimap. Most likely the first TS57 wasn't actually completed correctly: right next to the Krem spawn there is a step that requires you to collect specific items rather than just clear the area, and this step is commonly missed even after repeating the run. You cannot enter the final (heroic) TS unless all previous TS floors have been properly completed.
