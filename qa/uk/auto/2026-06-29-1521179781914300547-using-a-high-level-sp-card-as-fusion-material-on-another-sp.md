# Using a high level SP card as fusion material on another SP

**Q:** If you use another Partner SP card (for example a +100 card) as fusion material to level up a different SP such as a Feraspa, does the excess experience beyond what is needed to reach the target's next tier get preserved, or does it lock in the level jump and lose the rest of the experience? For example, starting from a +0 Feraspa and feeding it a +100 card, would it only jump to +19 and lose the surplus experience, or would that surplus carry over so fewer future copies are needed?

**A:** Any experience beyond what is needed for the level jump is lost, it does not carry over or get stored for future use. You still need the required number of copies in between to progress the SP further, regardless of how high level the material card used was. The SP fusion screen shows the exact level range gained (for example level 14 to 39) along with the gold and item cost required for the fusion.
