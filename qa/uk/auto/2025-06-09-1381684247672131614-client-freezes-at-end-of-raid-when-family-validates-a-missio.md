# Client freezes at end of raid when family validates a mission

**Q:** Why does the game freeze at the end of raids when a family mission gets validated? This seems to happen more when several characters from the same account/family are connected at once.

**A:** This is a known issue as of June 2025: when a family mission is validated, the client updates and reloads the mission list, which can cause a freeze, especially noticeable with multiple connected characters. No fix or workaround was given yet, but it has been acknowledged for further investigation.
