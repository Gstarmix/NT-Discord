# Is it worth upgrading the Sealed Heavenly Staff from +7 to +8?

**Q:** I currently use a Legendary Sealed Heavenly Staff +7. Is it worth upgrading it to +8 (I can afford the perfumes needed)? I mainly care about the 'Increased Damage to Plants' stat and the general monster damage stat, plus the Undisturbed effect.

**A:** Yes, upgrading from +7 to +8 is worthwhile if you can afford the perfumes required — it raises damage by 15, damage to plants by 18% (useful for the Land of Oblivion/LoL content), among other stat increases. Whether it's a good use of resources also depends on your character level: it's recommended for characters around c25-c35, but for c52-c59 it may be better to wait and save perfumes for the c60/c65 tier weapon instead.
