# Cuby seal timepiece direction arrow always points right

**Q:** When trying to locate a Cuby seal timepiece, the direction arrow only points to the right and stays at the edge of the screen instead of pointing toward the target. Is this normal?

**A:** This is a known issue related to the keyboard language setting. Changing your keyboard language (for example from English US to English UK) fixes the direction arrow so it points correctly again.
