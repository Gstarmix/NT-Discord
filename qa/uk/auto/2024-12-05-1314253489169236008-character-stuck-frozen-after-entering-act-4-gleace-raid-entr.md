# Character stuck/frozen after entering Act 4 (Gleace) raid entrance

**Q:** My character gets stuck (frozen/bugged) when entering Act 4 (Gleace). Restarting and repairing the client did not fix it. How do I resolve this?

**A:** As of December 2024 this was a known issue affecting more than one player: entering Act 4 (Gleace), particularly around a raid entrance, could leave the character effectively trapped, with the screen frozen. Restarting the game and repairing client files did not resolve it, and no confirmed workaround was reported at the time.
