# Do non-reputation PvP damage items (like the c53 ring) also reduce PvE damage?

**Q:** Reputation ('honorable') equipment gives a PvP damage bonus but applies an opposite penalty to PvE damage. Does other PvP-effect gear, such as the c53 hero ring, also carry this PvE penalty?

**A:** Only equipment obtained through the reputation system has this 'opposite PvE' penalty mechanic. Other items with PvP-only effects (such as certain hero rings, or items like the Mystic Amulet's PvP effects on token/glove, or Azrael's mask) do not reduce PvE damage. This can be tested by removing your weapon, attacking a target to note the base damage, then equipping the item in question — non-reputation PvP items leave the PvE damage number unchanged. Some players in the discussion still expressed uncertainty about this at low damage values, where the small percentage differences are harder to observe.
