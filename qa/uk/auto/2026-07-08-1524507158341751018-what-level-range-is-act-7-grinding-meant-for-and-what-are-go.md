# What level range is Act 7 grinding meant for, and what are good leveling breakpoints?

**Q:** Is Act 7 supposed to be used for leveling from around character level 10 to 60? How does today's leveling speed there compare to before Act 8 existed?

**A:** Historically, before Act 8 released, players used to grind Act 7 all the way to character level 60 (and before that, it was used up to the old level 99/class 60 cap), often competing heavily for maps during leveling events. Today, thanks to the League of Legends-style bonus map (LoL), extra experience costumes, and buff cards, leveling is much faster and less competitive. Common map/level breakpoints while leveling through this content are roughly character level 28, 38, 48, 60, and 70/75/80, and the NosPaki tool has a dedicated section covering this progression.
