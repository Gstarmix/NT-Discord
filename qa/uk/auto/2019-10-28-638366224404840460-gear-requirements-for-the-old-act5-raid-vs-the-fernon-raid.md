# Gear requirements for the old Act5 raid vs. the Fernon raid

**Q:** What did you need to clear the old Act5 raid compared to what's required to farm the Fernon raid efficiently today?

**A:** For the old Act5 raid, being level 90 with decent equipment (or just a decent armor if playing Holy, Mage or Swordman/Troyer) was enough to join. For the Fernon raid, requirements are much higher: you need 2 of your 5 alt characters ready, +38 upgraded gear, good R7 equipment, and gemmed SP cards if you want to clear more than 20 runs per hour.
