# Difference between CXP and CEXP shell stats

**Q:** Shell stat descriptions vary depending on translation: does 'Gain More CXP' refer to job level experience (as some translations suggest by literally showing 'CXP' next to the job level icon), and how is it different from CEXP?

**A:** CXP refers to job (profession) level experience, matching the in-game icon shown next to the job level bar, while CEXP is a separate stat referring to Champion experience. Translations that render 'CXP' as 'Job Level' are therefore accurate, and this is a case of confusing but not incorrect naming rather than a translation bug.
