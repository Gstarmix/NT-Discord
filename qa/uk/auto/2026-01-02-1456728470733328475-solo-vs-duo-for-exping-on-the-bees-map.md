# Solo vs duo for exping on the bees map

**Q:** What's the best way to gain exp on the bees map — solo or in a duo?

**A:** Soloing the bees map is recommended. Doing it in a duo can make mobs die too quickly for efficient exp gain, unless you're exploiting copied/instanced maps.
