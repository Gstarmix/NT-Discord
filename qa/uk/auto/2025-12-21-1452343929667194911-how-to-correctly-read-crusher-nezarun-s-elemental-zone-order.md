# How to correctly read Crusher Nezarun's elemental zone order

**Q:** Crusher Nezarun's raid order seems to kill players unexpectedly: after being told to enter a zone that isn't fire or shadow, then being told to enter the zone opposite to the previous one, entering fire results in death. Do the orders stack, or is there a misunderstanding?

**A:** This is a known translation/wording issue, not an actual mechanic bug. The instruction to enter the zone 'opposite to the previous zone' refers to the opposite of the previous TASK (i.e., opposite of 'not fire or shadow'), which is fire or shadow — not the opposite of the zone you actually entered. The confusing phrasing is on the list of translations to be reviewed after the holidays.
