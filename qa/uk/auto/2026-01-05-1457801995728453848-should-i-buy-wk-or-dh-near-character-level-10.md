# Should I buy WK or DH near character level 10?

**Q:** When close to reaching character level 10, should I buy the Wrath Keeper (WK) or Dark Healer (DH) specialist card? If planning to play in a team, should WK or DH be prioritized?

**A:** If you plan to play in a team (e.g. for Act 7), DH is generally better than WK for survival since DH can heal you while WK cannot. WK stops being needed past roughly class level 30, so don't invest heavily in it unless money isn't an issue — if you can afford it, getting both is fine.
