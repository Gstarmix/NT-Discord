# Reaching combat level 10 via Valhalla quests

**Q:** Can you reach combat level 10 (C10) just by doing all the Valhalla quests?

**A:** No, Valhalla quests only give normal experience, not combat level (Clvl) experience — Clvl comes only from killing mobs. To reach C10, completing the act 6 questline is recommended, as it rewards C10 equipment at the end.
