# Why can't I end/exit a time-space run a second time?

**Q:** Why did a time-space (TS) run fail to end properly the second time it was attempted, seemingly wasting hours of playtime?

**A:** Some time-spaces (e.g. the ancient dragon reputation one shown, level 90-99) require a specific item, the 'Orbe magique du gardien' (guardian's magic orb), not just to enter but also to exit. If you don't have this item in hand when trying to leave, the space-time cannot be properly ended.
