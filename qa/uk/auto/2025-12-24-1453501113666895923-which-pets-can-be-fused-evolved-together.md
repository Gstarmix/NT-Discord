# Which pets can be fused/evolved together?

**Q:** Can any pet be evolved together as long as they are 1-star or 2-star? For example, would a Tired Hamster and an Angry Hamster count as different pets for this purpose?

**A:** Yes, any two pets can be evolved together as long as they are 1-star or 2-star and you personally evolved them yourself (they cannot be pets bought already evolved by someone else). Once a pet reaches 5-star, you can no longer repeat the same pet species in the fusion chain.
