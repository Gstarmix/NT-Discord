# Kertos/Valakus all-resistance vs Pollu/Arma cores for PvE

**Q:** Should I prioritize the Kertos/Valakus all-resistance costumes or the Pollu/Arma cores for PvE survivability?

**A:** Pollu/Arma cores are the better investment since they provide the PvE resistances you actually need, more so than Kertos/Valakus. Kertos/Valakus mainly help mages who still use the Arcane Tattoo, since their defense bonus satisfies the tattoo's activation condition, mages swap them in to trigger the tattoo effect, then switch back to their normal PvE resistance gear.
