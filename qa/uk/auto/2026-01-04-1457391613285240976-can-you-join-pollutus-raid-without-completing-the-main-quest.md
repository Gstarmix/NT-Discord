# Can you join Pollutus raid without completing the main quest?

**Q:** Am I allowed to enter the Pollutus raid if I haven't completed the related main quest?

**A:** Yes — as long as you meet the requirements to enter Act 9 (level 80+), you can join the Pollutus and Arma raids without having finished the main quest line first.
