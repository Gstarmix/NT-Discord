# Can't log into GameForge client despite correct credentials

**Q:** I can log in through the website with my main email, but the GameForge client rejects the same correct email/password even after changing the password and reinstalling the client. How can this be fixed?

**A:** If the credentials are correct, avoid further login attempts and wait about 24 hours before trying again, since repeated failed attempts can cause a temporary client-side lockout. If the issue persists after waiting, open a support ticket with GF support.
