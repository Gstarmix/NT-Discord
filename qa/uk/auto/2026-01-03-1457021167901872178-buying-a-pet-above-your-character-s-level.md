# Buying a pet above your character's level

**Q:** If I buy an Otter that is level 99 while my character is level 85, will I still be able to use it, and will its level decrease automatically or will it just be unusable?

**A:** A pet's level will not decrease automatically; the pet can only be used if its level is equal to or below your character's level. To lower a pet's level so it can be used, you need to use strange pet food (a special pet food that reduces pet level).
