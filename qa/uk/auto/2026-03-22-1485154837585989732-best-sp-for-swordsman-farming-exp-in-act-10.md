# Best SP for Swordsman farming exp in Act 10

**Q:** As a Swordsman, which SP should I use for farming experience in Act 10?

**A:** Berserker or Stone SP are the best options. Crusader can also work but is more dependent on playing in a duo or trio. Farming Act 10 solo is not recommended since it gives bad time efficiency and therefore bad experience gains.
