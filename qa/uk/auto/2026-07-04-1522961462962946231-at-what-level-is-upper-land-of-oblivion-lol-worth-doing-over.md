# At what level is upper Land of Oblivion (LoL) worth doing over lower LoL

**Q:** I'm level 46 and still doing lower Land of Oblivion, but the experience gained has dropped a lot. From what level is it more worthwhile to move to upper LoL?

**A:** You should move up to a LoL tier where the monsters are about 6 to 8 levels above your own character level. At level 46, you should currently be doing middle LoL rather than upper LoL.
