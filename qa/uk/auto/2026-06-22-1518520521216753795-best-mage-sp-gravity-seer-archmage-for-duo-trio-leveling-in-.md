# Best mage SP (Gravity, Seer, Archmage) for duo/trio leveling in Act 9

**Q:** For a mage with Gravity, Seer, and Archmage SP all built similarly (85+/100 atk/ele) and c80 gear, which SP works best for duo or trio leveling, and on which maps?

**A:** Gravity is comfortable thanks to its provoke and buffs, while Archmage is also strong and has easier access to perfect rolls. Archmage suits polluted maps like rats and slimes, while Gravity or Seer suit robot/mullan maps (and possibly Fire Storm too). One player reported over 12%/hour in a rat map using Light SP +18 with non-optimal gear, sustained up to around character level 86.
