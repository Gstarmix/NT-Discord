# Sources of Gold Drop % bonus

**Q:** What sources of Gold Drop % bonus are there to reach the cap? I have the Bunny Hairband, a +32% gold weapon, a Tarot card, the Illusionist Hat and the Lucky Pink Pony but I'm only around 220%.

**A:** The gold bonus cap is 200%, not 300%. Gold bonus on items used to be doubled on the Nosfire server, which is why reaching high totals was much easier there; on the current server more sources are needed to stack. Known sources include pets/partners (Gloopy Cake Blessing 5%, Fortune Bushtail 5%, Super Fortune Bushtail 20%, Black Ink Rabbit 25%, Daniel Ducats 10-40%), skins/costumes (Summer Fruits Weapon Skin 5%, Illusionist Hat 20%, Bunny Hairband 20%), runes/amulets (Mother Nature's Tree Rune 20%, its Rare version 40%, Wind Rune 15%, Tarot Card The Tower 10%, Tarot Card Fool 10%), the Lucky Pink Pony mini-pet (10%), Shell options (33% +4%), Fairy options (25%), buffs (Confetti Shower 5%, Party Sparkler Wings 5%), and titles (Croesus 1%, Fountain of Fortune 5%).
