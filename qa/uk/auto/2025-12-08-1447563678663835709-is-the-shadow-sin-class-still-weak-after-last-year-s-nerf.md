# Is the Shadow (Sin) class still weak after last year's nerf

**Q:** After the nerf the Sin (Shadow) class received about a year ago, was it ever buffed back, or is it still bad?

**A:** Opinions differ: some say Sin remains weak in PvP since the nerf removed the absorb effect on J20 bomb and increased the cooldown on normal absorb, while key abilities were cut down except Blade Spin, considered the weakest skill in the kit. Others argue Sin did not get much worse in PvP overall, since its damage was buffed and oneshotting enemies actually became easier than before. It may also perform especially well with Act 10 equipment, though this is not certain.
