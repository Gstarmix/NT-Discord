# Team not teleporting properly at the start of the Neza raid

**Q:** Sometimes when starting the Neza raid, the player and/or their team and pets aren't teleported to the boss (the Neza boss instead teleports to them). What causes this and how can it be fixed?

**A:** This is a known but rare bug whose exact trigger isn't confirmed — it's suspected to be related to doing other content (such as Act 9/10 dailies or other raids) beforehand. Simply relogging before or after the issue occurs resolves it and lets the raid teleport work normally.
