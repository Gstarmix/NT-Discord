# Why Whale skill deals less damage than Lightning Storm despite higher stats

**Q:** For an SP6 Mage without a main weapon, why does the Whale skill deal less damage than Lightning Storm (Triple Thunder), even though Whale has 50 more attack and one more elemental point?

**A:** This is generally explained by debuff timing: skills like Triple Thunder apply a debuff that reduces the target's elemental resistance, and this debuff is factored in before the skill's own damage is calculated, so the hit lands on an already weakened resistance. Whale's initial toss carries no such resistance-reducing debuff, so it hits at full resistance and appears weaker despite better raw stats. There is some disagreement among players on whether the debuff actually applies before or after the damage calculation, so this explanation is not fully certain.
