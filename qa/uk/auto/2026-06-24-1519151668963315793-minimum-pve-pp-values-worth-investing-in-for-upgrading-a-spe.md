# Minimum PvE PP values worth investing in for upgrading a Special Card to +20

**Q:** What is the minimum PvE PP on a Special Card to consider it worth upgrading to +20? Does the split between attack and elemental PP matter, or only the total (e.g. is 35/35 better than 20/50 if the sum is the same)?

**A:** For PvE, only the attack and elemental PP stats matter, referred to as atk/ele. Values in the low 20s on both stats are below average. Reaching 30+ on one stat (with the other lower) is somewhat better, but a card is generally considered good once both attack and elemental PP reach 30+. Going further, 40+/30+ (or the reverse) is quite good, and anything higher is better depending on budget. The maximum possible value per stat is 54, and reaching the 50s is a big achievement since it is hard to obtain; a card like 50/30 is considered great. As a rule of thumb, 30+/30+ is the baseline for a card worth investing in, and you can judge higher combinations as desired.
