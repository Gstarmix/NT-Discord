# Mark of the Full Moon (level 6 debuff) disappearing after one hit

**Q:** Is it a bug that the level 6 debuff applied to raid bosses (Mark of the Full Moon) automatically gets removed?

**A:** This is not a bug. Mark of the Full Moon is a level 6 debuff lasting 20 seconds that increases damage taken from critical hits by 50% and adds 50% bonus damage to the very next hit landed on the marked target — but it is consumed as soon as that next hit lands, including hits dealt by pets, which is why it appears to vanish quickly. By contrast, the related debuff 'Bound by the Full Moon's Light' is not consumed early and lasts its full 10-second duration.
