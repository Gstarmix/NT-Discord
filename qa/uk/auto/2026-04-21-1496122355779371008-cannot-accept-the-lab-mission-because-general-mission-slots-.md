# Cannot accept the Lab mission because general mission slots are full of undeletable Soundflower missions

**Q:** I can't accept the lab mission because my general mission slots are full of Soundflower missions, and I can't delete those Soundflower missions because they are too high level. What can I do?

**A:** Try relogging: missions that are too high level to accept or complete are reported to get automatically deleted from your mission log after a relog, which should free up slots for the lab mission.
