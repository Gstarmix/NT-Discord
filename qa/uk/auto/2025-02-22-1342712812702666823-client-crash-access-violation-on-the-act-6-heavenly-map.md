# Client crash (access violation) on the Act 6 Heavenly map

**Q:** The game repeatedly crashes with an access violation error while on the Act 6 Heavenly map. How can this be fixed?

**A:** This is a known client crash issue on the Act 6 Heavenly map with no direct fix. As a workaround, avoid using a mount/vehicle while on that map, since that is believed to trigger the crash.
