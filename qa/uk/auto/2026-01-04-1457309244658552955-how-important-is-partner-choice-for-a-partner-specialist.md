# How important is partner choice for a Partner Specialist

**Q:** As a Partner Specialist, how important is it to have the correct partner between the available classes?

**A:** At low levels the pet matters more since it tanks for you, but as you progress the partner becomes important too - depending on which one you have, you get skills/buffs that increase your damage or received experience, plus debuffs that can help in combat. Over time, having a good partner ends up being a must-have.
