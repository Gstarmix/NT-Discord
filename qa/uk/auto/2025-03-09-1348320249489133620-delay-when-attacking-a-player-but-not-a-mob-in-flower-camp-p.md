# Delay when attacking a player but not a mob in Flower Camp (post rework)

**Q:** After the recent rework, hitting a mob in Flower Camp has no delay, but hitting a player causes a significant attack delay. Is there a fix?

**A:** This delay existed even before the rework, and it is one of the reasons a movement speed cap was added to the game. Not attacking while your pet or partner is also attacking at the same time may help reduce the delay.
