# How can you increase hit rate if armor/shells don't offer it?

**Q:** Why do equipment, shells, etc. offer so many dodge-related stats while armor already has more dodge chance than weapons at the same c-level, with seemingly no way to scale hit rate through shells or other equipment parts?

**A:** Hit rate is not meant to be scaled through armor shells. Instead, players get hit rate through tattoos, runes, and gloves.
