# Archer SP8 vs WK: which deals more damage and levels faster

**Q:** Is the archer SP8 specialist card stronger than WK in terms of damage, and which is better for leveling?

**A:** SP8 kills monsters faster thanks to more AoE range and better skill cooldowns, making it the stronger choice for farming and leveling. WK can be competitive in AoE only if paired with expensive gear like a costume that resets high cooldown skills and mecha wings, but without that investment SP8 outperforms it for general grinding.
