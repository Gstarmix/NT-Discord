# How partner gearing works at combat level

**Q:** How does gearing a partner work at combat level since partners don't seem to have their own separate gear level? If buying soulbound r7/r8 armor for a partner, does it need to be perfumed first? Does equipping partner armor remove the partner's shell?

**A:** A partner shares its hero's combat level. To gear it, buy equipment matching the partner's class (for example archer armor for a c10 partner) and equip it through the partner equipment NPC (Teodor or his twin in NosVille). Soulbound armor does not need to be perfumed before being equipped on a partner. Partners have no shell of their own, only the base armor slot.
