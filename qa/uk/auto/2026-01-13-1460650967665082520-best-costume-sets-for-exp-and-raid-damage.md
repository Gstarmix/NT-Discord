# Best costume sets for exp and raid damage

**Q:** Which costume set is good to buy for both leveling (exp) and raid damage?

**A:** Spirit Guide, Tiny Terror, or Monarch costumes are good picks that combine exp bonus with damage, while the Pharaoh costume is more focused on pure damage. For a lower budget, the Dino or Creepy Monk costumes offer a good damage/exp combination. The right choice ultimately depends on budget and what content you focus on.
