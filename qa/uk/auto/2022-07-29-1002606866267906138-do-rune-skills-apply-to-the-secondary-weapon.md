# Do rune skills apply to the secondary weapon?

**Q:** Does the skill effect of carved runes (the +13% all-attack type bonus, not special effects) apply to the secondary weapon as well?

**A:** Yes, all carved runes' skill bonuses work on the secondary weapon too, not just the primary one.
