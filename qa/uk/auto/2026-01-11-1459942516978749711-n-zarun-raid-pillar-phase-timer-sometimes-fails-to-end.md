# Nézarun raid pillar-phase timer sometimes fails to end

**Q:** During the Crusher Nézarun raid, in the phase where two pillars must be destroyed, the phase timer sometimes doesn't end even after both pillars are killed (possibly when the last pillar is killed by an Onyx Shadow). Is this a known bug?

**A:** Known issue as of this date: the pillar-phase timer can fail to register completion when both pillars are killed within the same second (or very close together). No workaround was given beyond awareness of the issue.
