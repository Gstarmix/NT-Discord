**Q:** For soloing Draco, is a Tide Lord or a Blue weapon at +15 better?

**A:** A Tide Lord at +100 will be cheaper to obtain than an equivalent Blue and should still be sufficient for soloing Draco, though the right choice depends on the rest of your equipment. Generally, Blue weapons are considered somewhat better in PvE overall.
