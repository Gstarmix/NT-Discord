# Gear advice for surviving the Bacoom raid boss as an archer

**Q:** I keep dying a lot in the Bacoom raid, mostly because of Bacoom itself. What should I change in my gear? (Current gear shown: a +4 Desert Robber's Bow, a +9 perfection pet, and an Antique Tunic set armor.) Is it also worth investing 200-300kk in a 100% perfection Dark Horse partner, since I'll need it eventually anyway?

**A:** For surviving Bacoom, use elemental stone resistances from Magmarose (fire) or the arena ones that grant fire and dark resistance, since Bacoom's damage is tied to those elements. For the bow, any bow from the Ibrahim set or the A5.2 (Act 5.2) set is recommended. The question about whether investing in a 100% perfection Dark Horse partner now is a good idea was not directly addressed.
