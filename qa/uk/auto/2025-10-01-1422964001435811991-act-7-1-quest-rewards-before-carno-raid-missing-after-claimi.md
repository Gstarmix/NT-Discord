# Act 7.1 quest rewards (before Carno Raid) missing after claiming

**Q:** After completing the act 7.1 quest step just before the Carno Raid, the reward items did not appear in the inventory or in the item inbox/mailbox, even after relogging. What causes this and how can it be fixed?

**A:** This usually happens when the inventory is full at the moment the quest reward is claimed, causing the items to be lost instead of being sent to the mailbox. Make sure to have empty inventory slots before claiming quest rewards. If items are already lost, submit a support ticket to have them restored.
