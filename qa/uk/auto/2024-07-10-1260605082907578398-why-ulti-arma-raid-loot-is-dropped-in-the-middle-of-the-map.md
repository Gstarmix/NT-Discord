# Why Ulti Arma raid loot is dropped in the middle of the map

**Q:** Is it intended that the loot fix for the Ulti Arma raid now drops loot in the middle of the map? This doesn't seem like a great fix.

**A:** This is a known workaround: dropping loot at the boss's position caused issues when the boss died mid-jump, so the loot is placed in the center of the map instead. This is considered the best available option for now while a better solution is worked on.
