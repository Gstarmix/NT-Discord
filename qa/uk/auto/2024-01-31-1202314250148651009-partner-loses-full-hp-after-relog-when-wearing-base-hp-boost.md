# Partner loses full HP after relog when wearing base-HP-boosting armor

**Q:** After relogging, does a partner lose their full HP bar if they are wearing an armor piece that increases their base maximum HP?

**A:** Yes, this is a known issue: a partner equipped with an armor that boosts base HP (for example one adding +4000 max HP) does not have a full HP bar restored after relogging, showing less than the new maximum HP.
