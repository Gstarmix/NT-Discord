# How rocketeer set cexp bonuses stack

**Q:** How does the cexp bonus from the rocketeer equipment set work? Individual pieces show 10% cexp, 10% cexp, and 30% cexp on the wings — do these bonuses add up together, or is the total fixed at 30%?

**A:** The cexp bonuses from each rocketeer piece add up rather than being capped at the highest single value — every equipped piece contributes its bonus. Wearing the full set gives roughly 40% cexp plus an additional 10% normal exp bonus.
