# Half of the inventory shows as locked on one character

**Q:** Some players report that half of their inventory appears locked (this happens on a specific character even after submitting a ticket). Is this a known issue?

**A:** This is an acknowledged but unresolved account issue: staff confirmed that if nothing abnormal is visible on the account's data side, there is currently no fix available until the underlying cause is identified. Affected players are asked to report whether the issue also occurs when logging in from a different PC, as this helps investigation, but no workaround exists yet.
