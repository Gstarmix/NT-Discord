# Can Martial Artists complete the Glacerus glacial seal time space quest

**Q:** Martial Artists (AM) cannot use the SP6 swordsman card needed for the Glacerus glacial seal time space quest. Is this a bug or discrimination against the class?

**A:** This is not a bug. The Glacerus glacial seal quest requires an SP6 specialty card, and the Martial Artist class does not have an SP6 specialty card, so it cannot complete that step. This is one of several pieces of content intentionally locked to specific classes rather than a client or quest bug.
