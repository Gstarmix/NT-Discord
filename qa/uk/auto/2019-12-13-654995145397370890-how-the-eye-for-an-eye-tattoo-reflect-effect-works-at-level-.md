# How the 'Eye for an Eye' tattoo reflect effect works at level 9

**Q:** At level 9, does the 'Eye for an Eye' tattoo give a 30% chance to reflect 70% of the damage taken from a critical hit (e.g. reflecting 14k out of a 21k hit)?

**A:** Yes, that is correct. However, the effect is largely useless against mages, except against their SP4 form, or if the mage's buff level is too low, in which case the reflect would be negated.
