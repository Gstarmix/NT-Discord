# Kuku partner price and buff usability while on a PSP

**Q:** Why does the Kuku partner cost so much, and what is the difference between it and Yertiland? Can Kuku's buff still be used while the partner is on a PSP?

**A:** Kuku gives a buff of 10% damage and 10 fairy, and is believed to be tankier (more HP) than Yertiland, which likely explains the price difference. Partner buffs and skills remain usable even while a partner is set to use a PSP: you just need to open the partner menu (K) and drag the partner's skills onto your skill bar.
