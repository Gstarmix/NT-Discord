# Screen freezes in minigames when clicking the reward button too fast

**Q:** In minigames, the screen suddenly freezes and I cannot collect items. Is this a known bug?

**A:** This freeze happens if the reward button is double-clicked too fast; avoid spam-clicking it and the freeze should not occur. This has been reported multiple times over the years with different reproduction methods, and as of mid-2024 it is still unresolved, with players waiting for developers to identify the root cause.
