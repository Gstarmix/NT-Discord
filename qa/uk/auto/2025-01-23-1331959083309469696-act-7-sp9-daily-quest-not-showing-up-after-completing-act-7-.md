# Act 7 SP9 daily quest not showing up after completing Act 7 content

**Q:** The Act 7 daily quest asking to gather 15/15 SP9 raid stones is the only Act 7 quest left after finishing all of Act 7.1 and 7.2, but it is not appearing to be picked up, even though it seems to have been done before. Is this a bug?

**A:** This daily quest only becomes available if you have completed the SP9 raid quest at least once; quest progress does not disappear on its own. If you have already completed the SP9 quest and the daily still fails to appear, open a support ticket so staff can check the account; otherwise, complete the SP9 raid quest first to unlock the daily.
