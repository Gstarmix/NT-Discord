# How elemental damage bonuses work

**Q:** Does having an elemental advantage matter against a target with no element, and what are the elemental damage bonuses between element types?

**A:** Elemental matchups grant bonus damage as follows: Fire versus Water and Water versus Fire give +100% damage; Light versus Shadow and Shadow versus Light give +200% damage. In the weaker advantage cycle, Water beats Light, Light beats Fire, Fire beats Shadow, and Shadow beats Water, each giving +50% damage. Any element used against a target with no element gives a +30% damage bonus.
