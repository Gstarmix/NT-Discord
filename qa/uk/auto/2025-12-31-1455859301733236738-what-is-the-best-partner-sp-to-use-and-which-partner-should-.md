# What is the best partner (SP) to use, and which partner should be bought accordingly?

**Q:** What is the best partner SP to use, and is there something better than Forga? Which regular partner should be bought to match a given partner SP?

**A:** You should generally pick a partner SP first, then buy the matching regular partner accordingly; the choice also depends on your budget. For raids, the new Nezarun is currently considered best but is very expensive. For leveling, Feraspa is recommended. For utility, Marie is considered the best overall partner. For cheaper options: Yuna for healing, Forga for damage and leveling experience, and Achenaton for AoE resistance-down (good for LoL).
