# Known issue: proc-based elemental attack bonus effects not triggering correctly

**Q:** Is the 'with X% probability, [element] attack damage is increased by Y%' effect (e.g. on the Thunderer/Achenaton-related debuffs) bugged? It doesn't seem to trigger as described.

**A:** As of this report, several proc-based '% chance to increase [element] attack damage' effects appear unreliable: testers confirmed this with the Thunderer-related debuff, and another player reported the same for a shadow-attack-damage-chance stat, suggesting this may be a broader issue affecting this whole category of proc effects (light/shadow/element attack chance bonuses). Separately, players also noted that Feraspa's skills can sometimes get stuck and become unusable, though the cause is unclear. No official fix or workaround was given.
