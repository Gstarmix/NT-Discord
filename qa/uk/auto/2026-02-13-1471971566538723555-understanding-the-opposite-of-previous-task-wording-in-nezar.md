# Understanding the "opposite of previous task" wording in Nezarun Crusher raid

**Q:** In the Nezarun Crusher raid, after a task saying to avoid everything except fire/light, the next task said to go to "the opposite of the previous task," which resulted in death when interpreted as the elemental opposite (shadow). What is this instruction actually supposed to mean?

**A:** "Opposite of the previous task" does not refer to elemental opposites (like water/fire) but simply means to do the reverse of what the previous task required. If the previous task was to avoid fire/light, the next task means you should go to fire/light. The wording has been considered confusingly written.
