# Friends list doesn't show friends on other channels

**Q:** Why does my friends list fail to show a friend's online status when they are on a different channel, only updating when they message me directly?

**A:** This is a known issue tied to having too many friends on your list: visibility across channels can break once you exceed a certain count. The exact threshold varies per account, so removing some friends until the list starts working properly is a workaround players have found effective.
