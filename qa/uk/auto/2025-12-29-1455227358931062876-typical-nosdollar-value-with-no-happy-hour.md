# Typical NosDollar value with no happy hour

**Q:** How much are NosDollars usually worth in gold when there is no happy hour discount, on both servers?

**A:** When there is no percentage discount on Mall items, a NosDollar is currently worth roughly 140k-170k gold on average (this has risen from an older baseline of around 100k-105k a couple of years ago). The exact value also depends on which items are cheapest in the Mall at the time. If you're not in a hurry, it's better to wait for a happy hour promotion to get better value.
