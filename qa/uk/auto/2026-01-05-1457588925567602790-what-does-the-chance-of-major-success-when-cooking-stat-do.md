# What does the 'chance of major success when cooking' stat do?

**Q:** What does the occurrence stat that reads 'Chance of major success when cooking increased by X%' actually mean? (Shown on effects like Secret Spice, Full Belly II and Full Belly III, tied to the Gourmet item.)

**A:** Similar to fishing, which can randomly yield tinctured tuna, eels, or boxes containing materials/potions, cooking has its own bonus mechanic: each time you successfully cook a dish, there is a chance to additionally receive a cupcake that grants the equivalent of materials or potions. The 'chance of major success when cooking' stat increases the probability of this bonus cupcake occurring.
