# Best jewelry and SP choice for character level 74/80 and raids

**Q:** At character level 74/80, what jewelry should I wear and what options should I prioritize? For Alzanor and water raids, which specialist is better: Grenadier (SP10) or Fog Hunter (SP11)?

**A:** Use the dedicated PvE jewelry set for character levels 72/75/78, which boosts attack and fairy element. For raids, both specialists are viable: SP10 (Grenadier) gives better DPS, while SP11 (Fog Hunter) is a strong debuffer and still deals good damage even if you're not focusing on debuffs.
