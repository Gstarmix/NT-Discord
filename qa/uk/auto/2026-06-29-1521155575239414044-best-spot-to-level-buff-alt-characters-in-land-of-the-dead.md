# Best spot to level buff alt characters in Land of the Dead

**Q:** Where in Land of the Dead should buff alt characters be positioned to level safely?

**A:** Place buff alts inside the dragon skull area so they do not get clipped by the Revenant Skeleton Warrior respawns. Setting up separate windows for each character beforehand makes managing multiple alts much easier. For the Flying Fire Devil, bring your tank character in last and drop it into a corner of the map, the mob will target the last character that entered the map and wander toward it, which keeps the other alts safe without needing to babysit it.
