# Is Instant Combat still worth doing every 2 hours at high level?

**Q:** Is it still worth doing Instant Combat every 2 hours before reaching high level, now that it no longer gives gold?

**A:** Yes, it is still worth doing. Around level 81 you get the last Instant Combat tier, and fighting every round can yield roughly 200k plus materials by the end.
