# Best pet for LoD/LoL runs: Otter, Rainbow Cake, or Panda?

**Q:** For LoD/LoL runs, which pet is more viable, Otter or Rainbow Cake? Is Panda practically better than Rainbow Cake, given Rainbow Cake has higher +% combat exp but Panda has a better skill (voke)? Would Rainbow Cake be fine in a 3-man LoL or a normal family LoL, or is the voke from Otter/Panda necessary to succeed?

**A:** Having more voke pets is a significant quality-of-life advantage, especially if the team includes a seer. A good LoL run generally needs 2 vokes per lure — one initial voke and one to clean up the rest of the lure. If your team already has 2 voke pets covered, you can afford to bring Rainbow Cake instead for its extra attack% and combat exp bonus; otherwise Otter (or Panda) is preferred over Rainbow Cake.
