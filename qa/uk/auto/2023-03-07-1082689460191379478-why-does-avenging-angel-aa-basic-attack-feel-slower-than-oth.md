# Why does Avenging Angel (AA) basic attack feel slower than other archer SP classes

**Q:** Why does the Avenging Angel (AA) archer specialist's basic attack feel slower than other archer SPs (like Ranger, Destroyer, Scout, FK, or DJ), and could animation canceling the basic attack by walking be added for AA?

**A:** Players report that AA's basic attack is noticeably slower than most other archer SP classes. For classes like Ranger, Destroyer, and Scout, sidestepping while attacking increases basic attack speed, but AA does not benefit from this movement-based speed up, leaving it at a slower baseline pace. Players requested that AA be allowed to animation-cancel its basic attack via walking like other classes do, but this was only a suggestion, not a confirmed change.
