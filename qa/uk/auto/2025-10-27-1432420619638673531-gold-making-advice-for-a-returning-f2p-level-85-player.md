# Gold-making advice for a returning F2P level 85 player

**Q:** As a returning F2P level 85 mage, what are good ways to make gold and progress? How long does the Act 5.1 questline take to complete, and does it require good gear?

**A:** Good gold sources at this level include running the Act 5.1 questline, farming ice flowers, and doing IC (Icy Canyon) runs. Fully completing the Act 5.1 questline is recommended since it grants about 23kk gold plus items along with champion-level experience; at level 85 there aren't many other strong gold-earning options available.
