# Do partners use the player's AW/champion weapon level

**Q:** Can partners wield weapons based on the player's AW (champion) level?

**A:** Yes, partners inherit the same AW/champion level as the player, allowing them to use weapons of that level. This level is not visibly displayed on the partner, but it still applies.
