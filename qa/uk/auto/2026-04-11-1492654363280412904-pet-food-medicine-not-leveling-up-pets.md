# Pet food/medicine not leveling up pets

**Q:** Pet food and medicine seem bugged — I used 30 exclusive pet food/medicine items and my pet still isn't leveling up. Is this a bug?

**A:** This is likely not a bug: a pet affected by 'dimensional sickness' will not gain levels from food or medicine while that condition is active. Clear the pet's dimensional sickness status before feeding it to allow leveling to work again.
