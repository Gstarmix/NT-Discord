# Requirements for the Moss Giant Pollutus raid as a mage

**Q:** What should I prepare before joining the Moss Giant Pollutus raid, and which mage specialists are good for it?

**A:** You should be at least combat level 80 with proper equipment to join; at that level Gravity is usable for utility. For a DPS role you need to be above level 90 with good equipment, at which point Seer, Archmage, or Gravity are all viable specialist choices.
