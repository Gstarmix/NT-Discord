# Do developers ever rebalance equipment after release?

**Q:** Why not just give the Swordsman class a flat PvP damage penalty to balance it against classes like Seer, Dagger and Scout, and do developers ever adjust equipment stats after release to fix class balance?

**A:** According to a developer, equipment stats have never been modified after their initial release, only skills receive balance adjustments. Adding ad hoc passive damage penalties per class was rejected as impractical, since it would require reworking many skills for little benefit while other classes (Seer, Dagger/Assassin, Scout) remain dominant in PvP.
