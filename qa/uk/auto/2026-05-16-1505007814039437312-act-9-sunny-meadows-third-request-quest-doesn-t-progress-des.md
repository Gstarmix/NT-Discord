# Act 9 / Sunny Meadows 'Third Request' quest doesn't progress despite having the required item

**Q:** I can't finish the 'Third Request' quest in Act 9 (Sunny Meadows), which requires delivering a Pollutus Core and Mystic Power Crystals to an NPC. Nothing happens when talking to the NPC even though I have the item, and relogging doesn't help. What's wrong?

**A:** This is a known issue: if the required quest item is sitting in your Raid Inventory instead of your normal inventory, the NPC won't register the delivery and the quest appears stuck. The workaround is to move the item out of the Raid Inventory into your regular inventory, after which the quest should progress normally.
