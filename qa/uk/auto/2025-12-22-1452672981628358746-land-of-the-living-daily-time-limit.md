# Land of the Living daily time limit

**Q:** Is there a daily time limit for Land of the Living (LoL), and do you get automatically kicked out after reaching it?

**A:** Land of the Living has a daily limit of about 2 hours plus roughly 10-15 minutes before you are automatically kicked and denied re-entry until the next day. You can check your remaining time for the day via the teleport menu option that shows 'time left on Land of the Living'. In practice this allows about two LoL runs per day.
