# Why do only +0 to +6 rare items stack, not +7/+8?

**Q:** Why can only rare items from +0 to +6 be stacked in the inventory, while +7 and +8 rare items cannot?

**A:** Rare (r) items are only stackable up to +6 so that higher upgraded +7 and +8 rare items keep their individual value, since stacking would make them indistinguishable from lower quality copies.
