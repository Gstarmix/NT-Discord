# Do you need to upgrade the second weapon and how do SL Damage stats stack

**Q:** If I'm upgrading my champion level 65 equipment and only use Staff SPs, do I still need to upgrade my gun, or is it purely for support stats without affecting my damage? If I have SL Damage Stat +10 on my wand and SL Damage Stat +11 on my gun, do these values add up or does only the higher one count? Also, what does the SL Property stat actually give?

**A:** SL Property refers to your element points. Orange text stats (shells) apply to both weapons regardless of which one you actively use, so you should still upgrade your second weapon even if it is only used for its SP-related shells. For SL Damage Stat, only the higher value between your two weapons counts, they do not add up. If your SPs only use the wand, you generally do not need to upgrade the gun's level since optimizations (pluses) on a weapon whose shells your SPs don't use are wasted, a +0 gun is as useful as a +10 one in that case. The inverse also exists: some gun-based SPs (like Firestorm) use the wand's shell, in which case you'd want that support wand's shell to be damage oriented rather than SL oriented.
