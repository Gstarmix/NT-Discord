# TS (Time Space) quests must be completed in order

**Q:** I'm getting a progress error on a mission tied to a Time Space (TS). Can I run all the TS dungeons without doing their associated quests first?

**A:** No, the TS dungeons and their quests must be completed in order; skipping ahead causes the related quest to show a progress/out-of-order error.
