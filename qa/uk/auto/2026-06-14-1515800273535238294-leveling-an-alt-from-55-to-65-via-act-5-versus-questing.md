# Leveling an alt from 55 to 65 via Act 5 versus questing

**Q:** How long does it take to level an alt character from 55 to 65 using Act 5 farming if questing feels too tedious?

**A:** It is recommended to just do the quests instead of avoiding them. The experience required from level 55 to 65 is far greater than from level 1 to 55, so while Act 5 grinding is faster than questing for the 1-55 range, for the 55-65 range it actually takes longer than Act 2 farming would.
