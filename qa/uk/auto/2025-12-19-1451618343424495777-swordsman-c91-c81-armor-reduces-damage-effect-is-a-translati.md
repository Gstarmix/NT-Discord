# Swordsman C91/C81 armor 'reduces damage' effect is a translation issue

**Q:** Is the last effect line on the Swordsman C91 armor (reducing damage to monsters in another dimension) bugged or just a translation problem? (C81 armor shows different wording for what seems like the same effect.)

**A:** This is a known translation issue, not a functional bug: the C91 armor's last line ('reduces damage to all monsters in another dimension by 15%') actually means damage RECEIVED, and it works correctly in-game despite the misleading wording. The C81 armor had the same wording issue before but functions properly. The translation text will be adjusted to clarify it refers to damage received, not damage dealt.
