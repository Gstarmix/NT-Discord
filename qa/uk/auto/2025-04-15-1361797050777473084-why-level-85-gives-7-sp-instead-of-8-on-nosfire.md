# Why level 85 gives 7 SP instead of 8 on NosFire

**Q:** Why do you get only 7 SP for reaching level 85 instead of 8?

**A:** This is not a bug. The 8th SP point comes from a box obtained through the Laurena progress book questline, which normally requires a level 70+ raid. Giving 7 SP at level 85 without requiring a Caligor clear makes sense as a faster, more accessible path compared to relying on that raid.
