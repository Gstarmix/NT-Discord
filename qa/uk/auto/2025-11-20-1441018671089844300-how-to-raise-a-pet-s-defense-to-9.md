# How to raise a pet's defense to +9

**Q:** How is it possible to get +9 defense on a pet?

**A:** Use samurai or pirate pet trainers: place the pet in miniland in S mode and let it wait there while you apply the trainers, using up to 10 trainers at a time to increase its defense stat.
