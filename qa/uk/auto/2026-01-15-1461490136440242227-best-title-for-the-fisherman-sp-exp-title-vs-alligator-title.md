# Best title for the Fisherman SP: EXP title vs Alligator title

**Q:** To complete the Fisherman SP, is it better to keep the EXP-increase title or switch to the "Alligator" title obtained on the Act 7 map? Does the increased catch chance from the Alligator title also apply to rare/big fish?

**A:** Use the EXP-increase title until around job level 45, then switch to the Alligator title (obtained in Spirit Forest, Act 7) for a better chance of catching fish. Whether this increased catch chance specifically applies to rare/big fish was not confirmed by repliers.
