# Spawning unexpectedly after walking into a locked portal

**Q:** Why does the player end up spawning at an unexpected location without having manually moved there?

**A:** This is a known issue: walking into a locked portal can cause the player to spawn at that portal's destination unintentionally, rather than the portal simply blocking movement.
