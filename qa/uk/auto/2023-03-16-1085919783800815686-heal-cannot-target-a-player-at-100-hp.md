# Heal cannot target a player at 100% HP

**Q:** Is it intended that you cannot use a heal skill on a player who already has 100% HP, and consequently cannot use an attack buff on them either?

**A:** Yes, this is intended behavior. If heal could be used on a full HP target, it would let players spam the skill purely to reset its cooldown (referred to as a J20 reset).
