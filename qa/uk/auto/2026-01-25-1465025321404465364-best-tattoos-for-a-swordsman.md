# Best tattoos for a Swordsman

**Q:** What are the best tattoos for a Swordsman, and is the 'Growing Experience' tattoo actually good compared to alternatives like 'Attack Stance' or 'Piercing Gaze'?

**A:** The best tattoo choice depends on whether you play PvE or PvP; for PvE, Attack Stance combined with Piercing Gaze (or alternatively Growing Experience) is recommended. Whether to keep upgrading a current tattoo (e.g. Lion Claws + Growing Experience) or delete/relearn for a better one depends on which is cheaper: deleting and relearning costs around 8kk gold per attempt at Eagle Loa, while a random reroll costs about 7-7.5kk, and a guaranteed selection scroll is listed around 75kk-120kk (price varies with sales). Many players choose to upgrade first and change the tattoo later once cheaper options are available.
