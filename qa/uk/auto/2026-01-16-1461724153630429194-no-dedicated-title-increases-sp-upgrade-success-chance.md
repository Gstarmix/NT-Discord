# No dedicated title increases SP upgrade success chance

**Q:** Is there a title that gives +50% chance for SP upgrade success (e.g. turning a base 5% chance into 7.5%)?

**A:** There is no title that boosts SP upgrade success chance. The only bonus to SP upgrade chance comes from special events, not from any title.
