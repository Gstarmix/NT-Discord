# Does the raid entry reduction effect apply to all limited-entry raids

**Q:** Does the mechanic that reduces required raid entries also work in Glacernon and Draco, or only in Pamon and Erenia?

**A:** It is not limited to Pamon and Erenia: it works on all raids that have a limited number of entries, including Glacernon and Draco.
