# Keeping the free Dark/Light SPs at +9 versus buying better ones early

**Q:** Is upgrading the free Dark/Light SPs to +9 enough for the whole leveling process, or is it better to buy better SPs as soon as possible?

**A:** Opinions on this differ. One view is that the free light SP is fine early on but it's worth buying a better dark SP. Another view is that there is no objectively 'better' option here, it comes down to personal preference. There is no strong consensus on this point.
