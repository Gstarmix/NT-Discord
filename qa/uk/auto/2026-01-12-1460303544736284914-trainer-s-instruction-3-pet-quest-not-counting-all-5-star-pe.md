# Trainer's Instruction 3 pet quest not counting all 5-star pets

**Q:** My pet album shows over 30 pets rated 5 stars or higher, but the 'raise 5-star rating on 75 different pets' quest only shows progress 26/75. Why doesn't it count all my high-star pets, and do pets that start at 3 stars count either?

**A:** The quest only counts pets that were originally 1- or 2-star by default and were then trained up to 5 stars or more. Pets that already start at a higher star rating out of the box (e.g. the Otter, which starts at 5 stars) do not count toward this quest progress, even though they display 5 stars in the album. Pets starting at 3 stars likewise don't count.
