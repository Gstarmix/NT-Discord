# Nezarun/Giga Arma daily quests were not nerfed

**Q:** Were the daily quest EXP rewards from the Nezarun/Giga Arma (Arma 2.0) raids nerfed? One day it gave 2.4% EXP, the next only 1%.

**A:** These daily quests were not nerfed and still give the same experience as before. As reference points, the Nezarune daily gives roughly 2% EXP at character level 90, and the Giga Arma daily gives roughly 2% at level 80. Perceived drops in EXP percentage are explained by the character's own level affecting the displayed percentage, not an actual reduction.
