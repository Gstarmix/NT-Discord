# Best ways to earn gold at low character level (Hero lvl 30)

**Q:** I'm Hero level 30. How can I earn good gold at this stage?

**A:** There's a daily reward you can claim via the big yellow coin icon in the bottom right corner of the screen (choose the biggest option for more gold). At around character level 30 there isn't really a strong gold-farming method yet; your best options are farming ice flowers, farming glace raid seals, farming in Act 7 for Moritius, and doing instant combat. Otherwise, the only fast way is spending real money. If you already have some gold saved up, you can also make profit by buying items cheap and reselling them later, though this requires patience and existing capital.
