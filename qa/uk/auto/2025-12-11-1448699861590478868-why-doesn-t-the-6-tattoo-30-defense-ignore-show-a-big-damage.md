# Why doesn't the +6 tattoo (30% defense ignore) show a big damage increase in PvE?

**Q:** With a +6 tattoo that grants extra critical and ignores 30% of enemy defense, I barely see any damage difference in PvE (only a slight increase in critical damage) when comparing Critical Damage vs Damage Increase options on the same mobs. Is this a bug, since ignoring 30% defense should be noticeably impactful?

**A:** This is not a bug. The tattoo is working correctly — the crit portion clearly increases damage, and the defense-ignore effect exists but its visibility depends on the target's defense value, plus damage floor/ceiling, soft crit, and other buffs interacting with the calculation. On a low-defense mob, ignoring 30% of a small number is barely noticeable, similar to comparing damage on a wolf versus a chicken. The effect becomes much more visible against high-defense targets, such as bosses in the Nezarun/raid maps, so testing should be done there rather than on regular mobs.
