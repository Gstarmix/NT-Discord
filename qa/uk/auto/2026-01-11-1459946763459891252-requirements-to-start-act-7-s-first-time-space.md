# Requirements to start Act 7's first Time-Space

**Q:** What are the requirements to start the first Time-Space of Act 7's main quest? At level 86 (+10 job level) I get a 'cannot be done due to low act' message — do I need to be level 90 and have cleared all Act 5.2 Time-Spaces?

**A:** You need to be class level 10 and have completed all previous Time-Spaces from Act 1 through Act 5 (including the 3 Time-Spaces of Act 5.2) to unlock Act 7. Only character level matters, not job/class level beyond C10 — it can be done before reaching level 90 (one player started it at level 88+C10). Act 5.2's Time-Spaces reportedly only require around level 81, but soloing them at 8x+10 is fairly hard.
