# Why is leveling alts from 65 to 80 via Land of Death (LoD) taking so many more runs than expected?

**Q:** Leveling characters from 65 to 80 through Land of Death (LoD) is taking 8-9 runs for me, while online sources say it should take only 3-5. What could I be doing wrong (using only Tarot and Ancelloan bonuses, no other exp bonus)?

**A:** Result depends on clear method and group composition: the recommended approach is one high level carry character grouped with two low level (alt) characters, clearing back to front and starting with knights. If the carry is only tanking while alts don't land hits (or hit under a level penalty), the alts gain far less experience, since even a single hit lands much better exp than none, though maximum damage isn't required for full exp. For leveling multiple alts to 85 for raids, doing LoD straight from level 65 to 85 is the fastest method (roughly 6 hours with decent clear speed and C65-equivalent gear), since questing takes much longer due to travel time and still requires doing Timespace anyway. At level 81 it's worth doing the Act 4 quest as a shortcut for one level, since it needs to be done regardless for stone and gear. Overall a full LoD leveling session should take at most about 5 hours if done efficiently.
