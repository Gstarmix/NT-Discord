# Do skills get separate PvE and PvP damage multipliers?

**Q:** Do the developers plan to give skills separate damage multipliers for PvE versus PvP content?

**A:** Some debuffs already behave differently in PvP (for example Time Lord's sleep), and some buffs include a PvP-specific bonus damage component. However, there are no plans to create fully custom separate damage multipliers for PvE and PvP across skills in general, as it would be too much extra work.
