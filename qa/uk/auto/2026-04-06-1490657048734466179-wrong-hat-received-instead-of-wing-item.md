# Wrong hat received instead of wing item

**Q:** I received a duplicate hat instead of the new wing item, how can I get it fixed?

**A:** This was a known distribution issue: some players received an extra/duplicate hat instead of the intended wings. The team removed the incorrect hats from affected accounts and manually sent the wings instead. If you still have an extra hat you shouldn't have, or never received your wings replacement, contact staff via DM with your character name and server for a manual fix.
