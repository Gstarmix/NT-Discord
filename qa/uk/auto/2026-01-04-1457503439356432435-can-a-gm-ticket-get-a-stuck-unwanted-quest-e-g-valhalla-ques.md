# Can a GM ticket get a stuck/unwanted quest (e.g. Valhalla questline) deleted?

**Q:** Can I ask a GM via ticket to delete an unwanted quest (specifically the Valhalla questline) from my character?

**A:** No, GMs will not delete quests via ticket, even if the quest has been stuck for years. The only workaround reported by players is to delete the affected character and restart from scratch, though this is a painful option if the character already has significant progress (books, titles, pets, etc.).
