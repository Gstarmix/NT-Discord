# Best leveling map before level 60 and how does LoL (Land of Oblivion) work?

**Q:** As an h29 swordsman, what is the best map to level up until h60? How does LoL work — can you enter whenever you want, and can you bring buff characters along?

**A:** Polluted Path is the best leveling map until around level 30 (c30), after which LoL becomes worthwhile — it levels roughly twice as fast as Polluted Path. LoL sessions last 2h10 and can be entered at any time except on odd hours, when Asgobas bosses spawn and it closes. Buff characters can be brought along once they reach c30, but unlike LoD there is no protected safe spot for them, so it's best to do LoL with family/guild members who can watch over them. LoL works like LoD in a group of 6, where everyone needs to hit the mobs to get full experience; most players use Obstinate (OBS) SP during it, and running IC (Icy Castle) hero mode also gives a lot of experience.
