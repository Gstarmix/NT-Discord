# Why Attack Stance can outperform Strong Attack in PvE

**Q:** Why is Attack Stance (percentage damage) sometimes better than Strong Attack (flat attack power) in PvE content?

**A:** It depends on your total attack power: there is a threshold beyond which percentage-based damage (Attack Stance) becomes better than flat attack power. For example, considering +10 tattoos, once your attack power without the tattoo exceeds roughly 2500, Attack Stance will always outperform Strong Attack. Since the SP stat 'Attack Stance' gives a flat 1000 attack at level 100, reaching that threshold is not very hard.
