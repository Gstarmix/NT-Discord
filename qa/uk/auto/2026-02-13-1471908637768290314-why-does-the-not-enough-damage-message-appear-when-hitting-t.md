# Why does the 'not enough damage' message appear when hitting the Asgard Boss (Asgo)

**Q:** Why does the game keep showing a 'damage too low' (4x insufficient damage) message when hitting the Asgo boss almost continuously, even though attacks are landing?

**A:** This is not a bug. When too many strong damage dealers are attacking the boss at once, individual hits on the boss itself can be capped/insufficient and trigger the 'not enough damage' message, while the mobs around the boss still take full damage. The suggested approach is to focus on killing the surrounding mobs instead of the boss directly, which makes clearing the encounter easier.
