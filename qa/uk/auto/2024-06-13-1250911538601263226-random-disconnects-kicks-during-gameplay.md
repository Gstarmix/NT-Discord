# Random disconnects (kicks) during gameplay

**Q:** Why does the game randomly kick me out for no apparent reason?

**A:** NosTale's client is very sensitive to connection stability: even a very brief (0.1 second) drop in the internet connection can trigger a disconnect. Server location/latency also has a significant impact on how often this occurs.
