# Choosing between champion level 81, 86 and 91 mage armor bonuses

**Q:** For mage champion-level armors, the c86 piece reduces soft crit (damage from critical hits) but not crit chance, while c81 reduces crit chance but not soft crit, and c91 also lacks soft crit reduction (only c99 has both). Is the c86 soft-crit-reduction bonus better than c81's crit-chance-reduction, is it mainly useful for PvP, and does it make more sense to use a decent c81 armor until reaching c91 and then switch to something built for PvE/PvP/PvX? Also, is it worth upgrading to c86 for mass PvP until a proper c97 PvP armor is available, and do players prefer %defense or effects/all resistance on their armor?

**A:** One player runs the effect slot with some defense stat on the armor rather than pure %def. The general recommendation from other players is not to over-invest in the c81/c86 armors: instead get a decent level 91 PvE armor with enhanced defense and an S-anti-element effect, then later transcend/upgrade that same armor to level 99, which is considered more reasonable long-term than chasing intermediate armors. No definitive answer was given on whether c86's soft-crit reduction outweighs c81's crit-chance reduction.
