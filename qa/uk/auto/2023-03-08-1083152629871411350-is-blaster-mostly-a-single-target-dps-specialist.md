# Is Blaster mostly a single target DPS specialist?

**Q:** Is the Blaster specialist effectively a single target DPS build rather than a true AoE one?

**A:** Blaster's area skill only covers a 1 square radius around the enemy, so in practice it behaves like a single target damage dealer when fighting alone, and it becomes cramped and less effective in team content where multiple specialists compete for the same small area.
