# Why a 5-star pet can't be placed in a pet bead

**Q:** Why can't I put my black ink rabbit into a pet bead even after resetting its level, despite it being 5 star?

**A:** This is likely because the pet was obtained as a limited/event pet. Limited pets cannot be placed into pet beads even if they are high star rank or have their level reset.
