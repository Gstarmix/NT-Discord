# Natural Heal skill does not work while training pets in miniland

**Q:** Is it a bug that the Natural Heal skill does not work?

**A:** Natural Heal does work normally, but it is disabled while training pets in your miniland, because doing so puts the character in a combat state, and Natural Heal cannot activate while in combat.
