# Fairy reset kit does nothing after confirming

**Q:** When using a fairy reset kit to lower a fairy's level by 1, the confirmation popup appears and gets confirmed, but nothing happens afterward. Is this a known bug?

**A:** This is a known issue that affects several item confirmation popups, not just the fairy reset kit. As a workaround, wait a few seconds before confirming and click the confirm button instead of pressing Enter; if the problem persists, record a video and report it for further investigation.
