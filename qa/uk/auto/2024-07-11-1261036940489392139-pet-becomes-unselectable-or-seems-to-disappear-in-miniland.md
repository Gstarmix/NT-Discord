# Pet becomes unselectable or seems to disappear in miniland

**Q:** Is there a bug where a pet (e.g. a baby panda) is visible in miniland but cannot be selected or found by clicking it?

**A:** This is a known issue that occurs when a mount is used in miniland and the active pet is then swapped. Relogging fixes it and makes the pet selectable/visible again.
