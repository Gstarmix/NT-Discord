# TS57 Hero blocked after completing TS57 1/2

**Q:** I completed TS57 1/2 but now the game says I can't start TS57 Hero because I missed a Time Space. Why wasn't this flagged earlier, when I finished TS57 1/2?

**A:** This usually happens when you picked up the quest items for TS56 but abandoned that Time Space without finishing it, breaking the quest chain. The fix is to redo TS56, starting from Western Kriemhild, to unlock TS57 Hero again.
