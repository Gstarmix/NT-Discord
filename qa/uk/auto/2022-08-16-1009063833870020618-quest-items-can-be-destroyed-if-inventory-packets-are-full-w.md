# Quest items can be destroyed if inventory/packets are full when accepted

**Q:** What happens if you accept a quest reward item while your inventory or packet slots are full?

**A:** If your inventory (or packets) is full when you accept a quest item, the item is destroyed and the game shows a message warning you about it. This has reportedly happened to players even across server merges, and recovering the lost quest item afterward may require opening a support ticket.
