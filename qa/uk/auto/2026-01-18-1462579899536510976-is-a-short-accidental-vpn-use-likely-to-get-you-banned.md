# Is a short accidental VPN use likely to get you banned?

**Q:** VPN use is against the rules, but I was only connected through one for about 5 minutes by accident. Should I worry about getting permanently banned for this?

**A:** VPN use is indeed against the rules, but enforcement mainly targets players who use one constantly (e.g. 24/7). A brief, accidental VPN connection is unlikely to cause a ban on its own, unless it was accompanied by unusual account activity or suspicious transactions.
