# Should you rush Act 5 or grind exp instead?

**Q:** In mid Act 5, should I rush through the story acts as fast as possible or start normal leveling by grinding first? Can I skip Act 5 and go straight to Act 6?

**A:** Act 5 is not considered particularly rewarding, so it is recommended to skip it and instead grind EXP in LoD until character level 85, then start Act 6 while exping and farming gold at the same time. After finishing the EXP grind in each zone, you can go back and do the zone's quests to obtain free equipment.
