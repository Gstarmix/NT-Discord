# Chocolate Bunny event raid: overlapping carrot spawns block a portal

**Q:** In the Beast Bloat / Chocolate Bunny raid event, when two carrot spawn points land on the same spot, one of the two portals cannot be activated. Is this a known issue, and when was it introduced?

**A:** This is a known, long-standing bug, not something introduced by the event itself: it has reportedly existed since 2015. When two carrot spawn points overlap on the same tile, one of the two associated portals becomes impossible to activate. No fix or workaround was given, and its age was cited as a reason it may not be prioritized.
