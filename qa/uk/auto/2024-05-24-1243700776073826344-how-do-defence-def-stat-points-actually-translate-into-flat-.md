# How do defence (def) stat points actually translate into flat defence and dodge?

**Q:** If a weapon gives +11 to a defence stat property, why doesn't the P (character) window show a full matching increase in flat defence, and why does switching between two copies of the same weapon change alldef by a small amount?

**A:** 11 points in a defence stat give 12 flat defence and 5 dodge, not a 1:1 bonus, so the P window increase looks smaller than expected. Separately, having 20 HP or MP stat points also grants 10 flat defence, which is why the difference between weapons can look inconsistent. Note that SL (skill level) stats are not the same as perfection stats: only perfection points give 10 flat defence per defence stat point, SL stats do not work the same way.
