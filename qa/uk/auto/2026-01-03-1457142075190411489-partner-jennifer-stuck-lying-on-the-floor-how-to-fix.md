# Partner (Jennifer) stuck lying on the floor - how to fix

**Q:** My partner Jennifer is stuck lying on the floor as a bug. How can I fix this?

**A:** This usually happens because the partner's revivals ran out, which keeps them on the ground for about 3 minutes. Try mounting up, and if that doesn't help, relogging or switching to a different partner should resolve it.
