# Gameforge client stuck on endless sign-in screen

**Q:** My Gameforge client broke and stayed unresponsive for about 15 minutes. After reinstalling it, it got stuck on an endless sign-in loop. How can this be fixed?

**A:** As of May 2025 this appears to be a known intermittent Gameforge client issue. Possible causes reported by players include a temporary soft lock (roughly 24h) triggered by too many login attempts, or a temporary problem on Gameforge's servers/network. Workarounds that have worked: force-closing the Gameforge client via Task Manager and relaunching it, or fully reinstalling the Gameforge client and the game (in one case it took about 10 reinstall attempts before login worked again).
