# What is soft crit and how does it differ from a normal crit?

**Q:** What is soft crit in NosTale? Where does it come from, and how is it different from a regular crit or the orange bonus stats shown on weapons?

**A:** A normal crit is simply a critical hit. Soft damage is the effect described as increasing damage by a percentage with a certain chance, this is the orange bonus shown on weapons. Soft crit specifically refers to a soft damage proc landing at the same time as a crit hit, not soft damage in general. The soft crit damage reduction stat introduced on some Act 10 gear exists mainly for PvP purposes, since monsters do not deal soft damage, so this stat has no effect on mages, who cannot land crits at all.
