# Does tattoo oil increase all damage or only physical damage?

**Q:** Does using the tattoo (permanent damage augment oil) give a 100% chance to increase damage, or does it affect all damage types?

**A:** At tattoo level 0, the oil gives a 100% chance to increase damage by 35%, but this bonus only applies to physical attack damage, not to all damage types.
