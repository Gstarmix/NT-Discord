# Why can't I post a job on the market Discord channel?

**Q:** When trying to post a job/thread on the market channel, I get a message saying I don't have permission. Why?

**A:** Posting new threads on the market channel requires being at least level 5, while commenting/replying on existing threads requires level 10.
