# Trade window shows wrong items and can cause item loss on accept

**Q:** During a trade where the other player put 10 items in the trade window, the items displayed on my side were wrong (showing an unrelated item, like a Berserk SP card). After both sides accepted, items were exchanged incorrectly: I received my trade partner's items, but they did not receive the items I had put in, even though both of us saw the correct items in the window before accepting.

**A:** Known issue: the trade window can visually display incorrect items during a trade, which is not just a display glitch. Confirmed by multiple players, accepting such a trade can result in one side losing their items while the other side keeps both their own and the received items, effectively causing an item duplication/loss bug. Report affected trades to staff; no workaround to prevent it is known yet, so caution is advised when trading large numbers of items.
