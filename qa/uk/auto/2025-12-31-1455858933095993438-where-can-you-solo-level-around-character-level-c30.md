# Where can you solo level around character level C30?

**Q:** Where can a character around C30 level up solo, and is soloing actually viable there?

**A:** The Polluted Path is a good leveling spot usable solo up to around C53. You'll gain more experience per hour in a duo than solo, but soloing is confirmed to be possible there, including as a Mage.
