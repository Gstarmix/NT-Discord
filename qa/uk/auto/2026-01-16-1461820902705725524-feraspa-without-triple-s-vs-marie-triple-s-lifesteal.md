# Feraspa without triple S vs Marie triple S lifesteal

**Q:** Is Feraspa worth using without triple S rank? Would a triple-S Marie be better because of her lifesteal?

**A:** Feraspa is decent for cexp even without triple S. Marie's triple-S lifesteal effect is not very impactful (only around 200 healing per skill use); her real value comes from the healing granted by her first skill's buff rather than from the lifesteal stat.
