# Mage weapon upgrade path before champion level 65

**Q:** As a mage, should I upgrade from the champion level 25 to the champion level 45 equipment or wand (for the heal buff), or should I just wait for the champion level 65 equipment? Is there a better option?

**A:** The champion level 45 wand is considered a top tier item for mages, not for its damage but for the heal buff it provides. You should keep using it (hitting with it to get the buff) all the way until champion level 99 for experience farming, and wait for the champion level 65 equipment before making your next real upgrade.
