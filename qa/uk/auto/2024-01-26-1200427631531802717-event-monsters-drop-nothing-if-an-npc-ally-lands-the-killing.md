# Event monsters drop nothing if an NPC/ally lands the killing blow

**Q:** Why do some Lunar New Year event monsters (e.g. the dragon fought in Act 6.1) sometimes give no drops even after dying?

**A:** If an NPC or ally deals the finishing blow to an event monster instead of the player (e.g. an NPC killing the dragon in Act 6.1 while the player never lands a hit), the monster drops nothing. Players need to deal the killing damage themselves to receive loot from these event mobs; this also makes effects that reduce a monster's HP to 0 prematurely (rather than a normal kill) risk voiding the drop.
