# Raid join confirmation popup can overlap the recruitment list

**Q:** When trying to join a raid team from the raid recruitment list, the 'do you want to join?' confirmation popup can overlap the list and block clicking, and doesn't always disappear. Is it supposed to always appear on a fixed side?

**A:** You can press Enter to accept the join confirmation instead of clicking it. The popup is intended to appear at whatever position your cursor was when you clicked (not fixed to one side of the screen), and moving your mouse away from the raid entry in the list should make it disappear.
