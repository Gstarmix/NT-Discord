# Is it worth raising SP attack to 115?

**Q:** Is it worth investing SP points to raise attack to 115, or is it better to stop at 100 and put the rest into element?

**A:** There is no meaningful benefit at 115. If you push attack further, 110 is a reasonable stopping point since it lines up with a breakpoint; the next real breakpoint after that is 120, not 115.
