# How long does it take to level from C10 to C53 without a LoL family?

**Q:** How many hours does it take to level a character from C10 to C53 without playing in a LoL (Land of Oblivion) family?

**A:** Leveling solo/without a dedicated LoL family takes a long time: roughly 12-15 hours to go from C10 to C30, but experience gains shrink drastically afterward. Past C40 you gain around 30% of a level per hour, and near the level cap only about 10% per hour, so the last stretch is very slow and not recommended. Finding a family for LoL is much faster, giving roughly 1-2 levels per hour on average.
