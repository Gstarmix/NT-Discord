# How does the Flying Fire Devil (Lord of Death raid) aggro work?

**Q:** How does the Flying Fire Devil's aggro work in the Lord of Death raid? Alts kept dying to it even while hiding in the corner near Skull Passage. Is there a counter, like placing a tank in the middle of the spawn?

**A:** The Flying Fire Devil always targets whoever entered the map last, regardless of where that character is standing. Hiding in the corner near Skull Passage doesn't protect against it, that positioning trick only works against the Revenant Skeleton Warriors, not the Flying Fire Devil. The reliable fix is to always send the tank into the map last, so the Flying Fire Devil locks onto the tank on spawn and leaves the alts alone.
