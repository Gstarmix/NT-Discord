# Time Space: "gate is locked" / stuck unable to leave after finishing the mission

**Q:** In a Time Space raid, after completing the mission, both characters got stuck: one couldn't leave with either the swordsman or the archer, portals showed as locked for everyone despite looking open, and the run ended up marked as finished without ever actually leaving. What causes this?

**A:** This is a known issue: the Time Space leave button is unreliable, especially when running in a group, sometimes leaving players unable to exit even though portals appear open. It may also relate to only the character who picked up the required artifact being able to leave, or to a client desync on one of the characters. When this happens, the run can end up marked as completed on the server without the player ever actually leaving.
