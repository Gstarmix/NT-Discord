# How does the SP +20 upgrade buff work?

**Q:** How exactly does the +20 SP card buff work — is it just a low-percent chance to summon a clone dealing 70% damage, and is it worth the price over a +18 card?

**A:** The buff gives a chance to summon a clone dealing a portion of your damage; in PvE this actually procs quite often rather than being rare. Upgrading also raises the maximum stat points you can allocate: base cap is 100 at +15, then 105 at +16, 110 at +17, 115 at +18, and 120 at +19/+20 (you need a high enough upgrade level to place points above 100). On top of that, each element gives a different secondary buff — e.g. fire gives 10 resistance reduction and 7% damage — and for PvE you generally want your card's element to match your build for the resistance reduction. Whether +20 is worth it over +18 comes down to price versus the extra stat cap and buff strength.
