# Content comparison between Act 9 and Act 10

**Q:** How does the amount of new content in Act 10 compare to Act 9?

**A:** Act 9 introduced 14 new maps, drones, a new enhancing system, 2 raids with 2 prerooms each, fairy timespaces 1 and 2, and 10 mainline timespaces plus 2 for secondary/daily quests. Act 10 introduces 12 new maps, 2 new raids with mechanics revamped from past raids, and 24 new timespaces, including 3 new farmable tokens dropped from map bosses. Overall Act 10 is considered lighter in new systems than Act 9, since new equips, jewels, the token system, and the +11 to +13 enhancement levels are not counted as genuinely new content; some expect Act 10.2 to add more new content later, following the pattern of previous Acts.
