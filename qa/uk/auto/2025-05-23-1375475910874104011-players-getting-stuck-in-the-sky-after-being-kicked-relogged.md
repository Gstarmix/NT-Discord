# Players getting stuck in the sky after being kicked/relogged during a raid

**Q:** After being kicked for inactivity or relogging, players got stuck floating in the sky instead of returning to the correct location. Is this a known issue?

**A:** This was a known, widespread issue at the time — many players in the same instance reported being stuck. Relogging teleports the character to the Citadel, but re-entering through the portal from there leads back to being stuck in the sky again, so there was no full workaround.
