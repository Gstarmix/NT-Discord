# How to gain Discord level for bazaar/comment access

**Q:** How can I earn experience in the Discord server to unlock chat permissions (e.g. Bazaar chat), and what are the level requirements for posting, commenting, and reacting?

**A:** Discord level is gained simply by chatting; there was a period with triple XP active, and reaching level 5 is fairly quick. Level 5 is required to create a post, level 10 to reply/edit, and level 20 to use reactions — the same requirements apply to the Dragonveil bazaar channel. Alternatively, becoming a server booster grants the needed permission directly; search 'rank' in the channel and check the GM/mod pinned post for details.
