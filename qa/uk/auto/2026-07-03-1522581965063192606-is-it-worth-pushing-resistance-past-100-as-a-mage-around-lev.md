# Is it worth pushing resistance past 100% as a mage around level 85?

**Q:** As an 85+1 mage combining Valakus' and Kertos' effects with Inferno and Paradise hero equipment, I can reach around 90% resistance on all elements. Is it worth switching to Nezarun equipment to push toward 100-110% resistance for experience farming and raids, given that some mobs/bosses may apply resistance-lowering effects?

**A:** For PvE leveling at that level, resistance should not be overthought, mage already has solid base resistance from its kit and buffs. Maritime gear (Sea View gloves and boots, farmed and crafted in Act 5.1) with an S6 rune is enough for leveling; adding an armor with an S resistance shell covers the rest if needed. It is true that higher-level content (around level 60+) has mobs that drain around 20 resistance, which is why players aim for 100+ resistance there, but that threshold is irrelevant at 85+1 and not worth gearing around yet. A stuff simulator (nostar.fr combine tool) can be used to test different resistance combinations before committing.
