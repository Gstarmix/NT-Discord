# Why is there such a big HP/dodge gap between certain c-levels?

**Q:** At higher c-levels (e.g. fighting a c99 as a c85), there seems to be a huge HP gap (around 100k) between consecutive c-levels, and dodge appears to stack very high on some classes. Is this intended, and is the resulting hit/miss rate normal?

**A:** Players confirm there is indeed a large HP jump specifically between c37 and c38. On top of that, the Blade class can reportedly stack up to +470 dodge, which combined with a c28 armor using high-dodge shells can make a lower-level attacker miss up to around 15 times in a row. This kind of large swing was described as intentional in part to make the early 1-25 level range less painful, though opinions differed on whether it's specifically a c-level design issue.
