# Playing with alts / multi-accounts and NosMall prices on the Steam version

**Q:** Why do some players say the Steam version of NosTale has issues with multi-accounting and NosMall prices, when they don't personally experience problems using it alone?

**A:** On the Steam version it is harder to play with multiple accounts (alts) compared to the standalone client, and NosMall currency is more expensive there. Some players consider having multiple accounts (multi/alts) necessary to make good money in the game.
