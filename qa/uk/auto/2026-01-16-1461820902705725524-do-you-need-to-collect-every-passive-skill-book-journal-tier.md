# Do you need to collect every passive skill book (journal) tier in order?

**Q:** Do you need to obtain each passive skill book (journal) tier individually to progress to higher tiers, or can some be skipped (e.g. skip tier 1 and go straight to tier 2 at c48+)?

**A:** You cannot skip tiers of passive skill books/journals — every tier must be obtained to progress to the next one, and you need S tier (or temporarily A tier during events via cheap green tickets) to move forward. Don't reset a book if you already rolled double S. For older, cheaper journals it's usually best to just buy them outright; for newer ones (Marie and later) it can be cheaper to obtain them yourself.
