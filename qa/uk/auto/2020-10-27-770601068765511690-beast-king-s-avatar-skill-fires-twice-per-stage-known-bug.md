# Beast King's Avatar skill fires twice per stage (known bug)

**Q:** Is it a known issue that using the Beast King's Avatar makes it perform its skill more than once per stage, and that its CDR buff is missing?

**A:** As of this report, it is a known issue that the Beast King's Avatar's CDR buff does not apply. Additionally, when summoned the avatar can perform its skill twice on the first stage entered, then stops using the skill entirely on subsequent stages. Logging out and back in resets this behavior (it will again perform the skill twice on the next stage before stopping). No permanent fix was available at the time.
