# How to recognize official staff behavior and how bot reports are handled

**Q:** How can players recognize scam attempts impersonating official team members, and how are botting reports processed?

**A:** Official team members will never send a friend request, contact a player first via DM, offer gifts, refer players to non official websites, or ask for login information. No account related support is provided on Discord (a third party platform); such requests must go through the official support system instead. When reporting suspected botters, note that even with evidence, reports (including bot reports) take longer to resolve because staff still needs to check them manually.
