# Why do h50 players feel much stronger than an h29 character?

**Q:** I'm h29 and I feel that h50 players are way stronger than me. Is the difference only due to skill books?

**A:** The main gap is equipment rather than just books: going from c38 all-attack gear to c48-tier gear plus the associated skill books can account for roughly +130 attack difference. However, this gap is not permanent, as it can be closed relatively easily by using shells to upgrade equipment.
