# Friend list does not show online friends

**Q:** The in-game friend list stops showing friends as online, even after repairing game files.

**A:** This typically happens when a player has too many friends on their list; once a certain threshold is passed, only friends who are online in the same channel will show as online. Removing some friends from the list can fix the display.
