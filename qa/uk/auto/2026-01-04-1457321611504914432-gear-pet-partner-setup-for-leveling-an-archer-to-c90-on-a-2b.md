# Gear/pet/partner setup for leveling an Archer to C90 on a 2b budget

**Q:** As an Archer at C46 with a 2 billion gold budget, what costume, pet, partner, resistance gear, title, and hat/mask should I get just for leveling to C90?

**A:** A solid budget setup is: Demon Hunter title level 15+, decent C60 equipment progressing to C80, Christmas wings, a Panda or Santa Claws pet for exp, all-60 resistance boots/shoes (e.g. C40 beast gloves + C28 shoes with 60/60/60/60), a good title (e.g. crit chance), an SSS partner card like Marie/Elf or Forgeron, and Godlike/Legendary Hero + Mystic buffs. A costume isn't required at this budget - cheaper options like a Dino costume, a random C45 dagger for healing, and a Laser costume weapon work fine, with the honey bee set covering hat/mask/costume/wings. Weapons can go C45 until C60 then up to C80, and armor C48 until C58 or C65.
