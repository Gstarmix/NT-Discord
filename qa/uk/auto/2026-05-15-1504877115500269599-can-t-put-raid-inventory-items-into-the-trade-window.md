# Can't put raid inventory items into the trade window

**Q:** Why can't I place items from my Raid Inventory into the trade window when trading with another player?

**A:** This is not a bug. The trade system was never designed/requested to support trading items directly from the Raid Inventory, so such items must be moved to the normal inventory before they can be traded.
