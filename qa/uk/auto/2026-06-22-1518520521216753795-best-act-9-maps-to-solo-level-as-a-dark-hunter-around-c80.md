# Best Act 9 maps to solo level as a Dark Hunter around c80

**Q:** Approaching character level 80, what are the best Act 9 maps to solo level as a Dark Hunter? A single map only gives around 5-7% exp per hour even with SP +20 and exp buffs, is that normal, and is grouping better?

**A:** Most Act 9 maps work for leveling. Invaded Mines is the easiest for solo leveling but gives low exp, around 5-7% per hour. If strong enough, Upper Labs or Supply Routes give similar rates solo. Leveling in a duo (10-12%/h) or in LoL (around 15%/h) is noticeably faster, and grouping in a duo or trio also splits costs and clears more mobs for drops and drone exp.
