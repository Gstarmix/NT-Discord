# Does Manashield increase or reduce damage taken (Mage class)?

**Q:** Will Manashield be removed/reworked for mages, and is its effect confusingly worded?

**A:** Manashield actually reduces the damage a mage receives, not increases it, but its tooltip wording has historically been confusing enough that many players (including on the international server) believed it worked the opposite way and increased incoming damage. There is no confirmation of an imminent removal, only that the topic could be revisited later.
