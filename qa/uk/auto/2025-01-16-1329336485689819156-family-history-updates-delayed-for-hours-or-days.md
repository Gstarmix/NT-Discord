# Family History updates delayed for hours or days

**Q:** Family History entries are not updating immediately, sometimes taking hours or days to appear for all family members. How can this be fixed?

**A:** This is a known issue caused by overly long family history messages breaking the update. Sending short, one line daily quotes tends to fix it, and avoiding long daily quotes prevents it from breaking again. Other overly long history messages, not just daily quotes, may also trigger the same problem.
