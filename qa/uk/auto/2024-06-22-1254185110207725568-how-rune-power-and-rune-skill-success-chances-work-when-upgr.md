# How rune power and rune skill success chances work when upgrading runes

**Q:** When upgrading a rune, is it guaranteed to get a rune power on levels like 6? What are the actual success chances for rune power versus rune skill on each rune level, since the in-game scroll descriptions seem confusing or outdated (e.g. level 5 shows 45% + 30%, level 6 shows 25% + 5%)?

**A:** Getting a rune power is not guaranteed on every applicable level, it is a separate chance shown in the '?' popup of the rune upgrading window. The base chance to receive a rune power on levels like 3, 6, 9, etc. is 95%, and rune scrolls increase that chance by an additional 5%. The current in-game text describing these percentages is known to be unclear or partly incorrect, and a request has been made to have it clarified and fixed.
