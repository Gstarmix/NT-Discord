# Martial Artist cannot complete the SP5/SP6 amulet quest

**Q:** Can a Martial Artist complete the SP5 and SP6 amulet quests, given the class has no SP6 available?

**A:** Martial Artists can complete the SP5 amulet quest, since it has no requirement to deal damage inside the time space. However, SP6 currently cannot be completed by Martial Artists, because that quest requires dealing damage with an SP6 specialist card inside the time space, and the class has no SP6 to use.
