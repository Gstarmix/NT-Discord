# Job level requirements for the free SP questline

**Q:** What are the job level requirements to progress through the questline that grants a free specialist card, including the step needed for SP3?

**A:** The free SP questline becomes available starting at job level 50, and the subsequent step required to reach SP3 needs job level 55. Repliers noted that attempting content like Land of Death with an unupgraded (0+0) specialist card is not advisable.
