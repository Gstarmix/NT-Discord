# Which tattoos are worth investing in for an archer

**Q:** As an archer around level 87+14, should I invest in a tattoo, and if so which one should I get?

**A:** Tattoos are worth investing in long term, but early on you should not heavily invest since tattoo type is RNG (there is an item to reroll, itself RNG, plus a rarer and more expensive selective reroll item). For archers, aim for the Lion side (Attack Stance is the meta pick for percent attack increase, with Lion Claw, Elemental Blessing, and Strong Attack as decent alternatives) and the Eagle side (Piercing Gaze is best for early to mid game thanks to crit damage and crit chance, while Growing Experience is more of a late game pick since it stacks per crit hit). Whatever tattoo you get, upgrade it to at least +5 (ideally +7, with +9 reserved for heavy spenders) since upgrade levels carry over when you later switch tattoos with a reroll item.
