# Priority upgrades when leveling a character from c30 to c60+

**Q:** What are the most important upgrades to prioritize while leveling a character from c30 to c60+, assuming decent current gear (c25/28, c60 xbow saved, zerk +11, Otter 6*, Yuna B with S heal, creepy monk, laser skin, 100% all res via fairy affinity/resistances/armor)? Should I save for a better zerk, get a shadow drone, save toward a baby panda, or just keep the gold?

**A:** Make sure both character tattoos are at least +5. A zerk around 99-100+11 is generally enough to reach 100 attack when combined with attack SL on your gear, and your current all-res setup should hold up until roughly c60-c80. It's worth upgrading your partner (e.g. to Perti/Forga/Feraspa) for better cexp, or alternatively saving your gold for good equipment at c65 (skip c60 gear and go straight for c65). For the shadow drone: if you already have around 80% fairy affinity you can skip it for now, otherwise it's worth getting early since it gives 3 res drain and 1 SL property (though it doesn't give exp). You also need to collect every passive skill book tier to progress properly.
