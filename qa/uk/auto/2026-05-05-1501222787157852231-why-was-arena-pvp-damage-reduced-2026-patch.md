# Why was arena PvP damage reduced (2026 patch)

**Q:** What is the reason behind Gameforge's change to reduce damage dealt in the arena?

**A:** Staff explained the change is meant to address certain class and gear combinations (data pointed especially at mages stacking damage reduction) that allowed near invincibility in arena PvP, which was not intended. Reducing the damage value was chosen because it is an easy, quickly reversible lever, unlike other proposed fixes which would take more time to implement. The change is not guaranteed to be permanent, it could be reverted or adjusted again depending on future balance patches, and players were asked to try it for a longer period before giving feedback.
