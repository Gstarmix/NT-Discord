# Act 4 raid drops rarely double-drop even with loot sharing

**Q:** Is it a bug or a recent change that item drops in Act 4 raids (e.g. Purrcival, Rainbow Cloud) almost never double-drop for group members anymore, even with loot sharing enabled?

**A:** This is expected behavior, not a bug or a recent change: most Act 4 raid drops cannot be duplicated across group members regardless of loot sharing. The main known exceptions that can still double-drop are equipment and shells.
