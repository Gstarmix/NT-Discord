# Best value resistance gear for Act 8-10 and A9 raid access as a c90 berserker

**Q:** What are the most cost-effective resistance options for Act 8-10, and does a c90 berserker have a real chance to join Act 9 raids?

**A:** For Act 8/9 there are only two resistance options: PvE resistances crafted from raid-dropped horns/cores (not tradable), and PvP resistances obtained from boxes — no other sources exist. For Act 10, all resistances (PvE and PvP) can be crafted through raids; the A10 PvP resistances are tradable and drop directly from raids but can only be upgraded/summed with copies of themselves, and you need a higher character level to wear them. If you play mostly PvE, investing in these resistances is worthwhile, especially the Act 10 ones as your character level increases. A c90 berserker can join daily Act 9 raids without trouble; access to Mara raids depends on your gear/SP setup.
