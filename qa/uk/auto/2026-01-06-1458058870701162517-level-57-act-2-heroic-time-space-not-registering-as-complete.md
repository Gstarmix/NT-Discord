# Level 57 Act 2 heroic Time-Space not registering as completed

**Q:** I followed the correct step order (per NosAPki) for the level 57 heroic Time-Space [2] but the game still shows some Time-Space Stones as not completed. Is this a bug?

**A:** This is usually not a client bug: the character info panel shows individual heroic TS entries as [1/2] or [2/2], meaning one of the two required objectives in that Time-Space wasn't fully finished. Open the Act > Time-Space completion list, check which entry is stuck at [1/2], and redo that specific Time-Space.
