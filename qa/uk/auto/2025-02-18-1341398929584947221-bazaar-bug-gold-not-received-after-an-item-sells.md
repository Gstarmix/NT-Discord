# Bazaar bug: gold not received after an item sells

**Q:** After an item sells on the bazaar, the gold from the sale is not received. Restarting the game and reinstalling the client did not fix it.

**A:** This is a known bazaar bug. The suggested fix is to list a random item at any price on the bazaar; this normally resolves the issue. If it doesn't, try listing a few more items and then removing them again. If the problem persists after that and a client repair doesn't help either, open a support ticket.
