# Skillbar equipment slots randomly swap with inventory items

**Q:** Certain equipment swap slots in my skillbar keep randomly swapping with random gear from my inventory. What triggers this and how can I avoid it?

**A:** This is a known issue: it happens when you pick up equipment or other wearable items from the NosBazaar while your character is transformed via Specialist (SP). Avoid withdrawing gear/wearables from the bazaar while transformed to prevent the skillbar slots from swapping unexpectedly.
