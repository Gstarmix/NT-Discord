# Fastest way to farm event seeds: raiding or grinding

**Q:** Is it faster or better to farm event seeds by grinding maps directly, or by farming raid tokens and converting them to seeds through raids, especially without a maxed drop rate setup (no Purrcival, only a Super Fortune Bushi and a double drop minipet)?

**A:** Grinding directly for seeds is considered better than converting via raid tokens. It helps to party up and split across three different maps, teleporting to each other to cover more ground. Also keep in mind that event weekends sometimes offer a 2x seed drop bonus, which is worth timing farming around.
