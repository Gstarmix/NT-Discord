# How PSP buff grade (S/A/B/...) is determined

**Q:** How do you get a high-level buff grade (e.g. S) on a PSP like Yuna? Is it purely luck, and is there a way to reroll the buff grade like you can with skills using a ticket?

**A:** The buff grade is the sum of the grades of the individual skills, where each skill grade has a numeric value (F to S corresponds to values 1 through 7). For example, reaching a B buff requires the summed value of all skills to total 15, achievable through various skill grade combinations that add up to that number (all B, or A+C+B, etc.). This is community-derived knowledge, not something officially documented.
