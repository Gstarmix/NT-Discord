# Bar (FP) can only be filled by healing below full HP or basic attacks

**Q:** If everyone in the party is already at full HP, is it true that you can't fill your resource bar except through basic attacks?

**A:** Yes, if there is nobody below full HP to heal, the bar cannot be filled through healing, basic attacks remain the only alternative way to build it. This is not seen as a major issue in practice, since most players don't rely on that resource (FP) anyway.
