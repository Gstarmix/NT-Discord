# Melon items disappearing from inventory after disconnect in raid

**Q:** Melons (raid currency/items) sometimes vanish from the inventory (e.g. had 15, then they were gone). Is this a known bug?

**A:** Known issue as of July 2025: melons can disappear from your inventory if you disconnect while inside the raid. The suggested workaround is to submit a support ticket to request a refund of the lost items.
