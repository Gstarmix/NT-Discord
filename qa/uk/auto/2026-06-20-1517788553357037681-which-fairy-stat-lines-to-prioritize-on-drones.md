# Which fairy stat lines to prioritize on drones

**Q:** Is a fairy stat that increases the equipped fairy's element by 24 good, and what stat priority should I look for on drones?

**A:** For physical damage classes (sword, archer, martial artist) the priority is % damage increase, then critical chance, then enhanced stat, then element/fairy bonus. For mage, the priority is % damage increase, then enhanced stat, then element/fairy bonus, meaning a pure element boost like +24 is a lower priority stat.
