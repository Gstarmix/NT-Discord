# Can another player take mobs on your map while you're exping?

**Q:** Is it against the rules for another player to enter the map you're exping on and start taking your mobs?

**A:** Players cannot claim ownership of a map, so another player entering and fighting mobs there is not against the rules by itself. It only becomes a reportable issue if they deliberately steal the aggro of mobs you are already fighting — in that case you should record it and submit a support ticket.
