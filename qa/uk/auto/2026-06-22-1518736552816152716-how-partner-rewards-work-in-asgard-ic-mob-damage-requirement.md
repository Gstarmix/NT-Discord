# How partner rewards work in Asgard IC (mob damage requirement)

**Q:** As an Adventurer partner in Asgard IC (Instance Combat), I'm not getting any reward. Do I just need enough time to land the last hit on mobs across the waves, or does total damage output matter? (Context: Pirate partner +20, skilled, 100 elemental, 35 attack, +7/+6 equipment, all available books at character level 31.)

**A:** Getting a reward requires dealing close to a full mob's worth of damage over the run, not just landing the final hit. Roughly, you need to have effectively killed close to one mob by waves 1 to 2, two mobs by wave 3, and slightly more than three mobs total to get the win reward, so maximizing damage output throughout is necessary rather than relying on last-hitting.
