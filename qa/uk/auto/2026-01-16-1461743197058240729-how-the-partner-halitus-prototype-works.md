# How the Partner Halitus Prototype works

**Q:** How does the Partner Halitus Prototype work? I'm job level 30 but can't activate it — is it because my SP is only job level 25?

**A:** Your partner can only equip the Halitus Prototype starting at partner level 30. In addition to the level requirement, you also need a partner with the appropriate affinity/type for Halitus.
