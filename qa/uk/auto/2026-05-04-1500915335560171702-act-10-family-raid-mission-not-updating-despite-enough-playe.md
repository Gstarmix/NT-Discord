# Act 10 family raid mission not updating despite enough players completing it

**Q:** Why does the Act 10 family daily raid mission (e.g. the catacombs/crusher raid) sometimes stay stuck at 0/5 or fail to update even though more than 4 family members completed the raid? Was the FXP reward still granted even when the mission progress didn't display correctly?

**A:** This was a known issue where completions from certain family members were not being counted towards the Act 10 daily family raid mission, causing the counter to stay at 0/5 or update incorrectly even when enough players had completed the raid. A subsequent patch was believed to fix the underlying cause. Whether the FXP reward was still granted while the mission display was broken remained unconfirmed.
