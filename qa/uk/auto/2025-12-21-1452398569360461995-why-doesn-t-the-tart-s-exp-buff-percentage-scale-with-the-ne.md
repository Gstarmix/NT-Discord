# Why doesn't the Tart's EXP buff percentage scale with the new level cap?

**Q:** Why hasn't the Tart EXP buff been increased for the new level cap? The EXP gained from it appears the same as before despite the level cap increase - is this a bug?

**A:** It's not a bug: the Tart buff's EXP percentage is unchanged, but its duration was extended to last until level 80 (as opposed to a lower cap before), following the new level cap increase to 99+99. This is a deliberate design decision players have no control over.
