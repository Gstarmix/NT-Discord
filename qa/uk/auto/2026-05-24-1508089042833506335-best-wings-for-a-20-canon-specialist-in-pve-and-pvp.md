# Best wings for a +20 Canon specialist in PvE and PvP

**Q:** What are the best wings for a +20 Canon (Fernon) specialist, for both PvE and PvP? Is it true that Runic wings require fast DPS while Canon does not benefit from them the same way?

**A:** For PvP, Gossamer wings are the preferred choice. For PvE, both Party wings and Runic wings work, but Party wings are generally preferred. Overall it comes down to balancing stats between the options rather than one being strictly superior.
