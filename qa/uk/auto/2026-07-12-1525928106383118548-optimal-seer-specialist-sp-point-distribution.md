# Optimal seer specialist SP point distribution

**Q:** For the Seer specialist, is the optimal SP point distribution X / 0 / 100 / 0, similar to other specialists?

**A:** This distribution works well only if X (attack points) is not too low, since increased attack also boosts elemental damage. For example, 20/0/100/0 performs worse than 60/0/90/0. There is some uncertainty here: results may end up roughly similar depending on gear level (bearing in mind comparisons are usually based on BL/best-in-slot player stats), and with fewer skill level slots (SLs) available, a mixed distribution such as 60 attack / 60 elemental may be preferable instead.
