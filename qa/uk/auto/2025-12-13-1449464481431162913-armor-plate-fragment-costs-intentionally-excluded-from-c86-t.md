# Armor plate/fragment costs intentionally excluded from C86 transformation cost reduction

**Q:** Was reducing the amount of armor plates and fragments needed for the C86 armor transformation forgotten? Other transformation materials were reduced, but plates and shards were not — is this a bug?

**A:** This is not a bug. Plates and shards were deliberately left out of the material cost reduction — the developer (Prism) did not request any change to the plate/shard requirements for armors. Players who want this changed are encouraged to submit the request in the feedback channel.
