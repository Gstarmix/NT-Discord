# Bug: cooldown-reset buffs don't work on 'blue circle' skills

**Q:** Why don't cooldown-reset buffs (like the +20 Water SP buff, Fairy Wings buff, or Recuperative Healing tattoo) work on skills such as Tide Lord's Whale, Monk's Mighty Hurricane, or Scout's Falcon's Astuteness?

**A:** Skills that are cast using the 'blue circle' targeting indicator (Tide Lord's Whale, Monk's Mighty Hurricane, Scout's Falcon's Astuteness) have a known bug where cooldown-reset effects from buffs such as the +20 Water SP buff, the Fairy Wings buff, or the Recuperative Healing tattoo do not apply to them, unlike other skills.
