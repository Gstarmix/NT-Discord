# Does the 'Increases all elements' rune apply to both weapons and does it matter for S Damage%?

**Q:** For a rune/shell stat that reads 'S - Increases all elements' (as shown alongside 'A - Increased shadow element'), does this bonus apply to the main weapon too, and how does it interact with S Damage%?

**A:** This stat only applies to the weapon that is actively dealing damage, not to both weapons simultaneously. Its practical impact is minor, adding roughly a 1% damage increase at maximum, so it's generally considered not very valuable.
