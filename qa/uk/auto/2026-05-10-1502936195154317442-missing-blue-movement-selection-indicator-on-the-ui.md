# Missing blue movement/selection indicator on the UI

**Q:** The blue indicator box used for selecting/moving things is missing from my UI and has been gone for 4 days. Is this normal, and how do I fix it?

**A:** This is a UI display issue that can be fixed client-side. Reset your resolution in the settings, or use Shift+F1 through F6 (any of the six), or press Ctrl+X to restore the blue boxes. Enabling 'Fixed UI' in settings can also resolve it.
