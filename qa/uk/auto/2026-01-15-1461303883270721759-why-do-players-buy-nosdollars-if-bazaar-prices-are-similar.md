# Why do players buy NosDollars if bazaar prices are similar?

**Q:** What's the point of buying NosDollars if bazaar prices are roughly equivalent? Couldn't someone just win the wheel jackpot and log out instead?

**A:** Players mainly buy NosDollars to pay other players directly for specific items, scrolls, or mounts they want, rather than for the wheel itself. This is more about direct player-to-player purchases than jackpot farming; the bigger the desired purchase, the more it makes sense to buy ND this way for a faster or better deal than searching the bazaar.
