# How to resist the Deadly Blackout debuff from the level 99 Mandragores (PTS speedrun)

**Q:** Is there an item or way to get immunity or protection from the Deadly Blackout debuff applied by the level 99 Mandragores, for a PTS speedrun?

**A:** Any 'overall anti-effect' item rated for a level equal to or higher than the debuff's level (it is only a level 3 malus, not a high level one) will block it. Alternatively, any special skill that grants invisibility works, including the Cheongbi partner skill.
