# Which buff characters are essential vs. just situational?

**Q:** When building a roster of buff characters (Red Mage, Holy Mage, Blue Mage, Dark Gunner, WK, Crusader, War, Normal Mage), which buffs are actually important and which are just bonus/situational?

**A:** The most important buffs come from Holy Mage, Crusader, WK, Demon Warrior, and the Wedding buff. Normal Mage, War, Red Mage, Blue Mage, Dark Gunner, Volcanor, Tide Lord, Draconic Fist, and Master Wolf are considered bonus or situational buffs rather than essential ones.
