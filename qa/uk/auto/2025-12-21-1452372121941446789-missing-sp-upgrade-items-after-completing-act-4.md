# Missing SP upgrade items after completing Act 4

**Q:** After finishing Act 4, how do you receive the associated items/SP upgrades? Could an older character miss out on them since they predate this feature's implementation?

**A:** You should still receive the Act 4 completion items/SP upgrades regardless of character age; if they don't appear, try relogging, as they may simply be misplaced in your inventory rather than missing.
