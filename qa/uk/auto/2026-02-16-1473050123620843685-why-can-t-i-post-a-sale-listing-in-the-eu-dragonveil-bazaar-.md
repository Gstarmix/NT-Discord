# Why can't I post a sale listing in the EU Dragonveil bazaar Discord channel?

**Q:** Why can't I post a screenshot to sell items in the EU Dragonveil bazaar channel on Discord?

**A:** You need to reach level 5 on this Discord server before you can post messages (such as sale screenshots), and level 10 before you can comment on posts. You can also look for listings through the NosBazaar Dragonveil tool/channel instead.
