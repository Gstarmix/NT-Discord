# Why don't hair dye colors match what you request?

**Q:** Why don't dye colors come out bright or matching what was requested? For example, yellow for blonde isn't quite blonde, and the pink dye from normal characters looks more like red wine.

**A:** This is a known, longstanding issue affecting several old color/translation systems in the game, and it's low priority to fix. Hair dye also isn't purely cosmetic on the hair itself — some costume items (such as certain wing costumes) recolor/tint to match the hair dye too, as shown by butterfly wing costumes appearing in different color schemes depending on the hair color used.
