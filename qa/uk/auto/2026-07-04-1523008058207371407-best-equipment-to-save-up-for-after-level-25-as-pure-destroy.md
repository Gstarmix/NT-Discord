# Best equipment to save up for after level 25 as pure Destroyer/Hunter (dagger and bow)

**Q:** Playing dagger/hunter (DH) only, which equipment should I save up for after level 25 for both dagger and bow? Is it better to have one weapon with S rank all-skill bonus and elemental attack, and the other with S rank damage%, or to focus both on S damage%?

**A:** Unless you want to overspend, prioritize enhanced damage, crit chance (especially on the dagger), and decent special skill lines to get a solid overall set, rather than chasing a specific perfect split between the two weapons.
