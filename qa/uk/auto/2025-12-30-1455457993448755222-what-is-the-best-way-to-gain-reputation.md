# What is the best way to gain reputation?

**Q:** What is the best way to gain reputation points?

**A:** There are three main ways to gain reputation: doing raids, running TS 85 (also needed for the fisher specialty), and doing the party time space activity. Party time space is considered the best option overall since it also gives good money, and it can be done solo by controlling 3 characters at once.
