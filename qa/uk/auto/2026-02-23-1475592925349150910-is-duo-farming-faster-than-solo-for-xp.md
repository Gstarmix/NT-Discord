# Is duo farming faster than solo for XP

**Q:** Is farming in a duo better than solo for gaining XP?

**A:** Duo farming is generally better than solo, provided you do not have to wait long for mob respawns. It results in faster clearing and more mobs killed overall, which translates into better XP per hour.
