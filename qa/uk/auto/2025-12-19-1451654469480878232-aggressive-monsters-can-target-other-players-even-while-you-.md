# Aggressive monsters can target other players even while you are invisible

**Q:** While invisible, an aggressive monster I attacked switched targets to attack a random AFK player instead — is this a bug?

**A:** This is not a bug. Aggressive monsters will auto-aggro onto another nearby player if the one who attacked them is invisible, since they cannot be targeted while invisible.
