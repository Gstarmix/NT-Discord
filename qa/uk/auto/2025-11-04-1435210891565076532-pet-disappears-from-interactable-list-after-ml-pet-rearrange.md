# Pet disappears from interactable list after ML pet rearrangement/star upgrade (still counted, not selectable)

**Q:** A pet (e.g. a 5-star Chicken partway through training, one of the 4 needed for a 6-star pet upgrade) can vanish from being interactable after rearranging pets for training or collecting essence, even though it's not released and still shows in the ML list and character window. Other pet portraits remain selectable but the affected one behaves like a ghost. How can this be fixed without losing time-limited events?

**A:** This is a known client-side display bug, not a permanent loss. In confirmed cases, logging off for a while (a simple relog was not enough) made the pet interactable again; it may also require waiting until the next maintenance. Suggested steps in order: try re-entering your mini-land, relog (and if that fails, stay logged off longer), repair the client, and if the pet is truly gone after all that, open a support ticket.
