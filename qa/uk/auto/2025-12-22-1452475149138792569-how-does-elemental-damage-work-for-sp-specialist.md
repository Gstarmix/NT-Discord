# How does elemental damage work for SP (Specialist)?

**Q:** How does element work for SP damage — does an opposing element deal more damage, or does it pierce through the target's elemental resistance? How exactly does it work?

**A:** Elemental advantage increases damage dealt rather than piercing resistance. Dark vs Light (and vice versa) grants +200% elemental damage. Fire vs Water (and vice versa) grants +100%. Light beats Fire and Fire beats Dark and Dark beats Water and Water beats Light, each granting +50% elemental damage. Any element used against a target with no element grants +30% elemental damage.
