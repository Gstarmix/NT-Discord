# Pet and partner skill bar missing during a mounted raid entry

**Q:** Why do pet skills (and partner skills) disappear/not show up, for example during a raid?

**A:** This is a known issue: entering a raid, Celestial Tower, or similar instance that doesn't allow mounts while you are still mounted causes pet/partner skills to malfunction or disappear. Workaround: dismount before entering such raids/instances.
