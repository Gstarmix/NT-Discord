# How to interpret the 'opposite element' instruction in the Crusher Nezarun raid

**Q:** In the Crusher Nezarun raid, the instruction says to take the 'opposite element' from the previous instruction. If the previous instruction was 'don't go into light or shadow', which portal counts as the opposite for the next step?

**A:** The 'opposite' instruction refers to the opposite of the previous task, not a literal opposite element pair. For example, if the previous instruction was 'don't go into light or shadow', a valid response was entering the water portal. If the next instruction is 'opposite', players should then go into light or shadow, since that follows the same opposite-of-previous logic.
