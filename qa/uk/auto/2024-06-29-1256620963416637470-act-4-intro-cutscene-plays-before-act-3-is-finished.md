# Act 4 intro cutscene plays before Act 3 is finished

**Q:** The Act 4 intro cutscene played for me before I had even finished Act 3, right after relogging in Seli-Lusha 5. Is this a known issue?

**A:** This is a known, reported bug: if a player completes a certain Time Space around level 78, the Act 4 intro cutscene can trigger instead of the normal Act 3 completion cutscene. No workaround was given at the time.
