# When can a character start making good money, and which raids are worth it after the gold nerf

**Q:** At what point can a character start reliably making money (e.g. via Zenas/Erenia raids)? Is C80 with a good set already viable, or is it better to push to C90 first? Do Zenas/Erenia raids still need level 90 alts, and are they still worth doing after the recent gold nerf to A6 raids? Should farmed boxes be sold or opened for better profit?

**A:** A character around C80 with a decent set can already be strong and profitable; since leveling is fast, it is worth rushing to C80 first. From there, a common approach is to level alt characters through Instant Combat and LoD to level 85 and +10 gear to build a team for A7 raids, or alternatively gear up around 8 characters to level 80 to form a team for Glacerus/Dracerus raids. Zenas and Erenia raids (which do require level 90 alts) are still considered a good source of gold per hour even after the A6 gold nerf, though some players are unsure how much this holds post-nerf. On Dragonveil at least, box prices from these raids have risen, making it worth selling the boxes rather than opening them.
