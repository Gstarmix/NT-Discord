# Are family perks that reduce teleport/ship costs still useful now that travel costs are 0?

**Q:** The family perks 'Reduced Teleportation and Ship Costs' (levels 1-3, reducing teleport/ship voyage costs by 25%/50%/75%) still exist even though travel costs were changed to 0. Is it intentional to keep these perks in the game?

**A:** For now, yes, it is intentional to keep these perks even though they currently have no practical effect since travel costs were set to 0. This may be changed in the future.
