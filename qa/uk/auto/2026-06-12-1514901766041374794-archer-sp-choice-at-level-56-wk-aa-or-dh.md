# Archer SP choice at level 56: WK, AA, or DH

**Q:** I just switched to playing archer and I'm level 56. Should I go Wisemen King (WK), Avenging Angel (AA), or Dark Hunter (DH)? Also, should I aim for +14/+15 upgrade cards early, or is a perfect 100% card good enough for early game?

**A:** Dark Hunter (DH) with +20 upgrade performs well above 70% PvE stats, and other cards can mostly be ignored until max level. For leveling through LoD, a basic Wisemen King (level 99, +5 to +10) is enough unless you're doing the main quests, in which case picking a good DH (ideally +15 with 100% perfection) is preferable early on. After that you can continue with Zenas side content, or keep going through Ruins with the WK depending on difficulty. From act 7 to act 10, a DH alone can perform excellently.
