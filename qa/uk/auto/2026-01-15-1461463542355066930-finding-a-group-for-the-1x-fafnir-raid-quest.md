# Finding a group for the "1x Fafnir raid" quest

**Q:** What are the realistic chances of finding a group to complete a "1x Fafnir raid" quest?

**A:** Groups for this raid are much easier to find during EXP events, likely because players use them to level alts. Outside of events, the best approach is to look for players during high-population hours, such as evenings.
