# Greni/Kertos mini-boss spawns not appearing on Act 5.2 west path

**Q:** At Act 5.2, when farming the Greni/Kertos mini-bosses for seeds, the west path sometimes produces zero spawns over an hour while the east path spawns normally. Is this a known issue?

**A:** This is a known, recurring issue where mini-boss spawns (Greni/Kertos) on the Act 5.2 west path can fail to appear at all over long farming sessions, even while the east path spawns normally. A suggested workaround is to switch channels, which sometimes resolves the missing spawns.
