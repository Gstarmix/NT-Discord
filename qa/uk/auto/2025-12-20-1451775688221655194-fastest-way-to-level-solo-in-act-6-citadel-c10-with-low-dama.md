# Fastest way to level solo in Act 6 Citadel (C10) with low damage

**Q:** What is the fastest way to solo-level through the Citadel maps (C10) when dying on every lure and dealing very low damage (e.g. only ~40% XP/hour in C6 with Ancelloan)?

**A:** With low damage, luring in Act 6.1 with small, careful pulls and a good pet is one option, or go to Act 6.2 and let NPCs on the first map tank while you take last hits on mobs. Otherwise, you either need to invest in better gear/stats to farm efficiently, accept that leveling will take a long time with current gear, or pay someone else to power-level you. There isn't a fast solo option in the Citadel itself when damage is very low.
