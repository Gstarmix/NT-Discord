# Level requirement to use the Bazaar/Discord trade channel

**Q:** At what Discord level can you start writing (posting threads and comments) in the trade/Bazaar channel?

**A:** You need to reach Discord level 5 to post new threads, and level 10 to comment/reply on existing ones.
