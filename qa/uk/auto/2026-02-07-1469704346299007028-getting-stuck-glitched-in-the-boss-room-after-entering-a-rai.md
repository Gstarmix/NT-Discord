# Getting stuck/glitched in the boss room after entering a raid portal

**Q:** What causes the display/state glitch that sometimes happens right after entering the boss room during raids, and how can it be avoided?

**A:** This is a known issue as of early February 2026. It typically occurs when a player enters the raid portal at the exact same time as another player, or when a player uses a dash/teleport skill while being teleported through the portal. Avoiding simultaneous portal entry with others and not using dash skills during the teleport transition is the known workaround. If the glitch occurs without ever touching the portal, a video reproduction was requested to investigate further, meaning that variant is not yet understood.
