# Act 8-10 dailies and Act 9-10 main quests are excluded from the XP event

**Q:** The XP event is advertised as giving double XP for quests, but Act 8-10 daily quests give normal XP — is this intended?

**A:** Yes, this is intended: Act 8-10 daily quests and Act 9-10 main quests are not affected by the XP event, even though the event is advertised with quest XP bonuses in general. You can verify this by hovering over the XP reward icon, which shows '(not affected by exp event)' in parentheses for excluded quests.
