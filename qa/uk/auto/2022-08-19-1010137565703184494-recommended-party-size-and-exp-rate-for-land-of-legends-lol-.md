# Recommended party size and exp rate for Land of Legends (LoL) farming

**Q:** How many people are needed to farm Land of Legends (LoL) efficiently, and what kind of exp rate can be expected there (e.g. around character level 38)?

**A:** With normal (non-optimized) gear, a party of 4 is best for LoL; with good gear, 3 players is enough. This setup is roughly 50% more exp-efficient than doing the full 'polluted' path. Around level 38, each asgobas kill grants about 13% exp per day, letting you reach ~52% exp in about 40 minutes; farming double asgobas spawns can yield ~104% exp in about 80 minutes total. It's also recommended to relog right before the run starts.
