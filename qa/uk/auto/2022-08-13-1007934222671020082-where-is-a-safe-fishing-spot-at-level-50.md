# Where is a safe fishing spot at level 50?

**Q:** Where is a safe spot to fish around level 50? I rarely go to Act 5, so I don't know a good location there.

**A:** A generally safe approach is to fish wherever a large crowd of players (often 3 to 30) is already fishing on top of each other, since a busy spot is usually considered safer — though there is always a risk someone brings mobs nearby, whether on purpose or carelessly. One specific spot mentioned as good enough is behind TS 90-3 on Katol's street.
