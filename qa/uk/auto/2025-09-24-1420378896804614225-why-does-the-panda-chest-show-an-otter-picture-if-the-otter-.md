# Why does the panda chest show an otter picture if the otter isn't a possible drop?

**Q:** Why does the shop page for the 'Boite de panda flamboyante' (panda chest) show a picture of an otter next to it, even though the otter isn't listed among the chest's possible drops?

**A:** The otter picture is shown because buying 50 of these boxes grants a free otter as a bonus reward, separate from the box's normal drop table (which includes items like the baby panda, Ratufu pets, and Ancelloan blessings).
