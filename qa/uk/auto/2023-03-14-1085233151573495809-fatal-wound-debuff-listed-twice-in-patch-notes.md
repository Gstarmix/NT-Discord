# Fatal Wound debuff listed twice in patch notes

**Q:** Why does the 'Fatal Wound' negative effect appear twice in the new debuff patch notes, tied to different 'Strong Impulse' levels with different proc chances?

**A:** The two 'Fatal Wound' entries are not a new mechanic, they describe the same debuff (never inflicts critical hits, magic damage reduced by 20%, movement speed reduced by 2, and a side effect after 0.1s that increases damage taken from all attacks by 10%). It just gets triggered by different sources or levels of 'Strong Impulse' (10%, 40%, or 100% chance depending on duration/level), and the duplication in the notes is considered accidental rather than a sign of a new trigger category.
