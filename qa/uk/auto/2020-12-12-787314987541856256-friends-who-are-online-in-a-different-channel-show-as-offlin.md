# Friends who are online in a different channel show as offline in the friend list

**Q:** I can't see people on my friend list as online when they are in a different channel than me. Is this intended?

**A:** This is not intended behavior but is a long-known issue. A full relog can temporarily fix it. It has also been reported to occur more often when you have a large number of friends, in which case cleaning up (removing some entries from) your friend list can help.
