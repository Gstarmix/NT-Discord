# Which pets have a voke skill

**Q:** Does the Purrcival pet have a voke (taunt) skill?

**A:** Purrcival does not innately have voke; any pet can learn voke through Tamer SP (levels 1-6). Among pets with natural voke behavior: Otter and Santa Claws voke around themselves/nearby targets, Panda vokes around the mob you clicked, and Raccoon vokes around itself but is considered not very useful. There was some disagreement in the discussion about whether Lumi also has Panda-like voke behavior, so that point is uncertain.
