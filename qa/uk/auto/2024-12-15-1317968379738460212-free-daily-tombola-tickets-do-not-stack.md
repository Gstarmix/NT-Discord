# Free daily tombola tickets do not stack

**Q:** If a player has both bought tombola tickets on 3 consecutive days and completed the act 4 quest requirement, do they get 2 free daily tombola tickets instead of 1?

**A:** No, the free ticket conditions do not stack. Regardless of qualifying through both the 3-day purchase streak and the act 4 quest completion, a player only receives 1 free daily tombola ticket.
