# Best partner to invest in for a PvE archer

**Q:** Which partner is best to invest in for a PvE archer?

**A:** It depends on your budget: Forga is cheap and good, Marie offers the best utility, and Nezarun is the strongest and newest option but very expensive. If the question is about which partner to level up in general (not partner-SP specifically), any partner is fine to use until you get your first partner-SP, after which you should choose based on the SP you want.
