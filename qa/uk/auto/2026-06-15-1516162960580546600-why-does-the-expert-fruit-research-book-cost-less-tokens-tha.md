# Why does the expert fruit research book cost less tokens than the advanced one?

**Q:** The expert fruit research book (50 atk/50 def) is described as stronger and replacing the advanced fruit research book (30 atk/30 def), so why does the expert book cost fewer tokens (125) than the advanced one (200)?

**A:** You are required to use the advanced (30/30) book first before you can use the expert (50/50) book, which replaces it. This is why the expert book alone is cheaper: its price reflects only the upgrade step on top of the advanced book you must already own and use.
