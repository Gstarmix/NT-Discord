# Does the main quest SP upgrade stone affect a 100% perfect SP?

**Q:** Is it safe to use the SP upgrade stone obtained from the main quest on a 100% perfect (100pp) SpecialistCard? Will it reset the perfection?

**A:** It is safe to use. The stone does not change the SP's perfection percentage (pp); it only affects the upgrade level (+) and job level.
