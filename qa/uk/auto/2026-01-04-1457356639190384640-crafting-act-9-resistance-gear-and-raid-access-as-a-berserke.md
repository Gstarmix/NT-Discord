# Crafting Act 9 resistance gear and raid access as a berserker

**Q:** Which materials are needed to craft Act 9 resistance equipment, and can a c90 berserker or crusader still join A9 raids?

**A:** A c90 berserker (or crusader) can still join Act 9 raids without issue, since the act was previously level-capped at 90. Act 9 resistance gear is crafted using special tokens dropped from raids across Act 8 to Act 10 (excluding SP-only raids like Asgobas or Ultimate Arma); these tokens function like crafting scrolls and can be used to craft several items, including resistances.
