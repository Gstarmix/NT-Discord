# Why does the Dragon gem price change relative to SP events

**Q:** Why is the Dragon gem more expensive when there is no SP (skill point) event, compared to during such events?

**A:** During SP events, many players sell their Dragon gems (to fund SP upgrades), which increases supply and pushes the price down. Outside of these events, fewer players are selling, so supply is lower relative to demand and the price rises.
