# Does the Act 4 completion pearl reset an SP with +100 upgrade?

**Q:** If I use the pearl that completes Act 4 on an SP that is already +100, does it reset the SP, or does it keep the job level and hero points (PP)?

**A:** No, using the Act 4 completion pearl on an SP does not reduce its job level, its upgrade level (+), or its hero points (PP).
