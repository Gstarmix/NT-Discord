# Starting a character at level 1 versus level 56

**Q:** Is it better to start a new character at level 1, or take the level 56 starter kit?

**A:** Starting at level 56 is strongly preferred over level 1. Levels below 56 give no gear worth keeping, no fairy, and no SP, and the early mobs teach nothing useful about the actual game. The level 56 start immediately grants level 50 gear, the four fairies, and the first two SPs, material that would otherwise need to be farmed or bought. Since the game effectively begins around level 80, skipping the empty early levels is a head start, not a loss.
