# Best ways to farm gold while leveling from c80 to c90

**Q:** What are good maps to solo level and farm gold at the same time between character level 80 and 90? Is Act 9 farming worthwhile compared to just rushing to LoL, and what are the best gold-making tips at this level range to afford c90 gear?

**A:** Profitability of farming spots fluctuates, so it is worth checking dropped item prices on NosBasar. Secret Lab was considered the most profitable farming spot, but it is hard to farm unless your character is strong. Items like the polluted beaker and various books also sell for a decent amount. For weaker characters, the southern basement path and Power Plant 1 are good maps that offer both solid experience and gold.
