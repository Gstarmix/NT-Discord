# Gear priorities and HP/mana regen for a returning level 75 player on SP4

**Q:** I recently returned to the game and am currently doing SP4 quests at level 75 (just finished the laboratory questline), with the gear shown in the screenshot (Legendary Slayer +8, Mysterious Armour of Valour +5, Sparkle Shoes, Shining Fast Gloves, Light Fairy). What should I do with my current gear, what's my next upgrade, and should I look for a family to run LOD with to level up? Also, my HP/mana regen is slow (I need to sit for a while) — is there a fix, or a budget food comparable to event foods?

**A:** Finishing the SP4/Act 4 questline is a good priority since it rewards useful gear. For HP sustain, investing in a Yuna partner with an S-rank heal skill is very effective, especially on a warrior. For budget alternatives to event food, look for meat-type snacks on the Nosapki price-check site, though they aren't necessarily cheap. As a returning player you can also create a new level 56 warrior character to get access to storage items like +5 SP cards and resistance cards for free/cheap gearing (resistance card availability may have changed, but SP cards should still work).
