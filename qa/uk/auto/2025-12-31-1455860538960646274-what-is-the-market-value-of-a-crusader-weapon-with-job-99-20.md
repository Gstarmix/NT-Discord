# What is the market value of a Crusader weapon with job 99, +20 perfection 100, 45 attack, 40 elemental damage?

**Q:** What is the approximate market value of a Crusader weapon with job level 99, +20 upgrade, perfection 100, 45 attack, and 40 elemental damage?

**A:** Take the price of a normal Crusader weapon with the same perfection (100) and add roughly 2 billion (2kkk) gold for these extra stats. The upgrade level (+) itself doesn't add much value here, so a similar weapon anywhere between +0 and +15 would be priced similarly.
