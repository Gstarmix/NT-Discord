**Q:** For the quest 'Hunt Rock Golem and collect 10 Golem Core,' the mobs disappear too quickly. Is there an easier way to complete it, and is it locked to Spirit Forest?

**A:** Head to the edge of the Spirit Forest map to find the Rock Golems needed for this quest.
