# Which quests reward SP upgrade stones to reach +9

**Q:** Will I get an item to upgrade my SP to +9 by completing missions in Dragonveil (Act 4 and beyond)?

**A:** Yes, if you have not completed the quest yet, you will get the item. The Act 4 quest rewards a stone usable for SP 1-4, the Act 6.1 quest for SP 5-8, and the Act 7.1 quest works for any SP. The Act 8 quest does not give a stone.
