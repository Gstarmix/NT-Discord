# Changing a wrongly purchased NosMall item

**Q:** I accidentally bought the wrong item on NosMall (cash shop). Can it be changed?

**A:** There is no self-service way to swap or refund a NosMall purchase in-game. You need to open a support ticket with Gameforge, and their team will decide whether to grant an exchange.
