# How does Iron Skin reduce archer damage against a swordsman in PvP?

**Q:** In a Ranger vs Warrior fight, how much is an archer's damage reduced by the swordsman's Iron Skin, and does proximity affect it further?

**A:** Against a swordsman using Iron Skin, an archer's (ranged) damage is reduced to roughly 40% of normal. If the swordsman closes to within 2 cells of the archer, the damage is reduced further, down to about 70% of that already-reduced amount. It is unclear whether this reduction also applies to critical/soft-crit damage.
