# Fairy booster damage increase with Sound Flower buff active

**Q:** Is it a known bug that using a Fairy Booster while the Sound Flower buff is active only shows the damage increase after the Sound Flower buff ends and a new one is obtained?

**A:** When tested, the Fairy Booster's damage bonus applied instantly on top of an active Sound Flower buff as intended, with no delay observed. This suggests the reported behavior is not a confirmed bug, though it was only checked by one player.
