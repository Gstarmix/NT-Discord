# Best partner build for LoL (survivability)

**Q:** Which partner is recommended for LoL? My Forga on Jennifer keeps dying a lot. Is Pir the best option to pair with Marie instead?

**A:** Forga is a pure attack-focused SP with no healing at all, which is why the partner keeps dying in LoL fights. Marie is the recommended SP instead, since her passive heal procs automatically in combat and keeps the partner alive much better. Rather than buying Pir, consider Graham, obtained for free through the A6.1 quest: he is a magic-type partner (so a level 63 wand converted into a partner wand gives him +10 to all resistances) but still fights in melee, making him tanky. Marie equipped on Graham is considered a strong combo. Keeping your partner alive also grants extra XP bonuses.
