# Can't redeem reward from the event minigame (e.g. anniversary wood-chopping game)

**Q:** In the anniversary event minigame (wood-chopping game with a reward-level selection screen), the reward could not be redeemed/claimed. What causes this and how to fix it?

**A:** This is a known issue as of October 2025. Restarting the game client typically fixes the reward not being redeemable. It also helps to avoid clicking the "reward" button twice in quick succession, as that can trigger the bug.
