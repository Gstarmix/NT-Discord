# Missing 'Needle of the Holy Light' quest item for Act 7.2 (Chief Tator)

**Q:** During the Act 7.2 quest step where Chief Tator asks for 1 Needle of the Holy Light and 1 Thread of the Holy Light, the Needle of the Holy Light is missing from both the inventory and the mailbox (the quest window shows the Thread received but the Needle still at 0/1). What should be done?

**A:** The Needle of the Holy Light is normally received from the related side quest ('Needle in the Haystack') after completing 'Act 7: 13 TS' — it's possible it was accidentally deleted. First restart the game client, since items can sometimes only appear after a relog; if the Needle still isn't in the inventory or mailbox afterward, open a support ticket to have it restored.
