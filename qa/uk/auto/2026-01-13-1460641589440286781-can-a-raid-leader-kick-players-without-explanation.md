# Can a raid leader kick players without explanation?

**Q:** Is it against the rules for a raid leader to kick a player from a raid without explaining why?

**A:** It is not against the rules; kicking without explanation may be considered rude, but it is entirely the raid leader's choice. Leaders can kick players to make room for friends/family, to bring in higher-level players, or for any other reason they see fit.
