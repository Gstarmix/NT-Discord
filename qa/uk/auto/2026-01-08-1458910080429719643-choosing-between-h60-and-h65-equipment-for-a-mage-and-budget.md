# Choosing between H60 and H65 equipment for a mage, and budget guidance

**Q:** Should I buy H60 or H65 equipment for my mage, and how much should I spend to get a decent set (a full set or just a good wand + secondary from the bazaar)?

**A:** It's generally better to wait for H65 equipment since it gives a damage boost usable in LoL content, and you'll need to replace it again at C80/C83 anyway so overspending isn't worth it. A reasonable budget is around 100-150kk for a good enough set (more if duoing LoL content), but with decent SP investment even ~50kk gear can perform well; a casual 3-5 player group doesn't require top-tier equipment. Recommendations depend heavily on your budget, main SP build, fairy, and resistances, so providing those details gets more precise advice.
