# Act 7.2 quest item stops dropping (Martial Artist)

**Q:** Are there known issues with Act 7.2 quest item drops (the quest after Kirollas) for Martial Artist? In a group I got 18/20 required items, but after leaving the group I got no drops for 45 minutes even though the drop rate is known to be low. Does having another active drop quest (e.g. an Act 5 quest like the egg quest) block drops for the Act 7.2 quest?

**A:** This is not a bug: if you have any other active drop quests, the game requires you to complete those first before items for your current quest (e.g. the Act 7.2 quest) will start dropping again. Having an unrelated active quest, such as an Act 5 egg quest, can therefore block drops for the Act 7.2 quest until it is finished.
