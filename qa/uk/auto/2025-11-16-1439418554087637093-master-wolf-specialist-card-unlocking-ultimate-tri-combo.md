# Master Wolf Specialist Card: unlocking Ultimate Tri-Combo

**Q:** On the Master Wolf Specialist Card, while using Ultimate Stance I can use all the ultimate skills but not the Ultimate Tri-Combo. Is this a bug or am I missing something?

**A:** This is not a bug. The upgraded Ultimate Tri-Combo requires both Ultimate Stance with 1000 points gained and having built up the 3rd Ultimate buff stage. To reach that stage you need to cast ultimate skills three times without losing the buff in between, since it does not last very long.
