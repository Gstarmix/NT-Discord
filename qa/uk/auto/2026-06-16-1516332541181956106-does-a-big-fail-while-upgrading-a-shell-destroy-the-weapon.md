# Does a big fail while upgrading a shell destroy the weapon?

**Q:** If a shell upgrade attempt on a weapon big fails, does it destroy the weapon, or does it just reset the shell's upgrade level?

**A:** A big fail does not destroy the weapon. Instead, similar to tattoo upgrades, the shell's upgrade level simply drops down by one level.
