# How group experience is split or bonused across different map types

**Q:** In a group, is experience simply divided among the number of players, or is there a bonus for grouping?

**A:** It depends on the map type. On normal maps, experience is split between group members (solo 100%, duo 75% each, trio 70% each), and each member must deal at least 11% of the damage to get the higher rate, otherwise a flat fallback split applies (50% each in duo, 33.33% each in trio). On LoL (Land of Twilight) maps, grouping gives a bonus above 100% instead of splitting: duo 110% each, trio 115% each. On LoD (Land of Life/Dark), the bonus is even higher: duo 115% each, trio 130% each, and the LoD multipliers stack further (group bonus, then an additional multiplier once the boss spawns). This distinction between LoL and LoD bonus values, and the fact that normal maps only ever add up to 100% (never a real bonus), was disputed by one player but reaffirmed with a source pointing to official 2023 XP update notes; the fallback flat-split rates (50/33) predate the 2023 patch and still apply when the 11% damage threshold isn't met.
