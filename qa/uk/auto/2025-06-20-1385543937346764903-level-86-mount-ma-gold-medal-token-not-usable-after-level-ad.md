# Level 86 mount (MA) gold medal token not usable after level adjustment

**Q:** A level 86 mount (MA) three-horse gold medal token can't be used. Is this a known issue?

**A:** Known issue: mount tokens/medals that recently had their level requirement adjusted, and that already existed before that patch, were not updated correctly, so they fail to work. Newly obtained tokens after the patch are not affected. A fix is expected but not guaranteed on any timeline.
