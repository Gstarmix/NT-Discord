# Does the Chi Tu mount remove negative status effects when using a speed booster

**Q:** Is the Chi Tu mount supposed to remove negative status effects (debuffs) when a speed booster is active, since it doesn't seem to do so?

**A:** No, removing negative effects while using a speed booster is not part of Chi Tu's design, and its item description does not state this behavior. By contrast, another special mount is known to remove debuffs when a speed booster is used even though its description also doesn't mention it, so behavior can differ between mounts regardless of what the description states.
