# Is it worth trying to get a shell to S++ at low upgrade levels?

**Q:** What's the best way to upgrade a shell? Is it worth trying to get S++ at +6 to avoid the more expensive higher upgrade levels?

**A:** It's actually cheaper overall to push the shell to +8 than to try to get S++ at +6 or +8, so aiming for S++ early to save costs is not the efficient path.
