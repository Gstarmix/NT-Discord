# Progression order: leveling alts vs pushing a main to c10/c30 for Erenia/Zenas

**Q:** With a level 80 mage, is it better to first push 14 alts to c10/c30 to farm other raids like Erenia/Zenas or Kiro/Carno, or should the main be leveled to c93+ first before investing in higher-class alts?

**A:** With a level 80 mage, the recommended order is to first level 14 level-80 alts, since LoD raids are easy to farm at that level; this can be done in parallel while pushing the main character toward c99. Only after the main reaches c99 is it worth considering pushing alts to c10/c30, which is a significant time investment. Erenia and Zenas are considerably harder than Glacerus and Draco, and there was a recent gold nerf on them, so while still profitable, it's currently unclear how their profitability compares to Glacerus/Draco-style raids.
