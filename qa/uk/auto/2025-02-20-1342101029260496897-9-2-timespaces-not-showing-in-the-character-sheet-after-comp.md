# 9.2 timespaces not showing in the character sheet after completion

**Q:** Is it normal that certain 9.2 timespaces do not appear as completed in the character sheet after finishing them? Is this a bug?

**A:** This is expected, not a bug. Those specific timespaces are side timespaces that come from an SP quest rather than belonging to a specific act, so they are not listed among the act's regular timespaces. All of an act's timespaces would need to be completed only for the SP quest requirement, not for the character sheet act listing.
