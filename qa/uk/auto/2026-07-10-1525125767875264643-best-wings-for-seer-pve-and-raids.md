# Best wings for Seer PvE and raids

**Q:** What are the best wings for a Seer in PvE, especially for single target damage and top 3 raid ranking rather than mob clearing? Considering Party, Runic, Xmas, Mecha and Steampunk wings.

**A:** Rough ranking is Mecha greater or equal to Runic, greater than Party equal to Xmas, greater than Steampunk. Mecha is considered the strongest overall but very expensive for the marginal gain. For a more reasonable budget, Xmas (valued for its on hit heal synergy with Seer's spam playstyle and buffs) or Party (valued for strong debuffs/buffs and max damage) are the recommended picks. For raids specifically, Party is favored for maximum damage, Xmas for sustain, and Runic is also viable thanks to its level 5 debuff, though overpaying for Runic is considered not worth it since it doesn't clearly outperform the cheaper options.
