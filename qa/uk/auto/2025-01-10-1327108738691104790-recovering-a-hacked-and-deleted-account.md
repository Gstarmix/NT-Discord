# Recovering a hacked and deleted account

**Q:** If my account gets hacked and deleted, is there still a chance to recover it and my items?

**A:** If you received an email confirming the account deletion, it should contain a link to cancel the deletion. If that email is no longer available, you need to open a support ticket with Gameforge support to request account recovery.
