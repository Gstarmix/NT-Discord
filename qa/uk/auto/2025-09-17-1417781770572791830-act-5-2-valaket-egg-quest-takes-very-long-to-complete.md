# Act 5.2 Valaket Egg quest takes very long to complete

**Q:** In the Act 5.2 quest requiring 10 Valaket Eggs from Flame Spider Valaket, is there a bug with the drop rate? Progress is extremely slow (e.g. 500 kills with no drops).

**A:** This is not a bug. The actual drop chance for Valaket Egg is around 1% (not 0.7% as some players recalled from tracking sites), so completing this collection quest can legitimately take anywhere from 10-20 minutes to 2+ hours depending on luck. Drop-rate values shown on tracking sites for quest-item drops may not be accurate. If you have other non-cancellable quests active, try finishing or cancelling those first, as they can interfere.
