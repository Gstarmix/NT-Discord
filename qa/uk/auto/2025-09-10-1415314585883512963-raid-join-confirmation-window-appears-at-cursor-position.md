# Raid join confirmation window appears at cursor position

**Q:** Is it normal that the raid team join confirmation window sometimes appears in a spot where you can't click it directly (e.g. behind the team list)?

**A:** Yes, this is intended for now: you can press Enter to accept the confirmation even when you can't click it directly. The confirmation window currently spawns at the position of your cursor when you click the raid entry, rather than at a fixed location.
