# Fair price for a +15, 80+ perfection Demon Hunter SP card

**Q:** I bought a +15 Demon Hunter SP card (level 99, 80+ perfection, as shown in the screenshot) for 1.8 billion gold. Did I overpay?

**A:** 1.8 billion is somewhat on the high side, but it's very common for an 80+ perfection Demon Hunter to sell for over 1 billion on the DV server. A cheaper one could be found, but it would take a long time to search, since act 9 started and players realized Demon Hunter is excellent for mobbing and pre-room clearing in act 9 raids, which pushed demand and prices up.
