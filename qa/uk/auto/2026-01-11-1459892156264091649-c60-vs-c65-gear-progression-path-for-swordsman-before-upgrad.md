# C60 vs C65 gear progression path for swordsman before upgrading to C80/83+

**Q:** For a swordsman, what makes more sense as a gear progression: C60 Belial + Beast King set now, switching to C83/90+ later (weapon and/or full set), or C60 Beast crossbow (for the useful) + C65 sword with a good PvE shell, later reforging into a C80 Dragonslayer sword?

**A:** A good C60 crossbow can remain competitive even up to C83 gear, since the difference isn't huge. For the sword, a good C60 sword can also last until C83, since the Berserker class itself deals strong damage so you won't feel a lack of it. That said, if strong C65 equipment is available, it's also fine to use it up to C83/90. It's recommended to check the market for what's actually available and pick whichever option fits best.
