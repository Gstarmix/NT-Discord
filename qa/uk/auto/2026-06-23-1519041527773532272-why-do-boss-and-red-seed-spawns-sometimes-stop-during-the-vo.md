# Why do boss and red seed spawns sometimes stop during the Volcano farming event?

**Q:** While farming the Volcano event as a Martial Artist on the last map (near Greniga's raid entrance), sometimes no bosses spawn for red seeds and no raid double tokens drop for a full hour even after clearing the map, is this a bug?

**A:** Farming the last map is the right approach, and normally it works fine (players reported getting around 30 raids and 7 double tokens in similar sessions). However, some players have occasionally experienced an hour with zero boss spawns for no apparent reason, even after switching channels. This seems to resolve on its own after some time (e.g. the next day), but the exact cause is unknown and may be outside players' control.
