# How long do bank transfer NosDollar payments take, and do you still get the event bonus if delayed?

**Q:** How long does it take to receive NosDollars via bank transfer, and do you still get the NosDollar bonus from an event if the transaction takes too long to process?

**A:** Bank transfers can take a while to process. If the event bonus doesn't get applied automatically, contact support, show them the date you sent the money, and politely ask for the bonus — support is generally generous about granting it in that case.
