# What does the shell's "Undisturbed" option do for spellcasters?

**Q:** What does the 'Undisturbed' shell option do, and does it apply to gun/main weapon users too? For context: when mages (or Jajamaru) cast spells, the cast can be interrupted/cancelled by taking damage during the animation. Mages have a Concentration stat that partially protects against this, but it isn't perfect.

**A:** The 'Undisturbed' shell option makes the wearer immune to spellcast interruption, so a mage-type character can be attacked by a group of monsters while casting and still complete the cast. This is in contrast to the Concentration stat, which only reduces (but doesn't fully prevent) the chance of a cast being interrupted. Whether this applies to the main weapon specifically was raised as an open question and not confirmed in the discussion.
