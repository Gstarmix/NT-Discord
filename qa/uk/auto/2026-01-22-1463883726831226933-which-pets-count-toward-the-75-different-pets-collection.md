# Which pets count toward the 75-different-pets collection

**Q:** For the pet collection requirement (needing 75 different pets), do they have to be pets you can catch yourself, or can NosMall pets count too?

**A:** It doesn't matter whether the pets are catchable in the wild or obtained from the NosMall — any pet counts toward the collection as long as it was originally a 1-star or 2-star pet.
