# Good ways to make money at character level 44

**Q:** What are some good ways to make money at character level 44?

**A:** Good early gold sources include farming seals from Erenia, Zenas, Kiro and Chicken King, farming Dragon Gems and Moritius Obsidian, and buying Nosmall items while on sale to resell later. Other suggested sources are running PTS and IC dungeons, Glacerus seals, act 1 hero chaos seals, and act 7 daily quests (seals and Kiro/Carno materials).
