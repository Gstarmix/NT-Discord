# 'Insufficient items' error handing in items to Soraya for The Death of the Kenkos

**Q:** During the 'The Death of the Kenkos' quest, delivering 1 Damaged Key and 4 Black Diamond to Soraya Style gives an 'insufficient items' error even though I have the required items - why?

**A:** This happens when you have another active quest that also requires delivering an item to Soraya (for example the Jajamaru quest) - the overlapping delivery requirement blocks the turn-in. Check your quest log for any other quest needing a delivery to Soraya and resolve it first.
