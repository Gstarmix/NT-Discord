# Do general fairy experience cards also work on drones

**Q:** The drone experience cards are stated to work only on drones, and the fairy experience cards are stated to work on all fairies. Does that mean the general (yellow) fairy cards also work on drones, or are the drone-specific (red) cards required separately for drones?

**A:** The general "all fairies" experience cards also work on drones, so drones can be leveled with either the drone-specific cards or the general fairy cards.
