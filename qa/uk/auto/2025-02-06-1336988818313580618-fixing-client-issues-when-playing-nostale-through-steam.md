# Fixing client issues when playing NosTale through Steam

**Q:** I play NosTale through Steam and I'm having client problems. What should I do?

**A:** Verify the integrity of the game files through Steam: right-click the game in your Steam library, open Properties, go to Installed Files, and select Verify integrity of game files.
