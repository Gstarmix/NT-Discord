# Character/NPCs not rendering, only terrain loads

**Q:** After returning to the game after 10 years, my character and NPCs aren't appearing, only the terrain loads (I can still move by clicking). After dying and returning to town, the UI also started flickering. What's causing this and how do I fix it?

**A:** This is often caused by being stuck in an Act 4 instance, which normally shows a blue screen; staying idle for about 1 hour will get you automatically kicked out of Act 4, which should resolve the issue. If you are not in Act 4, try repairing or reinstalling the game client. If reinstalling doesn't fix it, contact game support.
