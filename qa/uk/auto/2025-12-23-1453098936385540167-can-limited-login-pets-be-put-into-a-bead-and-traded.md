# Can limited login pets be put into a bead and traded?

**Q:** If I use a limited pet (one of the ones obtained from daily login rewards), can I put it into a bead and trade it?

**A:** No. Limited pets (such as those from login rewards) cannot be beaded and traded because of their limited status.
