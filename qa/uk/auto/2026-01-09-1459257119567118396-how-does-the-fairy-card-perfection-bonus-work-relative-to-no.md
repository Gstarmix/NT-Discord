# How does the fairy card perfection bonus work relative to normal SP points?

**Q:** How does the perfection stat work on a fairy card — is it independent from the normal SP points? For example, if a card is at 96 SP with a +4 perfection, does that effectively give the same bonus as reaching 100 SP?

**A:** Perfection is a flat bonus applied directly to the card's stats, separate from the normal SP point total — you can see it by scrolling/inspecting the card. In the example given (96 SP with +4 perfection), the +4 is an independent flat bonus and not literally equivalent to having 100 SP.
