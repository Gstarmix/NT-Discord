# Inventory expander from starter pack runs out after 30 days instead of 60

**Q:** Why does an inventory expander from a starter pack run out after only 30 days when it was supposed to last 60 days?

**A:** Starter packs contained two different inventory expansion items, one lasting 60 days and another lasting only 30 days. If an expander expired after 30 days, it was likely the 30-day version rather than the 60-day one, so this is not a bug but a mix-up between the two items.
