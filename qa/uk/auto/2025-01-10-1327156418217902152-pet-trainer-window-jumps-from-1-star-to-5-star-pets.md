# Pet trainer window jumps from 1 star to 5 star pets

**Q:** In the pet trainer window, why does the pet list sometimes jump straight from showing 1 star pets to 5 star pets?

**A:** This is normal when the 'All Pets' filter is selected: pets are displayed at their current, already trained star level, so a pet that started at 1 star can appear at a higher level if it has been trained before. The window can also visibly refresh or jump while a pet is actively being trained at that moment; the trainer feature is reportedly buggy, so avoiding it while browsing may help.
