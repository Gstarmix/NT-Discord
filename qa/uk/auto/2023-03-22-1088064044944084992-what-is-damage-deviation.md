# What is damage deviation

**Q:** What does "damage deviation" mean in NosTale's damage system?

**A:** Damage deviation refers to the minimum and maximum range of your damage output. Each time you attack, the game rolls a value somewhere between your minimum and maximum damage, which introduces randomness and makes precise damage testing difficult.
