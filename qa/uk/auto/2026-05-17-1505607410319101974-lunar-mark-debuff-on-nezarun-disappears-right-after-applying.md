# Lunar Mark debuff on Nezarun disappears right after applying

**Q:** Is it a bug that the Lunar Mark ('Marchio lunare') debuff/mark applied during the Nezarun encounter appears and then disappears again a second later?

**A:** This is not a bug: the mark is intentionally removed as soon as the affected target receives a hit, so it can appear to vanish almost immediately in combat.
