# How many trainers to use at once

**Q:** Should I use one pet trainer at a time or several at once?

**A:** There are 8 spots available near your pet, so you can use up to 8 trainers at minimum simultaneously. Some trainers attack from a 2 cell range, in which case you may need to use a different type of trainer to avoid retaliation issues.
