# Finishing Timespace 57 within the time limit without reading the story

**Q:** A high-level, fast character can clear Timespace 57 by rushing without reading the story dialogue, but how is a new/low-level player supposed to finish it in time if they can't out-damage the clock? A run with two 'wait 3 minutes' rooms left very little time to spare even for a fast character.

**A:** Most players skip the story text entirely rather than read it. There's a reported mechanic where the timer pauses to let you read while playing solo, but this reportedly does not work when running the TS in a party. Some TS 57 rooms involve a 'pick a path/lever' choice where picking wrong sharply cuts your remaining time — only one specific choice is said to not reduce the timer, so choosing correctly matters for staying within the limit.
