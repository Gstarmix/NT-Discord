# Missing quest/timespace reward item after completion

**Q:** I completed a quest and its associated timespace (TS) but didn't receive the reward item. Can I still get it from a shop?

**A:** That reward item is only granted the first time you clear the associated timespace; it cannot be bought from a shop afterward. If you didn't receive it and it's not in your inbox, you need to contact official support to have it restored.
