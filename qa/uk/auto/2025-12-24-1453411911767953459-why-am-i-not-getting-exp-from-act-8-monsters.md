# Why am I not getting EXP from Act 8 monsters?

**Q:** Why do I get no experience from Act 8 monsters as a character level 44?

**A:** Act 8 monsters are intended for characters around level 53/54 and above. Below that level range, an experience penalty chart applies, which is why a level 44 character gets little to no EXP there.
