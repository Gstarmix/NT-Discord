# Do GMs respond to direct messages, or is a ticket required?

**Q:** Do GMs respond to direct messages, or do you have to open a ticket instead?

**A:** Some GMs do respond to DMs, but not all of them, and a reply doesn't guarantee help — they will often just tell you to open a ticket. It can depend on what the issue is, so it can still be worth trying to DM a GM first.
