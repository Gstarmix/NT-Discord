# What should a level ~79 character do besides LoD and IC?

**Q:** Aside from Land of Death (LoD) and Icebreaker (IC), what should a character around level 79 (who is already past the early questline) be doing?

**A:** First make sure the main storyline is completed up to Time Space (TS) 55, since later acts unlock more content and objectives. Until around level 85, running Land of Death (LoD) repeatedly is considered the best and fastest way to gain experience.
