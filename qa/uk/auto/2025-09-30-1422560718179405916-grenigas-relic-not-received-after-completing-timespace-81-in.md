# Grenigas Relic not received after completing TimeSpace 81 in Rashid Center (act 5.2 main quest)

**Q:** During the act 5.2 main quest, after completing TimeSpace 81 in Rashid Center to obtain the Grenigas Relic and talk to Michol, the Relic was not received. Restarting the quest didn't help. How can this be fixed?

**A:** This typically happens when the inventory was full or the client crashed at the moment of claiming the time-space end rewards, causing the relic to be lost. Check your inventory and mailbox for the item; if it's not there, make sure you have free inventory space and restart the game to retry. If it's still missing, submit a support ticket requesting the item back, though this may take some time to be resolved.
