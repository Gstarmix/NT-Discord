# Family level shown as 1 and inventory items appear locked/missing after login

**Q:** After logging into the main account, the family level displays as 1 (even though it isn't) and the pet/partner are not shown, although their bonus effects are still active. This fixes itself after changing maps once or twice. Separately, the inventory sometimes appears permanently locked in places: some items show as available at the top but can't be used, and items from later acts (stored at the bottom of an act-ordered inventory) become invisible/ghosted, though picking up new items from the floor still seems to add them to the bag. Relogging or using the client's inventory fix tool does not resolve it, and it can persist even after a maintenance.

**A:** This has been reported by a few other players before, but account/character data was checked and found to be fine on the server side each time. The issue also sometimes fixed itself after a maintenance. Based on investigation so far, this does not appear to be a server-side bug but rather something related to the player's computer (corrupted local files or process). Suggested workaround: run system scans, launch NosTale without other programs running, and uninstall old or unused programs.
