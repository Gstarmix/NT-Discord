# Does the c91 armor's 'reduces damage to monsters in another dimension' stat lower my own damage?

**Q:** My c91 armor shows 'Reduces damage to all monsters within another dimension by 15%' (along with +288 dodge and +10300 HP). Does this mean I deal 15% less damage?

**A:** No, this is a defensive stat, not a penalty to your own outgoing damage — it reduces the damage you take from monsters belonging to another dimension. This is unrelated to the separate c92 gun issue, where that item's wording ended up reducing the wielder's own resistance instead of the opponent's.
