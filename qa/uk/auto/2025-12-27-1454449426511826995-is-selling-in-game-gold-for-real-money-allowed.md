# Is selling in-game gold for real money allowed?

**Q:** Can I sell in-game gold/money in Undercity in exchange for real-world currency ($)?

**A:** No — doing so is against the rules and can get your account banned. This differs from buying NosCash (Nos$) with real money, which is the official, sanctioned way to spend real currency in the game, since Gameforge already earns revenue from that purchase. Selling gold for real cash generates no revenue for Gameforge, which is why it's prohibited.
