# Why doesn't the Martial Artist block SP work like Assassin/Ninja block (charge lost on enemy miss)?

**Q:** For Martial Artist skills like Master Wolf and Mystic Arts, the block SP grants a benefit when an enemy's hit is blocked (ultimate bar charge for Master Wolf, a self-buff-amplifying buff for Mystic Arts). Unlike Assassin/Ninja block, which always grants its charge regardless of whether the enemy's attack actually connects, the Martial Artist block only triggers if the enemy's attack would have hit — if the enemy misses entirely, no benefit is granted. Why is this class's block designed differently, since it's very noticeable in PvP?

**A:** This is confirmed to be intended design, not a bug — a helper indicated this kind of change request belongs in the suggestions section rather than as a bug report, implying the current per-class block behavior (Assassin/Ninja always charging vs. Martial Artist only charging on an actual hit) is working as designed.
