# Fairy % power boost from Tropical Wings/Spirit Guide no longer visibly increases

**Q:** Why can't I see the fairy power % increase from the Tropical Wings and Spirit Guide anymore, whether in the character window or by right-clicking my character repeatedly? Does the bonus still apply even though I don't see the number go up?

**A:** The bonus damage still applies; only its visibility changed. Previously the fairy's power increase was recalculated on every server tick, so right-clicking your character at the exact right tick would show the updated percentage. It is now recalculated on every attack instead of every tick, which is why players no longer see it increment that way. Check the pinned message in the fairy channel for more detail.
