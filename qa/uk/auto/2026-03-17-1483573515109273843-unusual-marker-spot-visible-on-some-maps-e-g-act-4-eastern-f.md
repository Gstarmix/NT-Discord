# Unusual marker/spot visible on some maps (e.g. Act 4 Eastern Frostland)

**Q:** Why does the Act 4 map (Eastern Frostland) show an odd colored spot/marker that I've never noticed before, and that doesn't appear on the same map in NosVille?

**A:** That marker is the channel-select spot that exists on most maps; it's just normally hidden on maps that aren't "duplicated" (multi-channel) maps, which is why it looks new or out of place. This is a known, low-priority visual quirk with no fast fix planned — it is expected to remain as-is for now.
