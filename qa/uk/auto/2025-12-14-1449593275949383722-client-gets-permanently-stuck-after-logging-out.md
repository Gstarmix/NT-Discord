# Client gets permanently stuck after logging out

**Q:** After quitting NosTale, the client sometimes gets permanently stuck and you can't log back in — this is not the normal wait for character logout, it just hangs forever until the computer or service is restarted, and it has been happening frequently. Is there a fix?

**A:** This is a known client issue. Instead of restarting the whole computer, open Task Manager and kill any remaining NosTale process; if the game still won't let you log back in, also close the Gameforge Client window and kill its process as well.
