# NosTale window stays on top of other applications since last maintenance

**Q:** Since the last maintenance, the NosTale window always stays in front of other applications (e.g. League of Legends) even when it isn't focused, and minimizing it was the only workaround. Is there a fix?

**A:** Confirmed known issue caused by the Discord overlay. Turning off Discord's in-game overlay for NosTale fixes the always-on-top/focus-stealing behavior.
