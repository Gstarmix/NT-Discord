# Lord Melonoth raid counter for the 'Revenge of Melonlord' quest not increasing

**Q:** My raid count for the quest requiring 30 Lord Melonoth raids isn't increasing properly (I did 13 raids but only 3 are counted). Is this a bug and what should I do?

**A:** This is not a bug: the daily quest that requires 3 Lord Melonoth raids (rewarding 2 Perfis) takes priority and must be turned in first. Until that daily is completed and turned in, raids will not count toward the separate 30-raid quest counter. Once the daily is turned in, the other quest's counter starts progressing normally.
