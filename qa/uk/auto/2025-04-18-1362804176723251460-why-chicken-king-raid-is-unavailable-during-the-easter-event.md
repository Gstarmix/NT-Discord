# Why Chicken King raid is unavailable during the Easter event

**Q:** Why can I not enter the Chicken King raid while the Chicken Queen (Easter) raid is available?

**A:** This is intended behavior, not a bug. The Chicken King raid and the Chicken Queen Easter raid share the same map and portal, so for story/technical reasons the Chicken King raid is disabled for the duration of the Easter event and only the Chicken Queen raid can be entered.
