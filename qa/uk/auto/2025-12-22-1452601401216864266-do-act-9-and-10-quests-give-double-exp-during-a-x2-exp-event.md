# Do Act 9 and 10 quests give double EXP during a x2 EXP event?

**Q:** During a x2 EXP event, do Act 9 quests give double EXP like earlier act quests?

**A:** No, Act 9 and Act 10 quests do not receive the double EXP bonus during x2 EXP events, unlike quests from earlier acts.
