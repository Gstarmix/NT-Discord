**Q:** While using Druid skills in Jaguar speed form, I appeared invisible to other players even though I could still move around, though I couldn't cast/interact properly. What is causing this?

**A:** This is a known, long-standing client-side bug affecting the Druid's Jaguar transformation, reported to have existed for several years with no confirmed fix.
