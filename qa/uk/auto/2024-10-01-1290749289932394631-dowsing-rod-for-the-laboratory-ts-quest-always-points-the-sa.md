# Dowsing rod for the laboratory TS quest always points the same direction

**Q:** The dowsing rod used for the laboratory TS (Time Space) quests always points in the same direction and never updates. This seems to be a long standing bug. How can it be fixed?

**A:** This is a known old bug. A workaround is to change your keyboard's language in Windows settings to a different variant of the same language (for example, from English US to English UK), which reportedly fixes the dowsing rod direction.
