# How does elemental damage work in NosTale?

**Q:** How does the element system affect damage, and is there a way to calculate the bonus by percentage?

**A:** Elements follow a cycle where water is strong against light, light against fire, fire against dark, and dark against water, each giving a x1.5 damage multiplier. Light and dark are mutually strong against each other at x3 damage, and fire and water are mutually strong against each other at x2 damage.
