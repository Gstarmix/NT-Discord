# Does the Act 4 'warm body' drop bonus affect raid drop rates too?

**Q:** Does the Act 4 'warm body' feature (where drop chance decreases a lot once it runs out) also apply to raids? Testing it in a raid didn't seem to show the effect.

**A:** The warm body bonus applies as long as you are playing/online in Act 4, which would include raids entered from there. However, this wasn't fully confirmed inside raids specifically, since monsters killed inside a raid don't grant warm body points, making the effect harder to observe/test there.
