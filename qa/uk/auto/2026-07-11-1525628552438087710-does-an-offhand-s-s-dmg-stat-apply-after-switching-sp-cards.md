# Does an offhand's s.dmg% stat apply after switching SP cards?

**Q:** If I have s.dmg% on my mage's offhand weapon, does it still apply when I switch to playing a different SP card, like Seer?

**A:** No, stats like s.dmg% on the offhand do not carry over between SP cards. Only SL (stat level), bonus exp, and incantations apply consistently regardless of which SP card is active.
