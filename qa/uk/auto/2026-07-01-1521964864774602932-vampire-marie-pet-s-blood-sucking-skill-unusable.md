# Vampire Marie pet's blood sucking skill unusable

**Q:** Why can't I use my vampire Marie pet's blood sucking skill anymore?

**A:** This is a known bug: sometimes after your character dies and the pet uses a spell, the skill gets stuck. Removing the pet's special ability (PSP) transformation and re-equipping it usually fixes the issue.
