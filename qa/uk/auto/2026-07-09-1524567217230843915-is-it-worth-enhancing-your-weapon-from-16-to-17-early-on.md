# Is it worth enhancing your weapon from +16 to +17 early on?

**Q:** At what point should I start enhancing my weapon further? It's currently +16 and I'd like to get it to +17.

**A:** It's generally not considered worth investing more enhancement materials into a weapon at this stage. It's better to wait until you reach character level 65 and then swap to a new/upgraded weapon instead.
