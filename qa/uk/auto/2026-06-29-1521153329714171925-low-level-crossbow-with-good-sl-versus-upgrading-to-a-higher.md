# Low level crossbow with good SL versus upgrading to a higher level crossbow

**Q:** I have a lower level crossbow with really good stat lines (SL). Should I look for a higher level crossbow instead, or is it fine to keep the lower level one if I don't care about the Crusader stage? Does the crossbow's level affect damage output at all times, or only while actively attacking with it?

**A:** On the level 72 crossbow you essentially have two choices: keep it and lose the stat lines, or switch to a C25 crossbow and keep the stat lines but deal slightly less damage. Going with the C25 is generally recommended since it keeps damage more stable over the long run. Either way, the real goal is reaching C60 as soon as possible, since that is the point where you get both good stat lines and full damage.
