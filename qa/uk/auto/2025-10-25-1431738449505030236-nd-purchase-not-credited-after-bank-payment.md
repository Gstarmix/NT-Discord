# ND purchase not credited after bank payment

**Q:** I bought NosDollars (ND) and the money was withdrawn from my bank account, but I never received the ND. I opened a support ticket but got no response over the weekend. Is there a faster way to resolve this?

**A:** This is a known billing issue handled specifically by GameForge's billing support team, which only works weekdays (Monday to Friday) and not on weekends. If GameForge staff already replied on the ticket, there is nothing further to do but wait, no GM, moderator, or helper can speed up or bypass the billing support process.
