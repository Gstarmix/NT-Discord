# NosMall shows a blank white screen and won't open

**Q:** NosMall fails to open and shows a white screen on all my characters, even after reinstalling the game, restarting the computer, and using the launcher's repair function. Is there a fix?

**A:** This is a known issue with the in-game NosMall. As a workaround, clear your browser cache and use the shop button in the client to open NosMall in an external browser instead of the in-game overlay.
