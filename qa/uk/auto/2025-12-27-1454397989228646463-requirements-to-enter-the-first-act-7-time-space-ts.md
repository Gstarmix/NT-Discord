# Requirements to enter the first Act 7 Time Space (TS)

**Q:** What are the requirements to enter the first Time Space (TS) of Act 7, and where can I find a reliable list of Act 5 TS?

**A:** You need to have completed all previous Time Spaces from Act 1 through Act 5 before entering the first Act 7 TS. Note that Act 5 only has 3 Time Spaces. A reliable list of TS can be found at nosapki.com/en/ts.
