# Specialist life-steal: skill life-steal vs dagger life-steal

**Q:** For a life-steal-focused specialist build, is it better to rely on skill-based life-steal or on a dagger's life-steal, given that only certain specialist cards' skills (like the C45 skill) can trigger heal-on-hit?

**A:** Life-steal from skills is much weaker than life-steal from an equipped dagger. The C45 skill's heal is effectively about 50% dagger-based, which is what makes it worthwhile. Archer specialists reportedly have only one SP (skill) that benefits from this dagger-based heal, while swordsman specialists have several (around eight) skills that can make good use of it.
