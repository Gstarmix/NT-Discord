# Does finishing Act 5.1 take you from level 81 to level 85?

**Q:** After finishing Act 4 at level 81, does completing Act 5.1 bring you to level 85? How long does it take?

**A:** The Act 5.2 main questline (Volcano) gives no levels at all. Completing all of the Act 5.1 side quests (Desert) levels a character up to around level 86 and also rewards about 23kk gold.
