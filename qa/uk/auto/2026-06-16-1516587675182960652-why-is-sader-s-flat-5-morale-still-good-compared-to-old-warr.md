# Why is Sader's flat +5 morale still good compared to old Warrior morale

**Q:** Isn't the Warrior's morale skill superior? Why is Sader's flat +5 morale considered good in comparison?

**A:** The current Morale Increase buff (level 2) increases hit rate and dodge by Player Level times 2 for 300 seconds; it no longer gives the broader stat mix it once did. Historically, Warrior's morale skill gave a real, powerful mix of multiple stats (including hitrate and evasion) similar to Sader's, but it was considered overpowered, so it was changed to only grant hitrate and evasion at a reduced value. Sader's morale was left as a flat +5, which is modest but was never as excessive as the old Warrior morale, and at high-end character level the difference between the two becomes largely irrelevant; it may still matter more at lower levels.
