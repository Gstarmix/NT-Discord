# Can the Berserk Spirit buff's final stage be forced early?

**Q:** The Berserk Spirit disease buff has multiple stages (Weak, then stronger versions with escalating attack and shadow element bonuses tied to player level). Its tooltip says the effect ends if you take damage, but taking damage doesn't seem to actually end it. Is there a way to force the buff into its strongest stage instead of waiting out the full duration?

**A:** Taking damage does affect the buff, it just doesn't cancel it outright like the tooltip implies. Instead, each hit that triggers the buff's end condition cuts roughly 15 seconds off its remaining duration. So there is no way to instantly force the final, strongest stage, you either let hits whittle down the timer faster or wait out the full duration normally.
