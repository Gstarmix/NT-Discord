# What champion level should alt characters be leveled to?

**Q:** To what champion level are alt characters usually leveled, and for what purpose? Is C10 enough, or is it worth leveling further, e.g. to help with solo raids past Act 7?

**A:** There is no fixed target level; it depends on what you want to use the alt for. Alts are mainly leveled to farm boxes from specific raids you want to run with them. For example, if you want to use alts for Pollu, you'd level them to around C80/90.
