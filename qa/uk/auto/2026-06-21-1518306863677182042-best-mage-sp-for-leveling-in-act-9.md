# Best mage SP for leveling in Act 9

**Q:** Which SP is best for a mage leveling in Act 9?

**A:** Archmage is recommended for polluted maps with rats and slimes, while Gravity or Seer are better for robot and mullan maps, and possibly for Fire Storm as well.
