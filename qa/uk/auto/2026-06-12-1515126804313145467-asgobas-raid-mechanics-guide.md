# Asgobas raid mechanics guide

**Q:** Is there a guide for the Asgobas raid?

**A:** The host summons the raid with a stone and assigns players into 3 teams, each with a leader holding a orange dialogue bubble labeled with their team number (this matters most in the boss room, though some hosts also split into 2 groups for the second room). In the boss room, when Asgobas reaches roughly 75/50/25% HP he gains a 90% damage reduction buff and summons 6 dark pillars; each team must watch which pillar their own leader attacks and focus that same pillar, destroying 3 pillars quickly (one per team) or the raid wipes. After the 3 pillars are destroyed, wait at the map's center: Asgobas jumps and 4 colored fields (red, blue, yellow, black) appear at the corners/cardinal points. Each player is assigned one of these colors, shown via a buff icon, a colored dragon above their character, and a chat message, and must stand in their matching colored field before Asgobas lands or they will die.
