# Does the totem-healing removal also apply to the regular (Act 5) Celestial Spire, not just the Family Tower?

**Q:** The patch that removed the ability to heal pillars/totems in the Family Tower — does it also apply to the individual/regular Celestial Spire from the earlier act, since its totem no longer heals either?

**A:** Yes. According to the official Celestial Spire Catacombs patch notes, it is no longer possible to heal the totem in the regular Celestial Spire either — the change applies beyond just the Family Tower.
