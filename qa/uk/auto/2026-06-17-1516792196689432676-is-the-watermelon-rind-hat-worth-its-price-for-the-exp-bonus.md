# Is the Watermelon Rind Hat worth its price for the exp bonus?

**Q:** Is the Watermelon Rind Hat (with its 5+3% exp bonus) really worth around 115kk on UC, or is it mostly a collector item? Practically speaking, is it better for exp maps like A9/A10 or C80+ CLoL than headwear like Laurena's Disguise or the Mullan hats (which give atk%/SP property), or is 8% exp + 3% atk not as good long-term as raw atk stats?

**A:** The Watermelon Rind Hat is essentially a straight upgrade over Laurena's Disguise, and together they are the only headwear pieces that give an exp bonus. Whether it's worth the price depends on your current strength: if you don't struggle with cooldowns and already have enough damage for your leveling progress, the exp bonus is a solid pick, otherwise an atk-stat headwear may serve you better.
