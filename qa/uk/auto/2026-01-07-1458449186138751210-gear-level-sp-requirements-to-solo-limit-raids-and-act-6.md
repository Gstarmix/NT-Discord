# Gear/level/SP requirements to solo Limit raids and Act 6

**Q:** At what character level, gear, and SP setup could you reasonably solo Limit raids and Act 6 raids without it taking an hour per run? Would c60+ with a level 99+15 weapon and high perfection SP be needed, or is c30-60 with lower SP (+11/13, with or without perfection) enough?

**A:** For Limit raids, aim for around character level 60 with high-perfection SP. For gear, a level 55 or 60 item is fine (whichever is cheaper). The recommended SP combo depends on class: Archer uses Scout + Blaster/Canon, Swordsman uses Ninja/Monk/Waterzerk + either Zerk, War, or Stonebreaker, and Mage uses Gravity or Icemage + Vulcano, Redmage, or Seer. Act 6 raids were technically soloable at c60, but require good damage output — it's preferable to wait until around c80-83 for a smoother solo experience.
