# Best tattoo choice for a mage

**Q:** What is the best tattoo setup for a mage?

**A:** For a mage using a wand, the recommended tattoos are Attack Stance and Attack Magic (Arcane Wisdom). For a mage using a gun, use Attack Stance and Piercing Gaze instead.
