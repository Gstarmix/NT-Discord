# Best use of a 2x drop event for a low-level (c61) archer

**Q:** What is the best thing a c61 archer can do to take advantage of a 2x drop rate event?

**A:** At low level there isn't much you can farm efficiently, but there is a lifestyle specialty (chef) that requires many crafting materials to cook. Some of these materials drop in Act 3 areas (Maple/Fir Forest and surroundings) and are relatively rare and valuable, so focusing on farming those during the event is worthwhile. If strong enough, also consider farming some Act 9 materials if they have good value.
