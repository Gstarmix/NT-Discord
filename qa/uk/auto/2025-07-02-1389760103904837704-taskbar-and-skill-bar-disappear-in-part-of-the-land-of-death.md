# Taskbar and skill bar disappear in part of the Land of Death map

**Q:** In a certain part of the Land of Death map, the tab menu and skill bar disappear when walking to the right side. Is this a known bug and how to fix it?

**A:** Known issue. As a workaround, try pressing Ctrl+Z to restore the UI; if that doesn't work, restart the client. Some players also recommend picking up everything from the floor each time it happens, which seems to help avoid or fix the glitch.
