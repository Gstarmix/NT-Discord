# Does a pet keep its extra trainer level when transferred with a pet bead?

**Q:** Do pets keep their extra trainer (+) level when transferred to another character using a pet bead?

**A:** No, the trainer level resets to 0 when transferring a pet; only the star stage is kept. Note that 6-star pets can only be transferred using a pet trainer bead, which is roughly ten times more expensive than a regular pet bead.
