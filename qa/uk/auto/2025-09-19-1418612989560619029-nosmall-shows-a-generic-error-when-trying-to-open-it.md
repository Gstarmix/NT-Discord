# NosMall shows a generic error when trying to open it

**Q:** NosMall displays a generic 'An error has occurred' popup and fails to open. How can this be fixed?

**A:** This is usually related to connectivity or a client/server language mismatch. Suggested fixes include restarting your router or switching to a different internet connection, and making sure the server language selection matches your account flag's language before retrying.
