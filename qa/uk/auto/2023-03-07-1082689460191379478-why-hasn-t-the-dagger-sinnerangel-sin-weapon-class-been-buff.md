# Why hasn't the dagger (Sinnerangel/Sin) weapon class been buffed

**Q:** Are there any plans to buff the dagger (Sin) weapon class, since other classes have received balance changes but dagger hasn't?

**A:** According to a developer, dagger is very hard to balance because it already has extremely high critical damage values; bringing its other stats in line with other weapons would make it excessively overpowered. No dagger buff was planned at the time, though further changes for Archer (AA) and Renegade were being considered after Warrior received attention.
