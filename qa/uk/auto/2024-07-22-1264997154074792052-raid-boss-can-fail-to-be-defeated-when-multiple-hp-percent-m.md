# Raid boss can fail to be defeated when multiple HP-percent mechanics trigger at once

**Q:** In a raid with double box, a strange result/failure occurred after damaging a boss. What is causing this and how can it be avoided?

**A:** This happens when the boss is burst down fast enough that more than one HP-percentage-based mechanic triggers at the same time. It is a known issue, most commonly seen in the Ultimate Arma raid but also occurring in other raids, and a fix is still being investigated. There is currently no confirmed workaround besides avoiding extremely fast burst damage on affected bosses.
