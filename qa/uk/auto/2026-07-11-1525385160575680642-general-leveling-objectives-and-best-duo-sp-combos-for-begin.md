# General leveling objectives and best duo SP combos for beginners

**Q:** As a beginner without a guide, what are some general leveling objectives (levels to reach, when to stop and farm SP/gear, etc.)? Also, what are good duo SP combos for two real players leveling together?

**A:** From level 1 to 55, just follow the main quest, no purchases needed. At 55 you receive the starter pack with free full gear and your first SPs. The main quest then carries you to 80, where the game effectively starts. From 80 to 81 do the Act 4 quests, then Act 5.1 quests take you from 81 to 85; grind LOD (the Act 2 map) alongside these for your first real gear and gold, and Infected Camp (IC) also opens at 80. After that come heroic levels: Act 6 takes you from C1 to C10, Act 7 to C30, Act 8 to C90. Avoid spending gold on gear before 80, since the starter gear suffices; save gold for SPs and Act 5.1 gear instead. For a two-player duo, Swordsman + Mage is the strongest pairing: Swordsman goes Warrior to tank aggro and hold packs, Mage goes Holy Mage to AoE everything down, and this combo still works at heroic levels. Archer also works if one player wants ranged, starting Wild Keeper then switching to Demon Hunter for AoE. Even as a duo, expect to eventually roll an alt or two each, since buffs and LOD runs go much more smoothly with three characters per player. A full act-by-act progression guide with SP recommendations exists at nostar.fr/en/progress.
