# Time limit for Cloister of Lost Landscapes (CLOL)

**Q:** Is CLOL (Cloister of Lost Landscapes) bugged? I can't seem to do more than one run without getting kicked out.

**A:** This is not a bug. You have a total of 130 minutes of CLOL access per day: 1 hour at normal time and 1 hour at Asgobas time. Once the entrance closes and time runs out you get kicked and must re-enter.
