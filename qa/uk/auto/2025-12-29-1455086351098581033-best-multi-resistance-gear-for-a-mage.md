# Best multi-resistance gear for a mage

**Q:** As a mage (currently class level 45, willing to reach a higher class level eventually, with a budget of around 300kk), which multi-resistance items should I aim for?

**A:** If money isn't a strict concern, top options are the Act 6 Zenas/Erenia bots or S5 globes, which grant 120% resistance to all elements; Act 7 Carno/Kirollas items give the same bonus. These options are relatively costly and may exceed a tighter budget like 300kk, so cheaper alternatives would be needed in that case.
