# Why does swordsman feel stronger than mage in the current PvP balance?

**Q:** Why does swordsman seem overpowered compared to mage in PvP, beyond just equipment differences?

**A:** The imbalance is not only about equipment. Mages struggle because their shields have been halved, while most swordsman SPs come with antimalus effects, some with long shields that also heal and grant antimalus (such as SP10), or an armor rune that heals a large amount of HP every 6 seconds. Combined with the heavy maluses swordsmen can inflict, these small advantages add up to an unfair matchup against mages.
