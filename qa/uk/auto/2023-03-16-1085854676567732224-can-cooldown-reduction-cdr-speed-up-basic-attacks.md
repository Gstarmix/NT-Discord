# Can Cooldown Reduction (CDR) speed up basic attacks?

**Q:** I thought a recent patch removed the ability to reduce basic attack cooldowns. Does Attack Speed or CDR still affect how fast basic attacks fire?

**A:** Basic (auto) attack cooldown cannot be reduced by the CDR (cooldown reduction) stat. If your basic attacks feel fast, that is due to your Attack Speed stat, not CDR.
