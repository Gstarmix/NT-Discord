# Best leveling spot with strong level 83+ gear: Hell's Gate or Ancelloan's Will 3

**Q:** Should I level from combat level 1 in Ancelloan's Will 3, or is Hell's Gate better if I already have solid level 83 gear?

**A:** Hell's Gate is now considered much better and easier, since the monsters there were severely nerfed, so anyone with basic level 85 gear can clear it. If going to Ancelloan's Will / Hell Ruins 2 instead, it's recommended to lure only a few mobs at a time on the left part of the map if you have a group, enough damage, patience, and a taunt (voke) pet, otherwise fight mob by mob in area 6.2.
