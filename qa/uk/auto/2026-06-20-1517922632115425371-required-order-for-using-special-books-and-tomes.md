# Required order for using special books and tomes

**Q:** Do special books need to be used in a specific order, for example does Fairy Affinity 1 need to be used before Fairy Affinity 2?

**A:** Yes, most book lines must be used in sequence: Attack Strategy 1 then 2, Defense Strategy 1 then 2, Forgotten Heroes D/S/J/M 1 then 2 (each within its own letter), Unknown Hero 1 then 2, Fairy Affinity 1 then 2 then 3, Roshu's Primordial History then Forgotten Dimension Book of Experience, Advanced then Expert Fruit Research (Summer Event), Basic then Advanced then Expert Training for Chefs (Chef SP), and Pet Trainer's Manual 1 then 2 then 3 (Pet Tamer SP). The stat books (Loa Tome > Dragon Textbook > Techron's Research > Forgotten Dimension Book, one line per stat: Strength, Agility, Intelligence, Power, Magic) also follow their own order but are independent from the other lines and don't need to be used in relation to them.
