# Is there any reason to pick Wisemen King (WK) over Avenging Angel (AA)?

**Q:** Avenging Angel (AA) seems to have a better kit and AoEs, with the only drawback being the lack of WK's resurrection skill (which can be worked around with a buffbot in Miniland). Is there still a reason to choose WK over AA?

**A:** WK is generally considered better overall: it deals good damage while also having the game's best buffs and a debuff skill that can apply up to two debuffs, one of them at level 5. AAs that appear to outperform WKs usually do so because of gear availability rather than the class itself: daggers (AA's secondary weapon) have fewer players investing in them, so good dagger gear is cheaper and easier to obtain than gear for the more popular weapons (bow, sword, and especially wand), not because AA is inherently stronger.
