# How the elemental avoidance task order works in dimensional missions

**Q:** In the dimensional mission tasks where you must stand in a specific element to avoid damage, if the next task is the 'opposite' one, does that mean I can go into any of the three elements I previously avoided, or specifically the exact opposite element of the one I just used?

**A:** The 'opposite' follow-up task means going to the exact opposite element of the one used for the previous task, not simply any of the other three elements that were avoided before. Players confirmed this by directly pointing to the specific opposite element (for example fire) rather than accepting the broader interpretation.
