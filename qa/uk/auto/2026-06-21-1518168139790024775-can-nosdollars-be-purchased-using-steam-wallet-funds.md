# Can NosDollars be purchased using Steam Wallet funds?

**Q:** Can NosDollars (ND) be purchased with Steam Wallet funds?

**A:** Yes, NosDollars can be purchased via Steam Wallet. The only difference is the bonus ND given with the official GF Client shop versus the Steam version: for example, a 200 euro purchase gives 5100+200 ND through the GF Client but only 5100 ND through Steam.
