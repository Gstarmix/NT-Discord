# Equipped item render can get stuck floating on screen after crossing a portal

**Q:** What causes an equipped item (e.g. wings) to appear stuck, rendered large in the middle of the screen, after crossing a portal?

**A:** This visual glitch is suspected to occur when two players cross the same portal at the same time, which can leave an equipped item's render (e.g. wings) stuck floating on screen instead of properly attaching to the character. This wasn't fully confirmed as the sole cause, but matched what happened in the reported instance.
