# Where do produced Ginseng Raid Seals go?

**Q:** I produced 2 Ginseng Raid Seals at level 54 but cannot find them in my regular inventory. Where are they stored?

**A:** Raid Seals such as the Ginseng Raid Seal are not placed in the regular inventory. They are stored in the dedicated raid inventory tab.
