# Forga (low tier) vs Yuna (S tier) for cexp gain

**Q:** Would a Forga partner (only C or D tier) give better cexp than an S-tier Yuna (with S heal)?

**A:** Experience gain is fairly close between most partners overall, but tier still matters: a low-tier (C) Forga performs worse for cexp than a high-tier (S) Yuna. The same logic applies to other partners like Feraspa.
