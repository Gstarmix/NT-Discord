# Quest item collection stuck at 0/0 on new swapable maps

**Q:** Quest items seem bugged on the new swapable maps: I've been doing the 'Energy Transfer' quest at Spirit Forest Edge (collecting Spirit Forest Branch from Sylvan Spirit and Loa Runic Powder from Guardian Spirit) for over two hours and my progress is still stuck at 0/0.

**A:** This is a long known issue affecting quest item drop tracking on the new swapable maps. To work around it, make sure you don't have any other active drop-collection quests at the same time, then retry the quest.
