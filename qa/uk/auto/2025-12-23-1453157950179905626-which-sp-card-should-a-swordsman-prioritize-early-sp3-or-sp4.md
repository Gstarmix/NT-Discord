# Which SP card should a Swordsman prioritize early, SP3 or SP4?

**Q:** For a Swordsman early on, which SP card is better to rush first, SP3 or SP4?

**A:** SP4 is used in almost every situation, making it the priority card to rush. SP3 is more situational, being mainly useful for Land of Death, raids, and leveling from character level 1-30. For pets in the early game, use the daily reward pets you get for free, and aim to get an otter or panda later on.
