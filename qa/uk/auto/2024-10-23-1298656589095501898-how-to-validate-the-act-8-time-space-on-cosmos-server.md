# How to validate the act 8 time space on Cosmos server

**Q:** There is a problem validating a time space quest in act 8 on the Cosmos server. What is needed to finish it?

**A:** If it is the 3rd time space of act 8, a Guardian's Magic Orb is required to complete it. Without this item the time space cannot be validated.
