# Are Act 7 side quests worth doing, and why can't some be started yet

**Q:** Is it worth doing the Act 7 side quests, or should they be skipped? Also, why can't a specific Act 7 mission (Trial of the Orc Warrior) be started?

**A:** The mission cannot be started because it requires having completed the time-spaces (not just the story quests) from Act 1 through Act 5 first; a mission screen even shows "Cannot be done because of low act" until that requirement is met. Beyond that, most Act 7 side quests are considered a waste of time since a character can exp up to around c80 without needing them. The worthwhile exceptions are the daily quests located near the raid area, which give raid seals, kiro fragments, and obsidians (obsidian dailies are best farmed by spamming them across multiple alt characters).
