# Is the 'final damage' tattoo bonus multiplicative, and how do Piercing Gaze and Eagle tattoos compare at +9?

**Q:** Is the 'increase final damage by X%' effect multiplicative on the total damage pool, or just a small flat addition to the crit percentage? At +9, how do the Piercing Gaze and Eagle tattoos compare in terms of crit chance, final damage, and hit rate?

**A:** The final damage bonus is understood to scale with the whole damage total rather than being a small flat addition, so a high existing damage pool makes a percentage bonus like this much more impactful than it first appears. At +9, Piercing Gaze gives 5% critical chance and 25% final damage, while Eagle gives 10% critical chance and 90% hit rate instead of extra final damage. Which is better depends on how much raw damage and crit stacking a player already has in endgame.
