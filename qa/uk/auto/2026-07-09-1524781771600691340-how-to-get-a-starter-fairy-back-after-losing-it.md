# How to get a starter fairy back after losing it

**Q:** I sold or lost my starter fairy after being away from the game for a long time. Can I obtain it again, and is it only obtainable once?

**A:** Create a new level 56 character, which will receive its own starter fairy, then transfer that fairy to your main character through the family/shared warehouse.
