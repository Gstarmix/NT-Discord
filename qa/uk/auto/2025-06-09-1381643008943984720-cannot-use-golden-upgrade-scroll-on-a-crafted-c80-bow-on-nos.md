# Cannot use golden upgrade scroll on a crafted C80 bow on NosFire

**Q:** On NosFire, I cannot upgrade a C80 bow with a golden scroll from the battle pass, the game says the item needs to be assigned to my character, even though I can equip and use the bow with no problem. What is going on?

**A:** This is a known bug: a weapon crafted below rune level 5 is not automatically bound to your character, and the game requires the weapon to be bound before a limited/golden scroll can be applied to it. The workaround is to equip and wear the weapon first so it becomes bound, then the scroll can be used on it. If you are unlucky and only get R4 or lower from crafting, you must wear the item first before scrolls will work.
