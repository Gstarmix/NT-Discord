# Where to level a c80 SP Serker after Ice Dungeon stops giving XP

**Q:** Where can an 80c SP Serker (Serker specialist) go to gain experience after Ice Dungeon stops giving XP? Is Land of Oblivion (LoL) worth it even though its mobs are 7 character levels higher?

**A:** Land of Oblivion is a viable option even though its mobs are about 7 levels higher — you'll only receive around 10% less experience than fighting on-level mobs. This penalty shrinks slightly further after reaching c81, then grows again at higher levels.
