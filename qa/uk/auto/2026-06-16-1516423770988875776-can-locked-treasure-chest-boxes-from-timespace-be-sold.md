# Can locked treasure chest boxes from Timespace be sold?

**Q:** Can the locked treasure chest boxes obtained from Timespace (Normal/Rare/Legendary Treasure Chests) be sold?

**A:** These boxes can be sold, but in practice only legendary treasure chests find buyers. Normal and rare treasure chests are generally not bought by other players, so it's better to sell those directly to NPCs.
