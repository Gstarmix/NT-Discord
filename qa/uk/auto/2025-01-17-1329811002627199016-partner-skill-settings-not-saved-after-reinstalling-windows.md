# Partner skill settings not saved after reinstalling Windows

**Q:** After reinstalling Windows, the NosTale launcher no longer remembers my partner skill settings. How can this be fixed?

**A:** Partner skill settings are stored locally in the client rather than being tied to the account on the server. As a result, they always reflect whatever was set for the last account logged out from on that client, and a fresh Windows or client install resets them. There is no known fix beyond reconfiguring the settings again after reinstalling.
