# Tombola not showing buy/redeem buttons and no prize received (known issue)

**Q:** How is the Tombola event supposed to work? For two days in a row, no interactive button appears — only an item icon hint (e.g. 'Drunken Octopus Stew') — and no prize is received in inventory or mailbox.

**A:** Normally the Tombola should display blue ticket icons, each with a 'Buy with X jewels' button; after purchase it turns into a green 'Redeem' button that reveals the prize. As of end of July 2025 this is a known issue for some players — the buttons fail to appear and no prize is granted, confirmed as an existing bug by another affected player. No workaround was given.
