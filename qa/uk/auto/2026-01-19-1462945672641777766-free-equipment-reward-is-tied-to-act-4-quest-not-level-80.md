# Free equipment reward is tied to Act 4 quest, not level 80

**Q:** I was told I would get free equipment at level 80, but I didn't receive anything — did I miss something?

**A:** The free equipment is not awarded simply for reaching level 80; it is given after completing the Act 4 questline (the ice-themed act). You reportedly need to be around level 81 to complete the final time square/quest step of that line.
