# What to invest in as a level 35 Archer with a 1 billion gold budget

**Q:** As a level 35 Archer with about 1 billion gold, excluding gear and SP upgrades, what should I invest in next?

**A:** Save your gold and use cheap, minimalist experience-focused gear while leveling up to character level 99, then invest in proper equipment once you get there. If you don't already own it, the Laurena Hat is also a worthwhile buy.
