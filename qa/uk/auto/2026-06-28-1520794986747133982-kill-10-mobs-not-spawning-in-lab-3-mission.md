# Kill-10 mobs not spawning in Lab 3 mission

**Q:** For a lab mission requiring killing 10 mobs that are supposed to spawn in the middle of Lab 3, the mobs sometimes don't spawn at all. What's causing this and how can it be fixed?

**A:** The mobs for this kill-10 mission are supposed to spawn in a circle in the middle of Lab 3 when you stand inside it, but this can bug out and they fail to appear. Suggested fixes are to relog or switch to a different channel, as the spawn issue is sometimes channel-specific.
