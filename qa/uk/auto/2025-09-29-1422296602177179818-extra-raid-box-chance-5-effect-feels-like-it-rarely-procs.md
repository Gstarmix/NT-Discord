# Extra raid box chance (5%) effect feels like it rarely procs

**Q:** Is the 5% chance for an additional raid box (e.g. from a mini pet effect) working correctly? I used to get an extra raid box almost every Mara run in Act 9, but since a new mini pet came out I've gotten zero extras. Is this bugged?

**A:** The effect is not bugged, it's just a low proc-rate bonus so on its own it duplicates rewards infrequently — this matches experience with other items/titles carrying the same 5% effect. Such effects become noticeably more useful when stacked with other sources of the same bonus rather than relying on a single 5% source. A dry streak of several runs with no proc is considered normal bad luck, not a bug.
