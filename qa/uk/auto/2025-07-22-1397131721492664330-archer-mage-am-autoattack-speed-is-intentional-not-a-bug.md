# Archer Mage (AM) autoattack speed is intentional, not a bug

**Q:** Why does Archer Mage's autoattack (with a total cast time + cooldown of 0.9s) hit slower than other classes' cards listed at 1 or 1.1 seconds? Is this a known bug that isn't being fixed?

**A:** This is not a bug but an intentional, longstanding design/animation characteristic. The skill's listed values are casting time and cooldown (unavailability window), not a guaranteed attack-every-X-seconds rate, and nothing states you'll attack exactly every 0.9s. SP attack speeds/damage were deliberately rebalanced around 2022 (affecting ~80% of SPs, including AM) to keep DPS balanced across classes regardless of animation or cast/cooldown split. Some players argue this still disadvantages higher-cast-time SPs (more vulnerable to interrupts/range loss, especially under higher ping), but staff maintain the current values are intended.
