# Best SP cards for leveling a Martial Artist from character level 1 to 60

**Q:** Which SP cards are good for leveling a Martial Artist (MA) from character level 1 to 60? Is Thunderer really good, even for the Land of Oblivion (LoL)?

**A:** Thunderer is recommended as a strong SP choice for a Martial Artist from level 1 all the way up to level 99, and works well pretty much everywhere. However, for the Land of Oblivion specifically, Demon Warrior is considered a better choice than Thunderer.
