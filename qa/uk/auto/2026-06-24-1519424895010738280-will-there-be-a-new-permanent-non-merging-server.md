# Will there be a new permanent, non-merging server

**Q:** Will there ever be a brand new server (or a classic version) that stays long-lived without eventually being merged into other servers?

**A:** This is considered unlikely. The regular/classic server concept was abandoned after four such servers collapsed in population and were eventually merged into what is now Valehir (part of Undercity). The team has instead focused on the Nosfire format, which has proven more successful and better adopted by the community. There also isn't enough sustained demand for a permanently unmerged server, since populations tend to decline and players end up asking for a merge anyway.
