# Character transfer between servers via Battlepass

**Q:** Is it possible to transfer a character between servers, and how does the process work?

**A:** Character transfers are tied to the Battlepass: you need to reach a certain Battlepass level to unlock the transfer. In the past, only equipped gear could be transferred over, not the inventory. The process can be difficult to complete without game knowledge and enough free time.
