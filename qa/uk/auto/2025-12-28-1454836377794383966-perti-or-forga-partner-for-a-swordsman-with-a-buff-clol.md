# Perti or Forga partner for a Swordsman with A Buff (cLoL)?

**Q:** For a Swordsman C30+ using an A-buff partner, is Perti or Forga better, especially for cLoL?

**A:** Choose Perti if you need more survivability, since it provides a useful passive buff effect. Choose Forga if you're prioritizing more experience gain and damage output.
