# Why can't I write in the Undercity Bazar chat channel

**Q:** Why can't I write in the Undercity Bazar chat channel? Should I use the spam channel instead, and where can I see my current level and experience?

**A:** You need to reach level 5 before you are allowed to post in the Undercity Bazar chat. Chat experience is also capped, so you can only earn a maximum amount of exp per minute regardless of how much you type.
