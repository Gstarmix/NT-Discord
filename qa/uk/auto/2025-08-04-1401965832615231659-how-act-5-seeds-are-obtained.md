# How Act 5 seeds are obtained

**Q:** Where do Act 5 seeds come from? None spawned in the Eastern Path map for about 30 minutes.

**A:** Act 5 seeds only spawn as drops from mini-bosses. Alternatively, players can exchange raid tokens directly for seeds instead of waiting for a spawn.
