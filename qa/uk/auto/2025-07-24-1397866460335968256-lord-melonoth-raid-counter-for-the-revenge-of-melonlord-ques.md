# Lord Melonoth raid counter for the 'Revenge of Melonlord' quest not increasing

**Q:** The quest 'Revenge of the Melonlord' isn't tracking raids correctly — it shows 8/30 raids completed even though I've finished the raid 15 times and hold 15 marks. Is this a known bug?

**A:** This is not a bug: the related daily quest has priority, and raid completions won't count toward the 30-raid quest total until that daily has been turned in. Completing raids while the daily is still pending (even many times) won't increase the 30-raid counter.
