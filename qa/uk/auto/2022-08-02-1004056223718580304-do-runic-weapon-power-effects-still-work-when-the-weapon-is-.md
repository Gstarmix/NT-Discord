# Do runic weapon power effects still work when the weapon is damaged?

**Q:** If a weapon's runic power upgrade fails (e.g. going from +7 10% attack to +8 13% attack) and the weapon becomes damaged as a result, do the rune's stat effects still apply while using the weapon, even without repairing it?

**A:** Yes, the rune's effect remains active even on a damaged weapon. A damaged (broken) rune only prevents further enhancement attempts; it does not disable the currently active bonus.
