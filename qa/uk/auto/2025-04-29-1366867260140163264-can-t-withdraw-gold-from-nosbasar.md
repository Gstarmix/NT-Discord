# Can't withdraw gold from NosBasar

**Q:** Why is it not possible to take/withdraw gold from the NosBasar shop?

**A:** A known workaround is to list a new random item for sale for 1 gold in the NosBasar and then try withdrawing the gold again, this typically resolves the issue.
