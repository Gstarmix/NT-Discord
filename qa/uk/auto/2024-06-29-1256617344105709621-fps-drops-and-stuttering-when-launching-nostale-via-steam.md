# FPS drops and stuttering when launching NosTale via Steam

**Q:** The game runs smoothly when launched through Gameforge, but launching it through Steam causes severe FPS drops and stuttering. What causes this?

**A:** This is usually caused by mismatched settings between the two installations. Open NtConfig.exe separately in both the Steam installation folder and the Gameforge client folder, and make sure settings such as the FPS limit are identical in both.
