# Converting worn gear into partner equipment

**Q:** Can a piece of gear that has already been worn by another player be converted into partner (mate/pet) equipment, or is a perfume required first?

**A:** Yes, gear that has already been worn by another player can still be converted into partner equipment, no perfume is needed for this.
