# Which SP card should an Archer use for PvE leveling?

**Q:** As an Archer doing PvE, which SP card should I use? I already got War King +9 for free from a quest and I'm currently at level 83.

**A:** War King can be used for pretty much all activities (except Land of Oblivion) until you obtain a Dark Head SP, generally around class level 30. Since Dark Head uses a dagger, it's worth aiming for Avenging Angel next, as it pairs well with that weapon. Keep War King as your main until you get Dark Head, then use Avenging Angel for Land of Oblivion up to around class level 90.
