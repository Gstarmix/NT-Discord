# Why some clients show "demon damage" instead of "monster damage"

**Q:** In some non-English clients (e.g. Turkish, Spanish), a stat is labeled "demon dmg" — what does it actually mean, and is it related to Act 6?

**A:** The stat commonly called "monster dmg" (damage vs. society/regular monsters) was mistranslated in the Turkish and Spanish clients as "demon dmg". Because of this translation error, many players believed for years that the stat specifically boosted damage against Act 6 (demon) content, but it is actually a general monster-damage stat unrelated to Act 6.
