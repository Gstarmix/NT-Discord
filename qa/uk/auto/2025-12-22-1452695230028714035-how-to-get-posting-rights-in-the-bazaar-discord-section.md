# How to get posting rights in the Bazaar Discord section

**Q:** How does one get the rights to post or reply in the Bazaar section of the Discord?

**A:** You need to gain Discord experience by being active in the server: level 5 is required to post new threads, and level 10 is required to edit or reply to them.
