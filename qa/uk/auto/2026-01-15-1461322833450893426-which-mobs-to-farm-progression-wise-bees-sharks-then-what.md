# Which mobs to farm progression-wise: bees, sharks, then what?

**Q:** Up to what character level should you farm bees before switching to sharks, and what comes after sharks?

**A:** Bees are worth farming up to around character level 63, after which sharks become the better target (roughly the 60-70 level range). As a general rule, target mobs close to your own character level. After sharks, around level 72-73, the next farming spot is the Ice Dungeon.
