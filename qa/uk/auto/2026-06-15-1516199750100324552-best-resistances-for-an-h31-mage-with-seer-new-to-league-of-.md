# Best resistances for an H31 Mage with Seer new to League of Lions (LoL)

**Q:** As an H31 Mage with Seer new to League of Lions, what resistances should I aim for? Previously I used Honour Gloves and Shoes giving 120% Fire and Shadow resistance.

**A:** Early LoL shrooms deal fire and light damage first, with water stags appearing later, so prioritize fire and light resistance initially. A cheap option is Hazerumble and Roob gear with Fairy Affinity and an armor bonus, which pushes you above 100% fire/light resistance. Alternatively, invest stat points in the Element stat (100 points gives 15% all resistance), buy S-grade all-resistance armor (20 to 27%), and combine it with Fairy Affinity gloves/shoes (30% each) for roughly 60% resistance to any element.
