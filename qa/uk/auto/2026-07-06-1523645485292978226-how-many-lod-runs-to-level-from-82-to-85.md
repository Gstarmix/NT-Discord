# How many LoD runs to level from 82 to 85?

**Q:** How many Land of Death (LoD) runs does it take to level from 82 to 85 without significant EXP buffs, when afk leeching on full maps while being carried by a strong main character?

**A:** It depends heavily on the setup used (soloing, duoing with alts, or leeching off a carry). For an 82 character afk leeching on full LoD maps while carried by a strong main, expect roughly 20 runs, about 1h45 total. The leeching character's own level barely changes this, since clear speed is gated by mob respawn timers (around 5 minutes per lap for most mobs), though Death Knights respawn every 10 minutes and give a lot of XP, so the real number is likely a bit above 20 runs. An XP simulator (available on nostar.fr) can be used with your own gear to refine the estimate.
