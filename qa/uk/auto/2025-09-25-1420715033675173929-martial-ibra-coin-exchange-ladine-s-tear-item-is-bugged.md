# Martial Ibra coin exchange / Ladine's Tear item is bugged

**Q:** What's the status of the Martial class Ibra coin equipment exchange? Ladine's Tear appears to be exchangeable for the Cold Metal Gauntlet, but this seems bugged and also appears to be the wrong item for the intended adjustment (Cold Metal Gauntlet was meant to match the Ibra equipment level). Martial-class gear from Ibra raids is also still capped at level 88-89, putting the class at a disadvantage.

**A:** As of this date, staff acknowledged they are aware of and checking the Ladine's Tear / Cold Metal Gauntlet exchange bug, but confirmed it likely would not be fixed within that week. No workaround was given, and the Martial class Ibra raid gear level issue remained unresolved at the time.
