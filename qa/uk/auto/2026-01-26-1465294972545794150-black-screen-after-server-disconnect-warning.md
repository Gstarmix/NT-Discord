# Black screen after server disconnect warning

**Q:** Since a certain point I keep getting a warning that the server will log me out, and afterward I can join a channel normally but only get a black screen. If someone invites me to Miniland I regain limited access to my account and the screen loads again. Is there a fix?

**A:** This is a known issue that has been discussed before in the help channel. A workaround is to have another player teleport you via a raid stone, or to have someone invite you to their Miniland, which restores the screen. It may also be worth checking your Windows display/input language settings, as that has been linked to similar display issues.
