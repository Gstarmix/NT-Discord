# Which hat and mask to use while leveling an Archer

**Q:** Regarding accessories, I currently use the Laurena Hat and Azrael Mask. Should I get something else now, or wait until level 90 to switch to different pieces?

**A:** Masks are one of the least developed gear slots, there is no real PvE mask option before the level 90 Miner Mask, so it's fine to keep using what you have until then. For hats, you'll eventually want to rely less on Laurena and shift to normal (non-costume) hats, to keep your stats consistent and better judge when you actually need extra damage.
