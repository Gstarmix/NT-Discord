# How does the Asgobas Instance Challenge (IC) work?

**Q:** Is there a guide on how the Asgobas Instance Challenge works? What attacks does he use and what should I watch out for?

**A:** Asgobas IC has 4 waves, granting one defensive battle for finishing last round or winning the IC, plus three defenses of the dragonveil chest (one per round). His attacks include a typical close range hit, red circles on the map marking where he will jump to, a scream attack that spawns purple swirls on players' positions before a lightning strike hits after a few seconds (dealing heavy damage and applying debuffs like a temporary attack lock, similar to a skill reused from Laurena's raid), and occasionally a breath attack with a heavy debuff. Each round gives exp, champion exp, and some job exp along with chest rewards, dropping dratanium, dragon bones and scales, dragon health and mana potions, and gold (20k per round, with a 30k bonus stack after the final round). From level 5 debuffs onward the fight stacks fairly consistently in difficulty; level 4 and below is rougher but still doable for classes like elemental Wrathkeeper.
