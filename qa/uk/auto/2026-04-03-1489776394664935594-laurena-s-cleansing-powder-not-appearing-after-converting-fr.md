# Laurena's Cleansing Powder not appearing after converting from shards

**Q:** I converted shards into Laurena's Cleansing Powder but didn't receive the item in my regular inventory, what happened?

**A:** This is not a bug: like Act 8 raid potions, Laurena's Cleansing Powder obtained this way is stored in the raid inventory rather than your normal inventory.
