# New level requirements to post in EU Undercity Bazaar

**Q:** Why can't I write messages/threads in the eu-undercity-bazaar channel?

**A:** The same rules that already applied to the Dragonveil bazaar channel were extended to the Undercity bazaar: you need to be level 5 to create a new thread/post, and level 10 to comment/reply in an existing thread.
