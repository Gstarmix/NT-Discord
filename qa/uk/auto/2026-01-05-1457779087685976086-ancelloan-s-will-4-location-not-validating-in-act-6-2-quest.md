# Ancelloan's Will 4 location not validating in Act 6.2 quest

**Q:** During the Act 6.2 quest 'Ancelloan the Creator and Fernon the Destroyer - Explore Ancelloan's Will', going to the marked location (Ancelloan's Will 4) with the Dowsing Rod doesn't validate the objective. Why?

**A:** You need to use the Shining Dowsing Rod, obtained from an Act 6 NPC, instead of the regular Dowsing Rod for the location to validate.
