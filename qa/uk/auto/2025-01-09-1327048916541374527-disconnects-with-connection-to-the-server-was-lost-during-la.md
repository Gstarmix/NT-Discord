# Disconnects with 'Connection to the server was lost' during Land of Life

**Q:** The game sometimes closes with a 'Connection to the server was lost, NosTale will now close' message while playing Land of Life, even with a stable internet connection, causing lost progress or items since the map cannot be rejoined. Is this a known issue?

**A:** This is not specific to Land of Life. NosTale is very sensitive to connection interruptions, even a very brief hiccup (around 0.1 second) that would go unnoticed in other apps or on Discord can disconnect the client, and susceptibility can depend on your location or network route to the server. There is no known fix, only awareness that an unstable connection is riskier during content like Land of Life where rejoining is not possible.
