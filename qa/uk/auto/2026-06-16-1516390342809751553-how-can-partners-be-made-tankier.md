# How can partners be made tankier?

**Q:** Is there a way to make partners (pets/specialists' partner units) actually tanky? A Feraspa partner at +39 upgrade with 120% shadow resistance and a R7+8 C81 armor is still taking huge amounts of damage.

**A:** Partner tankiness mainly comes from resistances, armor stat, and the partner perfection system. A +39 upgrade level is not considered very high; as with specialist weapons/armor, higher upgrade levels give proportionally more benefit the further you push them. There are also ways to reduce incoming critical damage, though these were not detailed further.
