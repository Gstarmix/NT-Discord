# Freeze/error screen when entering Act 4

**Q:** Entering Act 4 sometimes produces a broken/frozen screen. Is this a known issue and is there a fix?

**A:** Support confirmed this is a known bug that occurs when entering Act 4, and that they cannot fully fix it. Repairing or reinstalling the game does not reliably resolve it. The workaround given by support is to change your character's location — e.g. have an alt invite you to a different country/channel — which typically clears the frozen screen.
