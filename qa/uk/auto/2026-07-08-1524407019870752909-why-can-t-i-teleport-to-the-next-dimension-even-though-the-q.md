# Why can't I teleport to the next dimension even though the quest requires it?

**Q:** The quest 'Pass to the next dimension' requires teleporting to a new dimension, but trying to do so gives the message 'You cannot use this.' Why does this happen and how do I actually progress?

**A:** You cannot teleport directly; you must go back to the NPC in the previous dimension where you completed your earlier quests there (in the shown case, a 'Dimension Doctor' type NPC near the portal you originally arrived from) and talk to them to be sent onward. In the screenshot this is the portal leading from the Joseon dynasty area to the Troy coast area. Once you interact with that NPC, the dimension transfer completes normally.
