# Level requirement to post and comment in Dragonveil bazaar chat

**Q:** Why can't I post anything in the Dragonveil bazaar chat, even though I can post in Undercity?

**A:** Posting in the Dragonveil bazaar chat requires being at least level 5. Commenting on posts requires being at least level 10.
