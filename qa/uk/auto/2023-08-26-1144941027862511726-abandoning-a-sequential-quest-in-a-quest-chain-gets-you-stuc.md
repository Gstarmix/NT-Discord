# Abandoning a sequential quest in a quest chain gets you stuck

**Q:** In a quest chain where only one quest can be accepted at a time (the next one unlocking only after finishing the previous), what happens if a player abandons one of these quests?

**A:** Abandoning one of these sequential quests leaves the player stuck unable to progress the chain; this was forwarded as a bug, and the only workaround is to open a support ticket to have the quest restored.
