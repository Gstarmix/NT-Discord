# Best exp maps in Act 7 and Act 8 by level range

**Q:** Are there other good exp maps in Act 8 besides the main one, since Act 7 (polluted path) gets boring after many hours? What about earlier level ranges like character level 30 to 50, is the LoL map the only option there?

**A:** In Act 7, besides Polluted Path you can also grind at Polluted Forest, Olorun Farm, or Beast's Lair. For Act 8 style progression by level: character level 54 to 63 use the bees map only (the blue dragons map above the city is worse); level 63 to 73 has a couple of options including the griffons map leading to Gravel Gorge (players usually prefer staying on the griffons themselves), the sharks map (right after bees), or the soldiers map right before Valehir; level 73 to 80 has only 3 usable maps, with the centaurs map being poor and most players preferring the Alza raid entry map (Ice Dungeon) over the Vale entry map. The LoL bonus map alone can carry a character through in about 3 days. Act 8 content overall is intended for character level 60+, with low level entry (around c20) only allowed so newer players can explore when it releases; the same pattern applies to later acts, Act 9 allows entry from level 60 but is recommended around level 80+, and Act 10 allows entry from level 80 but is recommended around level 90+.
