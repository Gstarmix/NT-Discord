# Does Holy's ultimate skill apply a fear/incapacitate effect?

**Q:** Does the Holy specialist's ultimate skill (Judgment Hammer) have a chance to incapacitate or fear the target?

**A:** Yes. The Holy ultimate skill, Judgment Hammer (range 11, hits enemies within 4 cells, 90 second cooldown, 145 MP cost), has a 100% chance to apply Slight Horror (a fear-like effect) on cast, in addition to a 70% chance to trigger Dispel Shadow and a 50% chance to trigger Holy Light. Players noted this is valuable because the fear effect lets you reset the cooldown of certain skills, though a separate related proc for that reset only has around a 10% chance.
