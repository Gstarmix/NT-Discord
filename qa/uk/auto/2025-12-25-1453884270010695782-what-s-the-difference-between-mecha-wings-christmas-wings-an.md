# What's the difference between Mecha Wings, Christmas Wings, and Steampunk Wings?

**Q:** What is the difference between mecha wings, christmas wings, and steampunk wings?

**A:** These three wing types each have a distinct effect: they apply different maluses (debuffs) to monsters and grant the player a different buff. Which one is best depends on your budget, your needs, your SP card setup, and whether you're focused on PvE or PvP — there isn't a single universally best option.
