# How to raise a partner's or pet's level above your character's default limit

**Q:** As a new/returning player, is it normal that a partner's level is capped relative to the character's level, and how can this be changed?

**A:** There is an item called partner's medicine that increases a partner's level by 1, up to a maximum of 5 levels below your own character's level. Pets have their own dedicated item that works the same way to raise their level.
