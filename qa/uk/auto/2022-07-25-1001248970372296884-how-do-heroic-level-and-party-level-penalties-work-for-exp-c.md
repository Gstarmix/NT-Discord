# How do heroic-level and party-level penalties work for EXP/CEXP?

**Q:** What level penalty was being referred to — is it about heroic level or normal level, and when does it apply?

**A:** There are two separate penalties. A heroic-level penalty applies when your heroic level is not close to a mob's heroic level, but this only matters in Act 8. Separately, there is a party-level penalty based on normal level: if you are partied with someone whose normal level is higher than yours, you receive reduced EXP and CEXP because of that level gap, independent of heroic level.
