# Skipping c80/c83 gear when going from c76 to c90

**Q:** Is it possible to go straight from level 76 class gear to level 90 gear, skipping the c80/c83 tier entirely? The c83 → upgrade → conversion to c90 → rune upgrade path seems very expensive, especially since ready-made c90 equipment is getting cheaper.

**A:** Skipping straight to c90 gear is possible but you will lack damage unless you exp in a solid, well-coordinated trio. A cheaper alternative is to get a "pass" c80/c83 equipment at +7/+8 without runes, just enough to carry you through until you can afford c90 gear.
