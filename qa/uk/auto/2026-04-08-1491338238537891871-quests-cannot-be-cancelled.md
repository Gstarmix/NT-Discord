# Quests cannot be cancelled

**Q:** Can quests be cancelled? I can't accept new quests until I finish ones I find boring (e.g. Valhalla, Act 8, Act 6).

**A:** Quests cannot be cancelled or deleted by players, and staff do not cancel them either. This is intentional: if quests could be deleted, players could retake them later, which could cause other issues.
