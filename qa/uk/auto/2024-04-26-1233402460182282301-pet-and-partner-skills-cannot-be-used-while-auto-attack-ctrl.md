# Pet and partner skills cannot be used while auto-attack (Ctrl+Z) is active

**Q:** Is it a bug that you cannot use pet and partner skills while the auto-attack toggle (Ctrl+Z) is active?

**A:** Yes, this is a known bug. It has existed since partner special skills and pet skills were introduced. A staff member confirmed it was not reported before and said it would be checked and forwarded to the development team, but no fix timeline was given.
