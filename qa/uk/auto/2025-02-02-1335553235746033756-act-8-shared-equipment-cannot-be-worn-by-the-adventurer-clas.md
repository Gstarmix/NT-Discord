# Act 8 shared equipment cannot be worn by the Adventurer class

**Q:** Is it a bug that Act 8 shared equipment, such as shoes, gloves, bracelets, necklaces, rings, hats and masks, cannot be equipped by Adventurer class characters?

**A:** This is considered a bug: all Act 8 shared equipment pieces currently exclude the Adventurer class from wearing them. The likely cause is that the 'can be worn by' class restriction was copied from the Act 8 hat item, which already excluded Adventurers, and this exclusion was carried over to the other equipment pieces even though the equivalent older gear could be worn by Adventurers.
