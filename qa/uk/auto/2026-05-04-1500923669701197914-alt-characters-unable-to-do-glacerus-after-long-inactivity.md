# Alt characters unable to do Glacerus after long inactivity

**Q:** After not logging into an alternate character (mule) for several weeks, the game reports the Glacerus daily as already completed, and that character cannot join Glacerus at all.

**A:** This is a known issue: characters that haven't logged in for roughly 30 days can get stuck thinking they've already completed the Glacerus daily, which then also blocks them from joining the Glacerus raid entirely. No official fix or workaround was mentioned beyond identifying the cause.
