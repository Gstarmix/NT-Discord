# Is Attack Stance plus Growing Experience a good tattoo combo for a swordman

**Q:** For a swordman (Sader tattoos), is keeping Attack Stance combined with Growing Experience a good tattoo combo, or should Growing Experience be replaced with Piercing Gaze?

**A:** Growing Experience is considered weak early game because it only stacks from crit hits, and low crit chance early on makes it inconsistent, whereas Piercing Gaze gives a flat crit damage and crit chance boost that is stronger at that stage. That said, keeping Growing Experience is not a disaster since rerolling tattoos is RNG based (and the guaranteed selective reroll item is expensive), so it can be fine to keep it until a better opportunity to switch comes along.
