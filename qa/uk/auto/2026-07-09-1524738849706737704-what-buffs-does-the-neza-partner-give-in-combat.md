# What buffs does the Neza partner give in combat

**Q:** How does the Neza partner's buff work, and what do its two different buff effects do?

**A:** Neza provides two separate buffs: one applies to you (its owner), giving bonus health and roughly 13% extra damage, while a separate 5% damage buff is applied to Neza itself and benefits allies around it. If you cast while targeting a mob, Neza heals itself and you receive the attack buff. Its health drain effect only works against light-element targets and does not heal the player. There was some uncertainty among repliers about whether this works exactly like the Alitus partner.
