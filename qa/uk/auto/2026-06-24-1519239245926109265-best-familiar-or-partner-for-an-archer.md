# Best familiar or partner for an Archer

**Q:** What is the best familiar or partner for an Archer?

**A:** For endgame archers at character level 99 with good equipment, Nezarun is considered the best choice, clearly better than Forga. Forga is recommended instead if you are still leveling/farming experience. Some players choose cheaper budget options instead of Nezarun.
