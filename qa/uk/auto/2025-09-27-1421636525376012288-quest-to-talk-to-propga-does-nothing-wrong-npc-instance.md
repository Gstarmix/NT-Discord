# Quest to talk to Propga does nothing (wrong NPC instance)

**Q:** My character has a quest to speak with Propga, but nothing happens when I talk to the NPC. How can I fix this without abandoning the quest?

**A:** There are two separate Propga NPCs in the game, one in Mortaz Desert Port and one in Port Moritius. If talking to Propga does nothing, you are likely interacting with the wrong instance of the NPC — try finding and talking to the other one.
