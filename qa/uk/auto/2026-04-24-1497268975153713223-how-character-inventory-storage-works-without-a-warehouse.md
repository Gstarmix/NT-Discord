# How character inventory storage works without a warehouse

**Q:** Does a character only carry what fits in the inventory, or is there extra storage even without buying a warehouse?

**A:** Characters have 3 main expandable inventory tabs plus 4 additional specialized ones for SP cards, costumes, mounts and raid items. Warehouses (only one active at a time, with different capacities) are also available and can be accessed through Miniland or the pet basket. In addition, there is a partner's backpack with 50 slots that can hold any item type.
