# Why x2 EXP events don't boost daily quest EXP

**Q:** Why doesn't the x2 EXP event apply to daily quest EXP rewards?

**A:** Daily quest EXP is intentionally left out of x2 EXP events because it is already balanced to give a boosted, fixed amount of experience. If daily quest EXP were also doubled during x2 events, the baseline daily quest EXP given on normal days would need to be lowered to compensate, which is why it's excluded instead.
