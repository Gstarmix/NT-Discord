# Getting teleported outside the map when dashing during TS/raids

**Q:** Is there a known fix for the bug where you get flown/teleported outside the map (e.g. on the Dragon Ranch TS map) when using a dash skill, or a way to avoid it? It happens most often on Act 8 TS2 as Death Hunter, but has also occurred with Wild Keeper and Archer Assassin.

**A:** This is a known issue with no official fix yet. It tends to happen when you use a dash (combo skills, not the basic one) while the map changes at the same time, for example when your team teleports you through a portal, or when you step on a locked portal while dashing. As a workaround, avoid changing maps or using dashes right when a portal teleport might trigger.
