# A7.2 'Energy Transfer' quest items not dropping (Spirit Forest Edge)

**Q:** On the Act 7.2 quest '(Sub) Energy Transfer' at Spirit Forest Edge, I need to collect 20 Spirit Forest Branch (from Sylvan Spirit) and 5 Loa Runic Powder (from Guardian Spirit), but after tens of thousands of kills and over 30 hours on the map I have not gotten a single item, while other players finish this step in 3 to 4 hours. Is this a known bug?

**A:** These quest items have a low base drop chance (about 2% for the Spirit Forest Branch, 0.5% for the Loa Runic Powder), but on top of that there was a known issue where having multiple drop related quests active at the same time reduced or blocked the drop chance of quest items. A maintenance patch on 2025-03-19 was supposed to fix quest item drop chances when multiple quests are active, but reports of near zero drops continued afterward. If you are stuck, try removing other active drop quests, for example the daily 'Wrath of the Spirits', to see if that restores normal drops.
