# High CPU usage and low FPS on newer high-end PCs

**Q:** On a new high-end PC (Ryzen 5 7600X, 32GB DDR5 6000MHz, RX 9070), NosTale runs at only 30 FPS per client and CPU usage reaches 100% by the 7th client opened. Is this a known issue with newer generation CPUs?

**A:** Several other players have also reported unusually high resource usage on newer or high-end PCs, suggesting NosTale may have optimization issues with recent hardware generations. Not everyone is affected: some players on Intel systems report no high CPU usage, only occasional lag spikes. There is no confirmed fix; affected players are advised to seek further help in the dedicated tech-support channels.
