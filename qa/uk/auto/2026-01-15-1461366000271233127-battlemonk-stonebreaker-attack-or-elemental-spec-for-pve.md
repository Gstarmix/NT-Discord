# Battlemonk/Stonebreaker: attack or elemental spec for PVE?

**Q:** Should Battlemonk and Stonebreaker specialist sets be built for attack or elemental stats in PVE?

**A:** Go for attack, since it grants crit-related substats. That said, a balanced split (e.g. 60% attack / 40% elemental) is generally considered stronger than an extreme split like 80/0.
