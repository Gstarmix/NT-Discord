# Do Lunar Wings and Petal Wings proc per hit or per skill?

**Q:** Do Lunar Wings and Petal Wings trigger per hit or per skill use, and do they only trigger while actively attacking in combat?

**A:** Lunar Wings appear to trigger per hit, and classes such as Renegade can proc them through normal attacks. Petal Wings function as a debuff and are expected to trigger per mob individually, similar to a regular skill. However, some players reported the Lunar effect triggering seemingly at random, even without attacking, so the exact trigger condition is not fully certain.
