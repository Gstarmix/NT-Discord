# Leveling path for a returning player from level 56 to act 6

**Q:** Is it worth restarting NosTale from scratch as a returning player? Is it possible to level from 52-65 up through act 6 using only the main questlines, and is doing acts 2-3 for experience still worthwhile?

**A:** A new/returning character can start at level 56 and complete the main quests of acts 2, 3 and 4 to reach level 81. Finishing act 4 grants new equipment and an item that upgrades SP stones 1-4 to +9. Completing act 5.1 (the side questline) then gives roughly 23kk gold and takes the character to about level 85-86, after which act 6 can be started with champion experience. It's generally recommended to fully complete each act's questline for the gold reward, even if you plan to pause at level 85 before starting act 6.
