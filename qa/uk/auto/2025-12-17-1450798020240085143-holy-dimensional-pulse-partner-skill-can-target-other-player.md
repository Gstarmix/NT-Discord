# Holy Dimensional Pulse partner skill can target other players/pets

**Q:** Is it a bug or intended that the partner skill 'Holy Dimensional Pulse' (its 'Dimensional Rejuvenation' buff) can be applied not only to your own partner but also to a pet or another player you click on?

**A:** This is intended, not a bug. The skill's secondary effect is a support buff designed to be cast on other players; it applies to your own partner instead only when you are targeting a monster.
