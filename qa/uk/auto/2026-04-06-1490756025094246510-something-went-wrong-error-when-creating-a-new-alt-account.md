# "Something went wrong" error when creating a new alt account

**Q:** Why do I get the error "something went wrong" when trying to create a new alt account, even across multiple emails?

**A:** This is likely because there is a limit of 3 new account creations per 24 hours. If you already created accounts recently, you may have hit that limit and need to wait.
