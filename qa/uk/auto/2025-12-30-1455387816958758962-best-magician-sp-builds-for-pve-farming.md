# Best magician SP builds for PvE farming

**Q:** Which SP cards (SP1, SP8, SP10, Mecha, Christmas, Steampunk, etc.) should magicians use for PvE, including maps like Mecha and the Christmas tree (Sapin) raid?

**A:** For most PvE content, the Christmas SP is the recommended choice for mages because of its resource (HP/MP) drain sustain, and it can be used on most mage SP maps/content. However, some specific fights require the Mecha SP instead — notably Seer and Vulcano need Mecha rather than Christmas.
