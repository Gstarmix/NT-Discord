# Does a weapon rune's 'extra damage to enemy' effect boost damage against demon-race bosses?

**Q:** Does the weapon rune effect that adds damage against a specific enemy race apply extra damage against the new act's demon-race boss? Is there any way to boost damage specifically for this act's raid boss?

**A:** There is no rune or item effect that boosts damage specifically against demon or human race enemies. The only relevant damage boost against demons comes from the family bonus obtained by completing the HC Erenia raid.
