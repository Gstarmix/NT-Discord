# Some tokens can't be equipped despite meeting the level requirement

**Q:** Why can't a level 83 character equip a clean token, even though the character meets the token's level requirement?

**A:** This is a known issue affecting equipment (such as MA tokens) that recently had its level requirement lowered, the game can still incorrectly block equipping it even though the character meets the new requirement. Players encountering the same problem with other equipment are asked to report it.
