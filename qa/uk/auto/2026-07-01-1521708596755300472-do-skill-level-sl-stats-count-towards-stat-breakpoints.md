# Do Skill Level (SL) stats count towards stat breakpoints?

**Q:** Do Skill Level (SL) stats count towards stat breakpoints? For example, if a character has 89 attack from SP points and 11 attack from SL, does the resulting 100 total grant breakpoint bonuses even though it doesn't show in the point menu card description? Also, when balancing stats between a main stat and elements, is it better to prioritize hitting round numbers like 100 in the main stat, or push further (e.g. 120) before switching to elements?

**A:** Yes, SL stats do count towards stat breakpoints, even though they aren't reflected in the point menu, they do show up in the overall card view menu. The only stat that doesn't count towards breakpoints is the perfection bonus, which is treated completely separately. As for the ratio between main stat and elements, it depends on SP amount and class, but generally the recommendation is to prioritize the main stat first and put remaining points into secondary stats like elements.
