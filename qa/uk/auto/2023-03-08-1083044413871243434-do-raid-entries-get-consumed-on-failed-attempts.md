# Do raid entries get consumed on failed attempts?

**Q:** In the patch note stating that raid entries are now only consumed when the respective raid is successfully completed, does this change apply to raid seals as well?

**A:** No, this change only applies to raid entry tickets (such as the one for Glacernon), not to raid seals. In other words, a failed raid attempt will no longer consume the entry ticket, only a successful completion does.
