# Divine Grace buff or War buff when mobbing with zerk?

**Q:** When mobbing with a berserker build, which buff should I prioritize: the Divine Grace (dg) buff or the War buff?

**A:** Use the War buff if your attacks are missing a lot, since it helps with accuracy. Otherwise, the Divine Grace buff is the better choice.
