# LoL XP rate vs self-leveling with bees / worth of a paid XP service

**Q:** Is the XP rate in LoL (Land of Oblivion) the same as elsewhere? Is it worth paying for an Act 8 XP leveling service, or is self-leveling on bees at a good rate (e.g. 50%/hour) better?

**A:** LoL gives roughly 3x more XP than any other map. It's recommended to level yourself rather than pay for an XP service: bees are very easy to kill, but relying on a paid service makes you miss out on a lot of XP and lose money.
