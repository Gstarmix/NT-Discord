# Why does a normal mob hit for 5000 damage despite decent gear

**Q:** Why am I getting hit for around 5000 damage by a normal mob while wearing A6 R5 +7 armor and level 73-75 jewelry (with max HP/MP but no critical negation), and my partner is also getting melted instantly? That doesn't seem normal.

**A:** That large hit is likely a critical hit, which explains why it hurts more without critical negation on the jewelry. Taking heavy hits from ranged mobs is otherwise considered normal, particularly from archer and wizard type mobs such as the witch in Polluted Path or the archer and wizard in Polluted Forest.
