# Best leveling map for character level 71

**Q:** Which map gives good experience for a character around level 71?

**A:** Levels 70-73 are considered the worst stretch in Act 8 for leveling, since there are no mobs with a level range above the player (within non-penalty range) — only mobs at or below, the same ones used from level 63-70 (Sharks, Griffon area, etc.), making these levels notably slow. The best option is to keep grinding at Clod until reaching level 73, at which point you can move on to the Alzia/Vale zones for much better exp.
