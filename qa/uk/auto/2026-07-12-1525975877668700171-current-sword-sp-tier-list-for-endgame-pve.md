# Current sword SP tier list for endgame PvE

**Q:** What is the current sword specialist (SP) tier list for endgame content? Between Stone Breaker (SB) and Dark Knight (DK), which is better if the player has no crossbow build, and how do SB and DK compare to Crusader (sader)?

**A:** For endgame PvE, Berserker is considered top tier, followed by Stone Breaker and Crusader, with Blade also viable for fire-based content. Stone Breaker has strong area of effect skills on all abilities and is quite fast, making it a strong pick even without a crossbow build. Dark Knight leans more toward dark-element and single-target damage rather than broad PvE performance, so Stone Breaker is generally considered to perform better than Dark Knight for general PvE.
