# Cheapest way to get an A-buff partner skill (Perti/Forga) for cExp bonus

**Q:** What is the cheapest way to obtain a Perti or Forga partner SP with an A buff for the cExp bonus — buying one already at A buff (30-40kk), or buying a base one (18kk) and rerolling it yourself with cheap green skill tickets (~80k each)?

**A:** Using green skill tickets to reroll is a viable and cheap strategy: reset with a green ticket to unlock the first two skills, then check their average grade — if it's B or higher, unlock/keep the third skill; if not, reset again (e.g., aim for combinations like D+A+S, C+A, or B+B ending in an overall A average). Given the low ticket price, this approach is described as fairly easy to reach A buff and cheaper than buying a pre-rolled A buff partner SP.
