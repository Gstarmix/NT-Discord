# Should you keep gambling SP tickets after rolling A/F/S grades?

**Q:** If a partner's specialty already rolled A, F, and S grades, is it better to keep using all remaining tickets, or use just one on the F slot hoping to upgrade it to A?

**A:** Before spending more tickets, check whether you can simply buy an equivalent A-grade buff cheaply on the market, as that may not be worth burning tickets for, unless the tickets you have are limited/untradeable and would otherwise go to waste. In general, it's not recommended to spend gold rerolling a specialty that is already relatively cheap to begin with.
