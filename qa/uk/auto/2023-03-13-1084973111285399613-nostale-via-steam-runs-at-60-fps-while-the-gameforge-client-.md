# NosTale via Steam runs at 60 FPS while the Gameforge client seems locked to 30 FPS

**Q:** Why does NosTale played through Steam feel smoother (60 FPS) compared to the Gameforge Live client, which feels locked to 30 FPS?

**A:** This is likely a client configuration issue rather than a fixed platform limitation. The Gameforge Live launcher has a dropdown next to the Play button with a dedicated 'config' tool (alongside 'opengl', 'Repair', 'Uninstall') that lets you change client settings, which can affect the frame rate. Checking and adjusting this config is suggested as the fix for the perceived 30 FPS lock.
