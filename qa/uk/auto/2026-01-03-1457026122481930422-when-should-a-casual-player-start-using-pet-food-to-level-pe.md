# When should a casual player start using pet food to level pets past 80?

**Q:** As a casual player, at what level should you start using pet food to get a pet past level 80? Does it only become worth it around level 30, or is there a point where leveling without food starts to drag?

**A:** Pets can gain experience fairly quickly through LoD, especially at the lower levels, so there isn't a strict level at which pet food becomes necessary for casual players — the early levels can be done without it, and food becomes more relevant once natural leveling slows down at higher levels.
