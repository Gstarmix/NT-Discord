# Shells with +10 to all stats and 15 attack (25 total attack)

**Q:** Are there shells that give +10 to all stats along with 15 attack, for a total of 25 attack on the secondary weapon?

**A:** Yes, any class-level (clvl) gear can roll that kind of combination, but you need rare 7 (R7) or rare 8 (R8) gear to get it.
