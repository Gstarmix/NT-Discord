# How do perfection stones work and what are they worth?

**Q:** How do perfections (perfection stones) work — how do you buy them, and is it true you trade SP for them? Roughly how much are they worth?

**A:** Perfections are bought by trading your SP (skill points) to another player. You need 2 card holders, and the price depends on the type of PP (perfection) stone. They generally sell for around 100k-600k each.
