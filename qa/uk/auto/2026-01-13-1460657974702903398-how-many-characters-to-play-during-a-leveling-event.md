# How many characters to play during a leveling event

**Q:** Is it advisable to level multiple characters at once during this event, and how many?

**A:** It depends on how much time you have. A reasonable approach is to build one strong character first, then level 2-3 more, using the strong character to carry them through quests and raids. Meanwhile, run Item Collector (IC) and other Battlepass activities on the side.
