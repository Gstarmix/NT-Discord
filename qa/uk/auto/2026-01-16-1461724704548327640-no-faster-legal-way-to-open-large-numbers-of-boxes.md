# No faster legal way to open large numbers of boxes

**Q:** Is there a legal/official way to open a large stockpile of boxes faster than one at a time?

**A:** There is currently no faster legal method to open boxes in bulk. A suggestion for this has been submitted and is being considered/worked on, but until it is implemented players have to open boxes individually.
