# Is the Training skill useless for mages?

**Q:** Is the Training skill (shown in the attached skill details, giving certain combat bonuses) pointless to use as a mage?

**A:** It is not entirely pointless for mages: besides its main effect, Training also grants melee defense, so it still provides some benefit even to a mage build.
