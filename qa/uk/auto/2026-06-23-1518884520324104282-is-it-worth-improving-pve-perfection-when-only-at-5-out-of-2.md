# Is it worth improving PvE perfection when only at 5 out of 22 points

**Q:** I currently only have 5 PvE perfection out of 22 total perfection points. Is it worth pushing for more?

**A:** It's not an ideal allocation but doesn't matter much at low perfection levels. Most players aim for perfection level 80 as a benchmark, if you already have 40 PvE/PvP perfection by that point, it becomes worth pushing all the way to 100.
