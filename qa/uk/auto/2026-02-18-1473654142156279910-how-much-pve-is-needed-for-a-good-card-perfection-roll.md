# How much PvE is needed for a good card perfection roll

**Q:** How much PVE stat is considered enough for card perfection (SP perfection)?

**A:** A minimal target is around 60 PVE, with 70 being better and 80+ considered optimal. Reaching a high PVE roll (70-80+) through self perfecting can be very costly, reportedly requiring around 1000 to 1500 perfection stones (this may vary depending on whether it is during a perfection event). Since perfecting is essentially gambling, it is generally not considered worth it unless you have money to spare on perfection stones or Zenas/Erenia boxes; some players instead prefer to simply buy an SP that already has 70+ PVE.
