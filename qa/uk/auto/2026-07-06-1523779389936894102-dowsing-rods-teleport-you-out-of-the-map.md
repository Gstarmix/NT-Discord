# Dowsing rods teleport you out of the map

**Q:** Why do dowsing rods bring me out of the map, even after relogging, and does it always happen unless I relog after using them?

**A:** This is a known issue linked to keyboard language settings. Switching your keyboard language fixes the problem so dowsing rods stop teleporting you outside the map.
