# Best stats for a top-tier PvE wand

**Q:** What are the best specs to look for on an optimal PvE wand?

**A:** Prioritize S.Damage together with S.Attack or S.Element. If the wand is top tier, aim for an enchant that covers all three stats (S.Damage, S.Attack, and S.Element).
