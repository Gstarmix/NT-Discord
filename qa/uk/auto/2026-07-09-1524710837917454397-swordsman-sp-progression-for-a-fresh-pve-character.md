# Swordsman SP progression for a fresh PvE character

**Q:** Starting NosTale fresh after previously playing Archer, which specialist card path is recommended for a Swordsman aiming at good PvE performance from early to late game?

**A:** Use SP1 until class 30, since it is considered very strong for leveling. After that, switch to SP4 and keep using it through class 90 (its viability at A10 was unconfirmed). SP4 is also considered good for late-game content.
