# What does putting SP points (SPP) on a partner change?

**Q:** What is actually the difference when you put SP points (SPP) on your partner?

**A:** Adding SP points to a partner improves things like its movement speed, HP, defenses, and skills. Overall, it makes the partner more effective and makes playing the game easier.
