# Why can't some crafting materials be found on NosBazar

**Q:** Can the materials needed for this crafting recipe (including Red-eyed Scorpion Venom) be posted on NosBazar? I can't find any listing for them.

**A:** These crafting materials are obtained from monster drops or from regular player shops, not through NosBazar. They are still tradeable between players, just not listable on the NosBazar system.
