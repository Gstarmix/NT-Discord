# Does the İbrahim raid boss debuff apply elemental damage?

**Q:** In the İbrahim raid, the boss applies a debuff of -150 to all resistances and sets elemental damage calculation to 0. Since this is treated as a non-elemental raid and no one deals elemental damage, why does the boss reduce resistances anyway?

**A:** Elemental damage is actually applied during this raid despite it being commonly thought of as non-elemental. The debuff that sets elemental calculation to 0 and reduces all resistances by 150 is specifically meant to counter the boss's own elemental attacks against the players' resistances, not to imply elemental damage is irrelevant.
