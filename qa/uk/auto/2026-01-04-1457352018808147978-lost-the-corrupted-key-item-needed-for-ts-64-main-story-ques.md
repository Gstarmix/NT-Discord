# Lost the corrupted key item needed for TS 64 main story quest

**Q:** I completed Time Space 64 and received a corrupted key, but I accidentally deleted it and now cannot continue the main story since the item cannot be purchased. What can I do?

**A:** If a required story item like this is lost and cannot be bought or re-obtained in game, the only option is to submit a support ticket to have it restored.
