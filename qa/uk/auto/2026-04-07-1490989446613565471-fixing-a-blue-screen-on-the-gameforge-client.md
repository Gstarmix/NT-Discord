# Fixing a blue screen on the Gameforge client

**Q:** The Gameforge client shows only a blue screen and won't let me log into the game. How can I fix this?

**A:** Restarting the launcher several times can eventually resolve the issue. If that fails, open Task Manager, find the Gameforge client process, close all running instances of it, and then restart the game client.
